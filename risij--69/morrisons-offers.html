The Value of Morrisons' Market Street

Published: October 19, 2024 | In: Fresh Food Finds

Finding Freshness and Value on Market Street

Morrisons' "Market Street" is its unique selling point, featuring in-store bakery, butchery, and fishmonger counters that offer high-quality, fresh produce. Often, these items provide better value than their pre-packaged counterparts. To find the best deals, keep an eye on the Morrisons offers this week on Risij.uk, where we regularly feature top picks from Market Street.

A Savvy Shopper's Market Street Strategy:

  • Look for promotions on "The Best" range for a touch of luxury at a lower price.
  • Chat with the counter staff for cooking advice and recommendations on the best value products.
  • Visit later in the day to find significant reductions on fresh items.

Unlocking My Morrisons Rewards

The "My Morrisons" loyalty program has shifted from points to instant, personalized money-off rewards. The app curates offers based on your shopping history. By simply activating these digital coupons before you shop, you can secure substantial discounts on the products you buy most often.
