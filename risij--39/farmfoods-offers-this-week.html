The Best Way to Shop at Farmfoods: Vouchers & Bulk Buys

Published: October 28, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

Farmfoods has a very clear goal: to offer well-known brands, especially frozen food, at fantastic prices. While they don't have the huge range of a big supermarket, they are experts at what they do. Understanding how they work is the key to getting the most from the Farmfoods offers this week.

How to Save the Most at Farmfoods

There are two main ways to save at Farmfoods: by using their multi-buy deals and by using money-off vouchers that they send to you.

Your Farmfoods Shopping Plan

  • Sign Up for Vouchers: This is the most important step! Go to the Farmfoods website and sign up for their emails. They will send you personal vouchers, like "£5 off when you spend £50," which can save you a lot of money.
  • Look for Multi-Buy Deals: Much of Farmfoods' stock is sold in multi-buy offers (e.g., "3 for £5" or "2 for £10"). These are great for stocking up the freezer.
  • Check the Weekly Leaflet: Farmfoods has a leaflet with all their latest deals, which you can always find on Risij.uk. Use it to plan what you're going to buy before you go.
  • Don't Forget the Cupboards: Although it's known for frozen food, Farmfoods also has great deals on branded cupboard items like breakfast cereals, pasta sauces, and soft drinks.

The Smart Way to Stock Up

Doing a big shop at Farmfoods every few weeks is a smart way to fill your freezer and cupboards with your family's favourites. This means you'll need fewer expensive last-minute trips to convenience stores like SPAR, saving you a lot of money in the long run.
