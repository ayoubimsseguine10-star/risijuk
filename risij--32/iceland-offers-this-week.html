Smart Shopping at Iceland

Updated: November 05, 2024 | Category: Shopping Guides

Iceland is a fantastic place to shop if you want to plan your meals ahead and save money, thanks to its huge range of frozen food. The Iceland offers this week are designed to help you stock up for less, and this guide will show you how to get the best value.

How to Find the Best Deals

Iceland's offers are usually simple, clear, and give you great value, especially their multi-buy deals.

1. The Famous "3 for £10" Mix & Match Deal

This is one of Iceland's best-known offers. You can mix and match three items from a huge range of popular branded products for just £10. It's a brilliant way to stock up on family favourites and save on each item. We list what's included in the deal on our Iceland offers page at Risij.uk.

2. The £1 Value Range

Iceland has a massive selection of items that are always priced at just £1 each. From frozen veg and chips to desserts, these are perfect for adding to your meals without breaking the bank.

An Extra Tip: Use the Iceland Bonus Card

The Iceland Bonus Card is a very clever way to save a little extra. It's not a points card – instead, you top it up with money to spend in-store, and for every £20 you add, Iceland gives you an extra £1 for free. That's a guaranteed 5% saving on top of all the other offers, so it's well worth doing!
