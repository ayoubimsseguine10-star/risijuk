Iceland's Bonus Card: The Easiest Savings Loophole in Supermarkets

Updated: July 2024 | Category: Loyalty Schemes

While other stores make you collect points, Iceland gives you cold, hard cash back. The Iceland Bonus Card isn't a points card; it's a savings account that pays you a bonus. It's the simplest, most direct savings hack in the UK grocery game, and it makes the Iceland offers this week even better.

How the Bonus Card Hack Works

The magic is in its simplicity. You top up your Bonus Card with money to spend later, and Iceland gives you a bonus for doing it. Think of it as paying yourself first, and getting rewarded for it.

Your Bonus Card Breakdown:

  • Get a 5% Kickback, Guaranteed: For every £20 you load onto your card, Iceland instantly adds £1. That's a 5% return before you've even bought anything. No other supermarket offers a guaranteed return like this.
  • Budget Like a Boss: Use the card to set your grocery budget. Load it up on payday, and that's your food spend for the month. It's a brilliant way to control your spending.
  • Unlock Exclusive Deals: Bonus Card holders get access to special member-only prices and promotions. It's another layer of savings on top of the 5% bonus.
  • Free Delivery Perk: Shopping online? The Bonus Card often gets you free delivery on orders over a certain amount, saving you even more cash.

Beyond the Freezer Aisle

Don't sleep on Iceland for your everyday items. They've massively expanded their range of fresh food, bakery items, and cupboard fillers. The famous multi-buy deals (like "3 for £10") often apply across the entire store, not just the frozen section. Always see the full range of offers on Risij.uk to plan your attack.
