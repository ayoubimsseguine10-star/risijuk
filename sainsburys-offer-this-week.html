A Guide to the Sainsbury's Offer This Week

Published on: 24th July 2024 | Category: Supermarket Deals

Sainsbury's balances quality and value, making it a favourite for many UK families. The key to unlocking the best prices is using a Nectar card to access exclusive deals. This guide will show you how to make the most of the Sainsbury's offer this week.

The Power of Nectar Prices

Similar to Tesco's Clubcard, Sainsbury's now offers "Nectar Prices" on hundreds of products throughout the store. These are significant, instant discounts available only to Nectar card holders. Simply scan your card or app at checkout to get the lower price. It's a no-brainer for any Sainsbury's shopper.

Maximizing Your Nectar Points:

  • Personalised Offers: Check the Nectar app for weekly bonus point offers on items you frequently buy.
  • Link with Partners: You can collect and spend Nectar points at hundreds of partner brands, including Argos, eBay, and Esso.
  • Nectar Connect: Securely link your bank card to collect points automatically when you shop at partner brands.

See the Nectar Prices! For a list of the latest Nectar Prices and other deals, visit the Sainsbury's Offer This Week page on Risij.uk.

Aldi Price Match

Sainsbury's is committed to being competitive on price. They run an "Aldi Price Match" campaign on hundreds of staple grocery items. Look for the price match signs in-store to be confident you're getting great value on your everyday essentials without having to shop around.

Your Sainsbury's Strategy

Always have your Nectar card ready. Before you go, check the Nectar app for personalised bonus offers to activate them. Plan your shop around the Nectar Prices and the Aldi Price Match items to ensure you're getting the best possible value on your Sainsbury's shop.
