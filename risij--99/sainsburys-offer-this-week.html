A Guide to Sainsbury's Nectar Prices

Published on Jan 18, 2025 | In Savings Guides

Sainsbury's has a wonderfully simple way to help you save money, and it's called Nectar Prices. It's all about getting instant discounts just for being a Nectar member. Here is our simple guide to the Sainsbury's offers this week.

Nectar Prices Explained Simply

When you're shopping in Sainsbury's, you'll see that some items have two prices on the shelf. There will be the regular price, and a lower price labelled "Nectar Price". All you need to do to get that lovely lower price is scan your Nectar card or app when you pay. It's as easy as that - the discount comes off straight away!

Three Friendly Tips for Your Sainsbury's Shop

  1. See the Nectar Prices Before You Go: It's a great idea to see what's on offer before you write your shopping list. You can find a full list of the current Nectar Prices on our main Risij.uk website.
  2. Check the Nectar App for Bonus Offers: Sometimes, the Nectar app has extra little coupons just for you. It's always worth a quick peek before you head to the shops.
  3. Look for the Aldi Price Match: On lots of everyday basics like bread and milk, you'll see a little red sign for the "Aldi Price Match." This is Sainsbury's promise that you're getting a great price on your essentials.

By always remembering to scan your Nectar card, you can make some really lovely savings on your weekly shop.
