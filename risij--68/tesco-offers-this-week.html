Get More from Tesco: A Clubcard Savings Guide

Published on July 26, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Programs

The Tesco Clubcard is one of the most powerful loyalty programs available to UK shoppers. To make the most of it, you need to actively look for "Clubcard Prices" when browsing the Tesco offers this week. This simple habit can lead to substantial savings on your grocery bill.

How Clubcard Prices Work

Many products at Tesco have two prices: a standard price and a lower price exclusively for Clubcard holders. These instant discounts are the primary benefit of the program. By focusing your shopping on these items, you ensure you're getting the best value Tesco has to offer.

Checklist for Tesco Savings

  1. Always Scan Your Card: Make it a habit to scan your Clubcard (or the app) with every purchase, no matter how small. This is the only way to get the exclusive prices.
  2. Check the App for Coupons: Before you shop, browse the "Coupons" section in the Tesco app. You can often find extra discounts on products you already buy.
  3. Plan Around the Deals: Use the Risij.uk platform to see all the Clubcard offers in one place. Plan your meals and shopping list around these deals to maximize your savings.
  4. Compare with Nectar Prices: For a complete picture, it's sometimes useful to compare Tesco's Clubcard prices with deals at other major supermarkets, such as the Sainsbury's offer this week for Nectar card holders.
