How Iceland's Bonus Card Saves You a Little Extra

Published: Oct 19, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Guides

Iceland's loyalty card, the Bonus Card, is a bit different from others, and we think it's brilliant! It doesn't use complicated points; it just gives you extra cash to spend. Let's see how you can use it with the Iceland offers this week.

How Does the Bonus Card Work?

It's wonderfully simple. Think of your Bonus Card as a savings pot for your Iceland shopping. You can add money to it whenever you like, and for every £20 you add, Iceland gives you £1 for free! That's like getting a 5% bonus just for planning ahead.

A Great Way to Budget

The Bonus Card is perfect for helping you stick to a budget. You can decide how much you want to spend on groceries, load it onto your card, and get your free bonus on top. It's a great feeling to know your shopping is already paid for!

Extra Savings on Top of Great Deals

Iceland is famous for its £1 deals and other fantastic multi-buys. When you pay for these bargains using the money on your Bonus Card, you're getting an even better deal because of the extra £1 you received. It's a wonderful way to make your money stretch further.

A Handy Perk: Free Delivery!

Another thing we love about Iceland is that they offer free home delivery when you spend over a certain amount in-store (usually around £25). This is so helpful when you're stocking up on heavy frozen goods. It's a service you won't find at places like Farmfoods.

Top Tip: Try adding a little bit to your Bonus Card each week. It soon adds up, and it's a lovely surprise to see how much you've saved (plus your bonuses!).
