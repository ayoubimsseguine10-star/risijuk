Waitrose Offers: Get Quality on a Budget

Published October 15, 2024 | In: Savings Guides

Yes, Waitrose is the premium option, but that doesn't mean you have to overspend. With a smart approach, you can get high-quality food without the shocking bill. Here’s your guide to hacking the Waitrose offers this week.

Your #1 Tool: The myWaitrose Card

The myWaitrose loyalty scheme is your key to saving. It's not about points; it's about instant discounts. Here's what you need to do:

  • Choose Your Vouchers: Every week, you get to choose two personalized money-off vouchers in the app. Take 30 seconds to pick the ones you'll actually use. It's the easiest saving you'll make all week.
  • Hunt for Green Tickets: In the store, look for the items with green price tickets. These are special discounts exclusively for myWaitrose members. Scan your card at the till to get them.

Target the "Essential Waitrose" Range

Don't sleep on the Essential Waitrose range. This is their own-brand line of everyday items, and the quality is excellent for the price. Many of the best weekly offers are on these products. By focusing your shop on the Essential range and combining it with your myWaitrose vouchers, you can dramatically cut your spend.

Final Pro Tip

The free coffee is gone, but the savings aren't. Always check the weekly offers list on a site like Risij.uk before you go. This allows you to match your chosen vouchers with the best in-store deals for what we call "double-dip" savings.
