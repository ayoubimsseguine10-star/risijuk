Finding Great Deals at Your Local SPAR

Published: Oct 18, 2024 | Category: Shopping Guides

We all rely on our local convenience store for those last-minute bits and pieces. While SPAR is perfect for that, you can also find some surprisingly good deals! Here's how to spot the value in the SPAR offers next week.

Good Value is Closer Than You Think

People often think convenience stores are expensive, but SPAR works hard to have great deals on everyday items you might run out of, like milk, bread, or your favourite snack.

Tip #1: Check for Reduced Items in the Evening

If your local SPAR has a bakery or sells fresh sandwiches, it's worth popping in later in the day. They often reduce the price of these items to make sure they sell, which means you can pick up a tasty and cheap dinner!

Tip #2: The Meal Deal is Your Friend

SPAR's lunchtime meal deals are often fantastic value. If you buy a sandwich, snack, and drink together, you'll almost always save money compared to buying them separately. It's a perfect, easy lunch option.

Look for Special Brand Offers

SPAR often has special offers on big-name brands that you might not see in the big supermarkets. So while you might do your main shop using the Co-op offers this week, SPAR is great for grabbing a treat or a top-up item at a good price.

Top Tip: SPAR is great for grabbing a bottle of wine or a few beers for the evening, as they often have better deals than you'd expect.
