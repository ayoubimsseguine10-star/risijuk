A Simple Guide to Tesco Clubcard Offers

Published on January 5, 2025 | Category: Loyalty Programs

When you shop at Tesco, having a Clubcard is not just an option—it's essential. Many products have two prices: a standard one and a much lower one for Clubcard holders. The best Tesco offers this week are only available if you use their loyalty scheme.

How Clubcard Prices Work

It's very simple: to get the lower price on hundreds of items, you just need to scan your Clubcard (or the Tesco app) at the checkout. If you forget, you'll end up paying more than you need to. The discounts are applied instantly as you pay.

Tips for Getting More from Your Clubcard:

  • Earn Points Everywhere: You collect points on your groceries, but also when you buy fuel at a Tesco petrol station or use Tesco Mobile.
  • Double Your Voucher Value: Every three months, your points are turned into vouchers. You can spend these on your shopping, but the best trick is to exchange them with Tesco's "Reward Partners." This often doubles their value for things like restaurant meals or days out.
  • Use the Tesco App: The Tesco Grocery & Clubcard app is the easiest way to access your card. It also gives you extra coupons for more savings on your favourite products.

Other Ways to Save at Tesco

On top of Clubcard Prices, Tesco also runs an "Aldi Price Match" on hundreds of basic items. This means you can be sure you're getting a competitive price on your essential groceries, allowing you to save across your entire shop.
