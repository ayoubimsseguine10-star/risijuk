Navigating Lidl's Middle Aisle

Updated: November 05, 2024 | Category: Shopping Guides

Lidl is known for its high-quality food at great prices, but the real fun is in the "Middle of Lidl". To get the best bargains, it helps to know how to make the most of the Lidl offers this week. This guide will show you how.

Exploring the "Middle of Lidl"

The middle aisle is where you'll find all the non-food special buys, with new items arriving every Thursday and Sunday. From DIY tools to kitchen gadgets, there are always some amazing finds. But be quick – the best stuff sells out fast!

Our Top Tip: Get a Sneak Peek

Lidl tells you what's coming to the middle aisle a week in advance in their app and online leaflet. We check this every week and share the must-have items on our Lidl offers page at Risij.uk. A quick look beforehand will help you decide what's worth a trip to the store.

Your Secret Weapon: The Lidl Plus App

If you shop at Lidl, the free Lidl Plus app is a must-have. It gives you:

  • Weekly Coupons: Get extra discounts on different products each week – just activate them in the app.
  • Exclusive App-Only Prices: Some deals are only for app users, so you get even more savings.
  • Digital Receipts: All your receipts are saved in the app, which makes it easy to keep track of your spending.
  • A Fun Little Bonus: You get a digital scratch card after you shop, with the chance to win a small prize.
