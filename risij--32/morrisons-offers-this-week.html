Fresh Deals at Morrisons Market Street

Updated: November 05, 2024 | Category: Shopping Guides

Morrisons is unique for its "Market Street," where you can get fresh food from specialist butchers, bakers, and fishmongers. This is a great place to find quality and value in the Morrisons offers this week, especially when you use their loyalty app.

Your Key to Savings: The "My Morrisons" App

The "My Morrisons" app is a fantastic tool for getting personalized discounts. Here's what it does:

  • Offers Just for You: The app learns what you like to buy and sends you money-off vouchers for your favorite things.
  • Exclusive Member Prices: Get special lower prices on certain items just by scanning your app at the till.
  • Little Surprises: Now and then, you might get a fun bonus, like a freebie or an extra discount.

Where to Find the Best Deals: Market Street

Market Street is the heart of Morrisons and a great place to find a bargain on fresh food.

1. At the Butcher and Fishmonger

Keep an eye out for multi-buy deals on fresh meat and fish, like "2 for £8" on chicken or salmon. Don't be afraid to ask the staff what the best value items are that day!

2. At the Bakery and Deli

You can often find great offers on freshly baked bread, cakes, and treats, as well as cooked meats and cheeses. The Salad Bar is also a brilliant, budget-friendly way to create your own healthy lunch.
