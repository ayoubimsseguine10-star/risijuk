How to Find the Best Iceland Offers This Week

Published: October 19, 2023 | Category: Shopping Tips

Iceland is the undisputed champion of the frozen food aisle, offering convenience and value in equal measure. But there's more to Iceland than just frozen pizzas and ice cream. With a simple but effective loyalty scheme and fantastic multi-buy deals, it's a must-visit for budget-conscious shoppers. Here’s how to find the best Iceland offers this week.

Get Your Bonus Card

The Iceland Bonus Card is one of the most straightforward loyalty schemes out there. It's not about points; it's about getting real money back.

  • Save and Earn: You can load money onto your card to spend later. For every £20 you save, Iceland gives you £1 for free. It's a great way to budget for a big shop or for Christmas.
  • Exclusive Offers: Cardholders get access to special prices and exclusive deals.
  • Free Home Delivery: Get free home delivery on in-store shops over £25 when you swipe your Bonus Card.

Tip: Look for the Famous "3 for £X" Deals

Iceland is famous for its multi-buy deals on frozen goods, particularly from brands like Birds Eye and Goodfellas. These offers provide significant savings compared to buying items individually and are perfect for stocking up the freezer.

Explore Beyond Frozen

Like its competitor Farmfoods, Iceland has expanded its range to include a wide variety of fresh and ambient groceries. You can often find great deals on everyday essentials like bread, milk, and fresh produce, making it a viable one-stop shop for many households.
