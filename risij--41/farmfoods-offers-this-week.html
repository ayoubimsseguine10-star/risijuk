The Farmfoods Formula: How to Stack Vouchers on This Week's Offers

Published on October 11, 2024 in Supermarket Guides

Farmfoods is a haven for savvy shoppers aiming to fill their freezers without emptying their wallets. The secret to unlocking maximum value is to strategically combine the already great Farmfoods offers this week with their powerful money-off vouchers.

Your Most Valuable Tool: The Farmfoods Voucher

The single best way to save at Farmfoods is with their discount vouchers. They consistently offer two tiers of savings:

  • Save £2.50 when you spend £25 or more
  • Save £5 when you spend £50 or more

Getting them is simple: just sign up for the newsletter on the Farmfoods website. The vouchers will be sent directly to your email, ready to be printed or shown on your phone at the till.

Pro Tip: Nail the Threshold

The smart strategy is to plan your shop to hit just over the £25 or £50 mark. This ensures you get the maximum impact from your voucher, effectively giving you up to a 10% discount on your entire shop.

Where to Find the Best Deals

Farmfoods is known for its incredible case-saving deals and big-brand bargains.

  1. Aggressive Multi-buys: Look for "3 for £10" style deals on branded frozen items like pizzas, ready meals, and desserts. These are often far cheaper than at major supermarkets.
  2. Bulk Up on Staples: Giant bags of frozen chicken, chips, and vegetables offer a much lower price-per-kilogram, perfect for stocking up.
  3. Explore the Pantry Aisles: Don't sleep on the ambient aisles. You'll find amazing deals on big-name cereals, coffee, sauces, and tinned goods.
