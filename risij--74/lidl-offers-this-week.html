A Friendly Guide to the "Middle of Lidl"

Published: Oct 21, 2024 | Category: Shopping Guides

If you've ever been to Lidl, you know all about the magical "Middle of Lidl." It's that wonderful centre aisle where you can find anything and everything! Here’s our friendly guide to making the most of the treasures in the Lidl offers this week.

Find Out This Week's Theme!

The best part about the middle aisle is that it's different every week. One week it might be all about gardening, and the next it could be filled with Italian food and kitchen gadgets. Knowing the theme ahead of time is the key to finding what you love.

Tip #1: The Lidl Plus App is a Must-Have

The Lidl Plus app on your phone is like a secret map. It tells you what the upcoming themes are and gives you special coupons for your grocery shopping. Using the coupons on your everyday items means you have more to spend on that surprise bargain in the middle!

Tip #2: Go on a Thursday for the Best Finds

The new Middle of Lidl stock arrives on Thursdays. If there's a popular theme, the best items can be snapped up really quickly. A Thursday morning trip is your best bet for getting first pick. This is very similar to the strategy for the Aldi offers next week.

A Delicious Tip: Don't Forget the Bakery!

While you're exploring, make sure you swing by the bakery section. The smell of fresh bread and pastries is irresistible, and it's the perfect treat to reward yourself with after a successful shopping trip.

Top Tip: The Middle of Lidl is perfect for finding unique gifts for people, from quirky kitchen tools to cosy winter socks.
