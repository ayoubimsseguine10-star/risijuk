Fresh Deals: Making the Most of Morrisons Market Street

Morrisons sets itself apart with its "Market Street," a collection of in-store counters featuring skilled butchers, bakers, and fishmongers. This focus on freshness provides a unique shopping experience and an opportunity for great value, especially when combined with the Morrisons offers this week.

The Market Street Advantage

Shopping at the fresh counters allows you to buy exactly the amount you need, which helps to reduce food waste. You can also get expert advice on cooking and preparation from the staff. Morrisons frequently runs promotions specifically on their Market Street products.

How to Save at Morrisons:

  • Check Counter Offers: Look for multi-buy deals at the butcher and fishmonger, like "2 for £8" on selected cuts of meat or fish.
  • My Morrisons Rewards: The loyalty app provides personalized offers and discounts. Check it before you shop to activate your deals.
  • Seasonal Promotions: Morrisons has excellent seasonal offers, especially around holidays, on everything from fresh produce to baked goods.
