Your Ultimate Guide to Lidl's Offers This Week

Published on July 27, 2024 in Shopping Tips

Shopping at Lidl is an adventure, thanks in large part to the treasure trove known as the 'Middle of Lidl'. This central aisle is where you'll find an eclectic mix of non-food items that changes twice a week. From kitchen gadgets to cycling gear, the variety is endless. To make the most of the Lidl offers this week, you need to know what to look for and when.

Decoding the 'Middle of Lidl'

The Middle of Lidl is updated every Thursday and Sunday with new themes. One week might feature DIY tools and gardening supplies, while the next could be all about fitness and wellness. These themes are advertised in advance, giving you time to plan.

Key Strategies for Success:

  • Use the Lidl Plus App: The app is your best friend. It not only shows you upcoming themes for the Middle of Lidl but also gives you access to exclusive coupons and a digital receipt library.
  • Timing is Everything: Just like with other discount supermarkets, the best items go quickly. If a popular theme like home electronics is featured, be prepared for items to sell out on the first day.
  • Check for Quality: Lidl's non-food items often come from German brands known for their quality, such as Parkside (tools) and Crivit (sports gear). Keep an eye out for these for excellent value.

Don't Forget the Food Deals

Beyond the middle aisle, Lidl offers exceptional value on groceries. Look out for their weekly highlights on fresh meat, fish, bakery items, and a fantastic selection of international foods during their themed weeks (e.g., Italian Week, Greek Week). Checking the Farmfoods offers this week can also be a great way to complement your Lidl shop for frozen goods.
