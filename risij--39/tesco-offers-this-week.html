How to Make the Most of Tesco Clubcard Prices

Updated: October 26, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Guides

Your Tesco Clubcard is more than just a way to collect points—it's your key to getting much lower prices. "Clubcard Prices" are a big deal, and understanding how they work with the Tesco offers this week can save you a lot of money on your weekly shop.

What Are Clubcard Prices?

Clubcard Prices are special, lower prices on hundreds of items that are only available to Clubcard holders. When you scan your card or app, you get instant discounts right at the till. It’s Tesco’s way of rewarding its customers and is essential for anyone looking to save.

Simple Tips for Using Your Clubcard

  • Always Scan Your Card: This is the most important tip! If you don't scan your Clubcard (or the app on your phone), you won't get the special prices.
  • Use the Tesco App: The Tesco Grocery & Clubcard app is really helpful. It has your digital card, sends you extra coupons, and shows you the best deals currently available.
  • Look for the Yellow Labels: In the store, Clubcard Prices are easy to see. Just look for the bright yellow labels on the shelves to spot the deals.
  • Combine Deals for Extra Savings: Sometimes, a Clubcard Price can be combined with another offer (like "2 for £5"). This is a great way to get an even bigger discount!

Don't Forget Your Points!

While you're saving money instantly with Clubcard Prices, you're still collecting points. You can turn these points into vouchers to spend on your shopping or get even more value by using them with Tesco's Reward Partners for things like family days out or restaurant meals. It's a great way to get rewarded both now and later, something you don't get at stores like Co-op.
