Morrisons Offers: A Guide to Market Street & My Morrisons

Published on July 29, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Programs

Morrisons carves its own niche in the UK supermarket scene with "Market Street"—its in-store hub for fresh meat, fish, and baked goods. The key to unlocking great value here is to pair that freshness with their digital loyalty app and weekly deals. Let's break down the Morrisons offers this week.

My Morrisons: Your Digital Key to Savings

The My Morrisons app is your gateway to personalized savings. Forget collecting tiny points on every purchase; this app delivers tailored digital coupons based on what you actually buy. The more you use it, the smarter (and better) your offers become.

Your Morrisons Game Plan:

  1. Activate Before You Shop: This is crucial. Open the app before you head to the store and activate all your personalized offers. They'll apply automatically when you scan your card.
  2. Master Market Street: The deals at the butcher and fishmonger counters often provide better value and quality than their pre-packaged counterparts. Don't be afraid to ask for advice!
  3. Become a "Yellow Sticker" Hunter: Morrisons is famous for its generous end-of-day reductions. A late afternoon or evening visit can yield some incredible bargains on items nearing their sell-by date.
  4. Stack Your Savings: Your personalized app offers can be used on top of the general Morrisons weekly offers you find on Risij.uk. This is how you achieve maximum discount.

Who is Morrisons For?

If you prioritize fresh food and appreciate a traditional supermarket feel, Morrisons is an excellent choice. By leveraging the app and staying updated on the weekly flyer, you can enjoy that quality at a price that competes with rivals like ASDA and Tesco.
