Morrisons Offers This Week: The 2025 Guide to Fresh Savings

Published on January 17, 2025 | Category: Saving Strategies

Morrisons sets itself apart with its "Market Street" concept, offering a traditional butcher, baker, and fishmonger experience within a modern supermarket. This focus on fresh, combined with a revamped loyalty scheme, creates unique saving opportunities. This is your guide to the Morrisons offers this week.

Unlocking Value with the Morrisons More Card

The Morrisons More card is your gateway to the best prices in-store. It functions in two primary ways:

  • More Card Exclusives: These are instant, member-only discounts on a wide range of products. Similar to other supermarket schemes, you simply scan your card or app to get the lower price.
  • Points for Pounds: You earn More Points on specific products and at the fuel pumps. Once you hit 5,000 points, you can convert them into a "Morrisons Fiver" voucher to spend on your shopping.

Where to Find the Best Morrisons Deals

  1. Target Market Street: The fresh food counters are not just for quality; they're for value. Look for multi-buy deals on fresh meat, fish, and deli items that often provide better quality and price than their pre-packaged counterparts.
  2. Activate Your App Offers: Before you shop, open the Morrisons More app and activate your personalized offers. These are tailored to your buying habits and can provide significant extra savings.
  3. Use "Too Good To Go": Many Morrisons stores partner with the Too Good To Go app to sell "Magic Bags" of surplus food at a deep discount at the end of the day. It's a fantastic way to save money and fight food waste.

By focusing on fresh deals from Market Street and diligently managing your More card offers, you can make your Morrisons shop both high-quality and high-value. Stay updated on all the key promotions on the Risij.uk main site.
