Get Ready for Next Week's Aldi Finds

Updated: November 05, 2025 | Category: Shopping Tips

Shopping at Aldi can feel like a treasure hunt, especially when it comes to their famous 'Specialbuys'. The key to a great shop is planning ahead. Looking at the Aldi offers next week in advance is the secret to grabbing the best deals. Here’s our simple 2026 guide to help you prepare.

Step 1: Check Out the Leaflet Early

Aldi's Specialbuys are amazing limited-time items that arrive every Thursday and Sunday. The best way to know what's coming is to check the online leaflet.

Our Tip: Plan Your Must-Haves

The leaflet tells you what's on the way, from garden furniture to kitchen gadgets. We review this info and post the highlights on our Aldi offers page at Risij.uk. This helps you decide what you really want before you go to the store.

Step 2: Simple In-Store Tips

  • Go Early: Popular Specialbuys can sell out quickly. Arriving early on Thursday or Sunday gives you the best chance to find what you're looking for.
  • Head to the Middle: The middle aisles are where you'll find the Specialbuys. Grab your favourites there first, then do your regular grocery shopping.
  • Stick to Your List: It's easy to get excited by all the great deals! Having a list and a budget in mind helps you buy what you need without overspending.
