How to Shop Smart and Save Money at Waitrose

Published: October 15, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

Waitrose is synonymous with high-quality food, but that doesn't mean it has to come with a premium price tag. With a strategic approach centered on their loyalty scheme, you can easily find great value. Here's how to shop smart with the Waitrose offers this week.

The myWaitrose Card is Your Best Tool

The free myWaitrose loyalty card is essential for any Waitrose shopper. It unlocks several ways to save.

  • Personalised Vouchers: Check your app or emails every week for a selection of money-off vouchers tailored to your shopping habits. You choose which ones to use.
  • Exclusive Member Prices: Just like other supermarkets, Waitrose offers instant discounts to members. Look for the special myWaitrose price signs in-store and scan your card to get the lower price.
  • Fish & Meat Counter Deals: myWaitrose members often get a discount (e.g., 20% off) at the fresh fish and meat counters on certain days of the week, typically Friday and Saturday.

Choose the "Essential Waitrose" Range

One of the easiest ways to cut costs is by opting for the store's own brand. The "Essential Waitrose" range includes hundreds of everyday products, from pasta and tinned tomatoes to cheese and cleaning supplies. The quality is excellent, and the prices are highly competitive.

Look for End-of-Day Reductions

Waitrose is a fantastic place to hunt for "yellow sticker" bargains. In the evenings, the store reduces the price of fresh items nearing their sell-by date. This is a brilliant opportunity to buy premium ready meals, bakery items, and high-quality meat for a fraction of the original cost.
