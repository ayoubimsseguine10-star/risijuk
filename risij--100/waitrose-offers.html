Intel Drop: October 15, 2024 | Target: Premium Supermarkets

Hacking the myWaitrose Program

Shopping at Waitrose doesn't have to break the bank. You just need to be smarter than the average shopper. The myWaitrose card is your key. It gives you personalized vouchers and member-only prices that slash costs. Check our analysis of the Waitrose offers this week on Risij.uk to see where the myWaitrose deals can give you the biggest advantage.

The Waitrose Protocol:

  • Check the myWaitrose app before you shop and activate your personalized vouchers. It's a must-do.
  • The free coffee and newspaper are nice perks, but the real value is in the member-only product discounts.
  • Focus on items that have a specific myWaitrose price to get exclusive deals.

The "Essential" Foundation Strategy

Here's the pro strategy: build the foundation of your shopping list with products from the "Essential Waitrose" range. They're competitively priced but still meet high quality standards. Then, use the weekly promotions to add the more premium, luxury items to your basket at a discount. This two-pronged attack gives you the best of both worlds: quality and value.
