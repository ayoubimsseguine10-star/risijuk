A Simple Guide to Saving at Morrisons

Category: Shopping Tips

Morrisons offers a unique mix of modern digital deals and traditional fresh food counters. To get the best value, you need a two-part strategy: master the Morrisons More app and make smart use of 'Market Street'. Here's what you need to know about the Morrisons offers this week.

Part 1: Use the Morrisons More App

The Morrisons More loyalty scheme is now app-based and gives you direct discounts. To make it work for you, follow these simple steps:

  • Activate Your Offers: Before you shop, open the app and check your personalised offers. You have to tap to 'activate' them before you get to the till.
  • Look for 'More Exclusives': In-store, keep an eye out for the special shelf labels for 'More Exclusives'. These are instant discounts you get just for scanning your card.
  • Earn Points on Fuel: Don't forget you get 5 points for every litre of fuel you buy at a Morrisons petrol station. These points add up to money-off vouchers for your groceries.

Part 2: Shop Smart at 'Market Street'

Morrisons' 'Market Street' – with its in-store butcher, fishmonger, and bakery – is its secret weapon. Here's why it's great for saving money:

  • Buy Exactly What You Need: Instead of being forced to buy a pre-set pack size, you can ask for the exact amount you want. This reduces food waste and saves money.
  • Find Unique Offers: The counters often have their own special deals that you won't find in the main aisles.

By checking the app for deals and then visiting Market Street for your fresh items, you get the best of both worlds. See all the latest 'More Exclusives' on the Morrisons page on Risij.uk.
