A 2025 Tactical Guide to This Week's Lidl Offers

Published: December 2024 // Category: Savings Strategy

Shopping at Lidl is a unique experience, blending everyday grocery savings with the thrill of discovering limited-time bargains. A smart approach is key to getting the most value. This guide provides a clear strategy for mastering the Lidl offers this week.

The Main Attraction: The "Middle of Lidl"

The central aisle, famously known as the "Middle of Lidl," is where you'll find an eclectic mix of non-food items, from electronics to gardening tools. New products arrive every Thursday and Sunday, and the best deals disappear quickly. Planning ahead is essential.

Your Essential Tool: The Lidl Plus App

The Lidl Plus app is a must-have for any regular Lidl shopper. It offers several ways to save:

  • Weekly Coupons: The app provides a new set of digital coupons each week. You must activate these coupons in the app *before* you scan your card at the checkout.
  • Coupon Plus: This feature rewards you for your monthly spending. As you hit certain spending targets, you unlock valuable rewards, such as free items or money-off coupons.
  • Partner Offers & Digital Receipts: The app also gives you access to discounts with other companies and conveniently stores all your receipts digitally.

Don't Forget Theme Weeks

Look out for Lidl's weekly culinary themes, such as "Italian Week" or "Alpine Week." These events are a great opportunity to buy authentic, specialty products at very competitive prices.

Plan Your Lidl Shop in Advance

To see what's coming to the "Middle of Lidl" and pre-activate your coupons, visit the official Lidl offers this week page on the Risij.uk platform.
