Using the Morrisons More Card with Market Street

Category: Savings Guides | Published: October 2024

Morrisons stands out with its in-store "Market Street," featuring specialist butchers, bakers, and fishmongers. The most effective shopping strategy involves pairing this fresh food with the benefits of the Morrisons More loyalty program.

How the Morrisons More Program Works

The Morrisons More card is a points-based loyalty scheme. You earn points on fuel and selected products in-store. When you accumulate 5,000 points, you receive a Â£5 voucher. The associated app also provides personalized digital coupons.

A Practical Shopping Strategy for Morrisons

  • Activate Offers in the App: Before you shop, open the More app to activate your personalized coupons. These offers are based on your previous purchases and help you accumulate points more quickly.
  • Utilize the Market Street Counters: Instead of buying pre-packaged items, you can ask the staff at the fresh food counters for the exact amount you need. This can be more cost-effective and helps reduce food waste.
  • Look for "More Card Exclusives": Throughout the store, certain products have a lower price exclusively for More card holders. These deals, part of the Morrisons offers this week, provide instant savings.
  • Consider the "Wonky" Range: For fruit and vegetables, the "Naturally Wonky" range offers produce with minor imperfections at a lower price point.

Actionable Tip: Plan your meals by reviewing the latest More Card Exclusives. You can see a complete list of Morrisons offers for this week on our main website.

By using the More card consistently and taking advantage of the unique fresh food counters, you can shop for high-quality food while staying within your budget.
