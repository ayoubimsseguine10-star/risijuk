A Shopper's Guide to Morrisons Offers & The 'My Morrisons' App

Published on July 29, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Programs

Morrisons stands out with its "Market Street," offering fresh meat, fish, and baked goods prepared in-store. Combining this focus on freshness with their loyalty program and weekly deals is the key to great value. Let's explore the Morrisons offers this week and the benefits of the 'My Morrisons' app.

Unlocking Savings with 'My Morrisons'

The 'My Morrisons' app (formerly Morrisons More) is a digital-first loyalty scheme that provides personalized offers. Instead of collecting points on every pound spent, the app gives you digital coupons based on your shopping habits. The more you shop, the more tailored your offers become.

Maximizing Your Morrisons Shop

  1. Activate Your Offers: Before you shop, open the app and activate your personal offers. They will be automatically applied when you scan your card at checkout.
  2. Visit Market Street: The deals at the butcher, fishmonger, and bakery counters are often better than their pre-packaged equivalents.
  3. Look for the Yellow Stickers: Morrisons is excellent for end-of-day reductions. Time your visit for late afternoon or evening for the best chance at clearance bargains.
  4. Check the Main Flyer: Your personal offers work on top of the general Morrisons weekly offers, which you can find on Risij.uk. This is how you "stack" savings.

Is Morrisons Right for You?

If you value fresh food and enjoy a more traditional supermarket experience, Morrisons is a fantastic choice. By using the app and keeping an eye on the weekly flyer, it can be just as budget-friendly as its competitors like ASDA or Tesco.
