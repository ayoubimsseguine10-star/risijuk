A Savvy Shopper's Guide to Tesco Offers This Week

Published on: 24th July 2024 | Category: Supermarket Deals

Tesco is a giant in the UK grocery market, and for good reason. With a massive range of products and a powerful loyalty scheme, it offers countless ways to save. The secret weapon for any Tesco shopper is the Clubcard. This guide will show you how to use it to get the best Tesco offers this week.

Clubcard Prices: The Key to Savings

The most significant change in Tesco's pricing strategy has been the introduction of "Clubcard Prices." Hundreds of items across the store have two prices: the regular price and a much lower price exclusively for Clubcard holders. If you shop at Tesco, having a Clubcard is non-negotiable; the savings are too big to ignore.

How to Get the Most from Your Clubcard:

  • Scan Every Time: Make it a habit to scan your Clubcard (or the app) with every single purchase, no matter how small.
  • Check the App: The Tesco Grocery & Clubcard app often has exclusive coupons and lets you view your points balance.
  • Redeem Vouchers Wisely: Every three months, your points are converted into vouchers. While you can spend them on your shopping, you can often double or triple their value by exchanging them with Tesco's Reward Partners for things like restaurant meals or days out.

Don't Pay More Than You Have To! For a full list of current Clubcard Prices, visit the Tesco Offers This Week page on Risij.uk.

Look for Aldi Price Match

Tesco actively price matches hundreds of its staple products against Aldi. Look for the red "Aldi Price Match" bubble next to items like bread, milk, vegetables, and pasta. This ensures you can get the core of your weekly shop at competitive, low prices without needing to visit another store.

Your Tesco Game Plan

Before you shop, take a moment to check the latest offers online. Build your shopping list around the Clubcard Prices and Aldi Price Match items. This two-pronged approach ensures you're accessing exclusive discounts while also getting great value on your everyday essentials.
