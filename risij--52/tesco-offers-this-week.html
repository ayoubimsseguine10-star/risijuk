Unlocking Value with Tesco Offers This Week

Published on November 4, 2023 | Category: Smart Shopping

Tesco has revolutionized supermarket savings with its Clubcard Prices scheme. For those with a loyalty card, the discounts can be dramatic, making it one of the most powerful saving tools available. Understanding the Tesco offers this week is all about understanding the Clubcard.

Making the Clubcard Work for You

A Clubcard is free and essential for any Tesco shopper. The difference between the regular price and the Clubcard price can be as much as 50% on some items. From weekly staples to the Finest range, Clubcard Prices are applied to a vast array of products.

Your Tesco Savings Plan:

  • Never Shop Without Your Clubcard: Whether it's the physical card or on the Tesco Grocery & Clubcard app, make sure you scan it at checkout.
  • Look for the Yellow Labels: Clubcard Prices are clearly marked with bright yellow shelf labels that show both the regular and the member price.
  • Check the App for Coupons: The Tesco app delivers personalised coupons that you can stack on top of Clubcard Prices for extra savings.
  • Turn Points into Vouchers: Every 150 points earned becomes a £1.50 voucher, which can be spent on groceries or tripled in value with Reward Partners.
Forgetting your Clubcard at Tesco is like willingly paying more for the same items. It's the most important thing to have in your wallet.
