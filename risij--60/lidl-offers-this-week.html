A Savvy Shopper's Guide to Lidl Offers This Week

Published on July 26, 2024 | Category: Shopping Tips

Lidl has won the hearts of UK shoppers with its high-quality products at budget-friendly prices. To truly master shopping at Lidl, you need a strategy for tackling its weekly food deals and the legendary "Middle of Lidl." Here’s how to make the most of the Lidl offers this week.

The "Middle of Lidl" Explained

The "Middle of Lidl" refers to the central aisles where non-food items are sold. These change every Thursday and Sunday and can include anything from DIY tools and kitchen gadgets to clothing and fitness equipment. The key things to know are:

  • They are limited stock: Popular items can sell out very quickly, sometimes on the first day.
  • Check the leaflet first: Lidl releases its leaflet a week in advance, so you can see what's coming up and plan your visit.
  • Themes change weekly: One week might be "Kitchen Essentials," the next could be "Gardening Week."

Maximise Savings with the Lidl Plus App

The Lidl Plus app is a must-have for any regular Lidl shopper. It offers several ways to save:

  1. Weekly Coupons: Every week, you get a new set of coupons for discounts on specific products. You must activate them in the app before scanning at the till.
  2. Coupon Plus: The more you spend during a month, the more coupons you unlock. For example, spend £100 and get a £2 coupon.
  3. Digital Receipts: The app stores all your receipts, which is great for budgeting and makes returns easy.
  4. Partner Offers: Get discounts on other services like cinema tickets or magazine subscriptions.

By combining savvy Middle Aisle shopping with the Lidl Plus app, you can ensure you're getting the absolute best value. Risij.uk conveniently lists all the upcoming Middle of Lidl themes and top food offers each week.
