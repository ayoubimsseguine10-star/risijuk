Unlocking the Value of Your Tesco Clubcard

Published: Oct 16, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Guides

The Tesco Clubcard has become one of the best tools for saving money on your weekly shop. With Clubcard Prices, it's more important than ever to use it! Here’s our simple guide to getting the best value from the Tesco offers this week.

First Things First: Clubcard Prices

You've probably seen them in-store: special lower prices just for Clubcard holders. The discounts can be huge, so the most important tip is to scan your Clubcard (or the app on your phone) every single time you shop. If you don't, you're missing out on easy savings!

Simple Trick #1: Always Have it Ready

Make it a habit to get your Clubcard out as soon as you get to the checkout. The easiest way to remember is to use the Tesco app on your phone, as you'll always have it with you. No scan means no discount!

Simple Trick #2: Make Your Points Worth More!

This is where the real magic happens. You can use your Clubcard points to get money off your shopping, but there's a much better way! With Tesco's Reward Partners, you can often triple the value of your points. This means £5 in points could become £15 to spend on a family day out or a meal at your favourite restaurant.

Doing the Supermarket Maths

Always have a look at how a Clubcard Price compares to a multi-buy deal. Sometimes, buying one item with your Clubcard discount is cheaper than a "2 for £X" deal on something similar. It's a skill that's also very useful for checking the Sainsbury's offers this week when you're comparing prices.

Top Tip: Before you spend your Clubcard vouchers in-store, check the Reward Partners section of the Tesco website. You might find a much better deal for a fun treat!
