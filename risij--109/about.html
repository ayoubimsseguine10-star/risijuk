Our Mission: Consumer Empowerment Through Data

The mission of Risij.uk is to democratize access to retail market data, providing UK consumers with the intelligence required to navigate an increasingly complex pricing landscape and optimize household expenditure.

Our Methodology

Our core platform, Risij.uk, aggregates and processes vast quantities of publicly available pricing data from the UK's leading supermarket retailers.

This blog, the Market Brief, is where our analysts distill that raw data into actionable intelligence. We provide:

  • Strategic Analyses: In-depth reports on retailer loyalty programs, promotional cycles, and pricing strategies.
  • Weekly Briefings: Concise summaries of key market movements, from the latest Tesco offers this week to emergent trends in the discount sector.
  • Objective Reporting: Unbiased assessments of value propositions, free from commercial influence.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing clarity and transparency in the UK retail sector. We believe that an informed consumer is an empowered consumer. Thank you for utilizing our service.
