How to Shop Affordably at Waitrose

Category: Savings Guides | Published: October 2024

Waitrose is known for its high-quality products, but it is possible to shop there without overspending. An effective approach combines the use of the myWaitrose loyalty program with their extensive own-brand range.

A Two-Part Strategy for Waitrose Savings

The best way to manage your budget at Waitrose is to focus on two key areas: the free myWaitrose card for personalized discounts, and the "Essential Waitrose" product line for everyday value.

Your Practical Waitrose Shopping Plan

  • Select Your Vouchers Weekly: The myWaitrose card allows you to choose your own personalized money-off vouchers each week through the app or website. Select offers for products you already plan to buy.
  • Base Your Shop on the 'Essential' Range: The "Essential Waitrose" line includes hundreds of everyday items that offer Waitrose quality at competitive prices. Make these products the foundation of your shopping list.
  • Look for 'Dine In' Offers: Waitrose frequently offers "Dine In" meal deals, which bundle a main course, side, and dessert or wine for a set price. This is often much cheaper than buying the items separately.
  • Check the Clearance Section: Like all supermarkets, Waitrose has a reduced-to-clear section where you can find items nearing their expiry date at a significant discount. This is often a good place to find bargains, particularly in the evening.

Actionable Tip: Before you shop, check for the latest member deals and view the full list of Waitrose offers for this week on our main website.

By using personalized myWaitrose vouchers and making the Essential range the core of your shopping list, you can enjoy the quality of Waitrose while adhering to your grocery budget.
