Finding Value in Convenience: A Guide to Spar

Published: October 18, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

Spar is the go-to store for last-minute essentials and quick meal solutions. But beyond the convenience, savvy shoppers can find some excellent deals. Knowing what to look for can make your local Spar a valuable part of your savings strategy. Here's a look at the typical Spar offers next week.

Look for the Meal Bundles

Spar excels at putting together easy meal solutions at a sharp price point. These are often the best source of value in the store.

  • Lunchtime Meal Deals: A classic favourite, Spar's meal deals for a sandwich, snack, and drink are usually very competitive.
  • 'Tonight's Tea' Offers: Keep an eye out for evening meal bundles, which might include a pizza and a side, or a ready meal and dessert for a fixed price.
  • Drinks Promotions: Spar frequently runs strong promotions on wine, beer, and soft drinks, perfect when you're stocking up for the weekend.

Don't Overlook the Own-Brand Range

A simple way to save at any convenience store is to choose the own-brand option. Spar's own-brand products cover a huge range of categories, from pantry staples like pasta and sauces to biscuits and snacks. They typically offer the same quality as branded alternatives at a significantly lower price.

Check Your Local Store's Deals

Many Spar stores are independently operated, which means they often run their own unique deals alongside the national promotions. It always pays to keep your eyes peeled for special signs and clearance sections in your neighbourhood store.
