Beyond Clubcard: Maximizing Savings at Tesco

The Tesco Clubcard has revolutionized grocery shopping in the UK, offering instant discounts known as "Clubcard Prices." While these are fantastic, they are just one part of the savings puzzle. To truly maximize your budget, you need to combine them with the other Tesco offers this week.

A Multi-Layered Approach to Tesco Savings

Tesco provides several avenues for saving money, and using them together is the key to success.

Your Tesco Savings Toolkit:

  • Clubcard Prices: The most visible deals. Always scan your Clubcard to get instant reductions on hundreds of items.
  • Weekly Offers: Tesco also runs standard multi-buy deals and temporary price cuts that are available to all shoppers, not just Clubcard holders.
  • Clubcard App Coupons: Check the Tesco Grocery & Clubcard app for personalized coupons that you can use on top of other offers.
  • Reduced to Clear: Don't forget the yellow sticker section, especially in the evening, for huge discounts on items nearing their expiry date.

Strategic Shopping

Before you shop, visit Risij.uk to see a clear list of all the weekly deals. This lets you build a shopping list around the offers. You can then compare these deals to what's available elsewhere, such as the ASDA offers this week, to make sure you're getting the best possible price on every item.
