Navigating Morrisons' Weekly Savers & Market Street Deals

Category: Shopping Guides

Morrisons stands out with its "Market Street," a collection of in-store counters offering fresh produce, meat, fish, and baked goods. Smart shoppers know that combining Market Street quality with the weekly Morrisons offers this week is the recipe for great meals at a lower cost.

How to Get the Best Value at Morrisons

Your strategy at Morrisons should blend loyalty rewards with a keen eye for their fresh food promotions.

  • My Morrisons Rewards: This app-based scheme provides personalized money-off coupons based on what you typically buy. Activate them before you shop.
  • Market Street Offers: Look for special deals at the butcher, fishmonger, and bakery counters. These are often fresher and better value than pre-packaged alternatives.
  • Seasonal Events: Morrisons runs excellent promotions around holidays and seasons, offering great prices on everything from BBQ essentials to Christmas treats.

Top Tips for Shopping at Morrisons

1. Check the My Morrisons App First

Before you even write your list, open the app to see your personalized offers. This can help shape your meal plan for the week and ensure you don't miss any savings.

2. Don't Skip Market Street

Engage with the staff at the counters. They can offer advice on the best cuts of meat or the freshest fish, and you can buy the exact quantity you need, reducing waste.

3. Look for the Yellow Stickers

Like many supermarkets, Morrisons reduces items later in the day. Their "Magic Boxes" on the Too Good To Go app are also a fantastic way to get a surprise selection of food for a low price.
