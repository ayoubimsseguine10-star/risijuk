Beyond the Points: Supercharge Your Savings with Tesco Offers

Published on July 26, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Programs

Many shoppers see the Tesco Clubcard as a way to collect points for a rainy day. The savvy shopper, however, knows its true power: it's a strategic tool for unlocking immediate, significant savings. The key is to actively combine its benefits with the regular Tesco offers this week.

Unlocking Clubcard Prices

Tesco's pricing strategy often includes two tiers: a standard price and a deeply discounted "Clubcard Price." These exclusive prices are where the real power of membership lies. By planning your shop around items with a Clubcard Price, you can dramatically lower your grocery bill. On Risij.uk, we always highlight these special deals.

Your Tesco Savings Checklist

  1. Never Shop Without It: Make scanning your Clubcard or app an unbreakable habit. It's the only way to access instant discounts and accumulate points for future vouchers or partner rewards.
  2. Activate Your App Coupons: Before you shop, open the Tesco app. It often contains personalized coupons based on your buying history. Activate these to apply them automatically at checkout.
  3. Practice "Value Stacking": This is the pro move. Find an item that has a Clubcard Price and also has a personalized coupon available in your app. Combining these two discounts is how you achieve maximum savings.
  4. Stay Competitive: For major branded products, a quick check against competitors is smart. The Sainsbury's offer this week could potentially beat a Clubcard price on a specific item, making you a truly informed shopper.
