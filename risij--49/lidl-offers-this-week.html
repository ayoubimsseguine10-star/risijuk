Lidl's Middle Aisle Magic: A Shopper's Guide

Published on: October 21, 2024 | Category: Shopping Tips

Much like its rival Aldi, Lidl is famous for its central aisle of rotating non-food items, affectionately known as the "Middle of Lidl." This is where you'll find amazing bargains on everything from DIY tools to kitchen gadgets. Let's explore the Lidl offers this week.

Understanding the Weekly Themes

Lidl's non-food offers are typically organized around weekly themes, such as "Italian Week," "DIY Essentials," or "Fitness Gear." These themes dictate the types of products you'll find. The Lidl Plus app and the weekly leaflet are the best places to see what themes are coming up.

Tip 1: Use the Lidl Plus App

The Lidl Plus app is essential for any regular shopper. Not only does it show you the upcoming Middle of Lidl themes, but it also gives you access to exclusive digital coupons that can be scanned at the till for extra savings on your groceries. This is a step up from the offers you'll find at stores like Morrisons, which often require a physical loyalty card.

Tip 2: Shop on Thursday for the Best Selection

New Middle of Lidl stock arrives on a Thursday. For popular themes (like garden furniture in spring), the best items can be gone in a day. If you have your heart set on something, make Thursday your shopping day. This is the same strategy we recommend for the Aldi offers next week.

Don't Overlook the Bakery

While you're hunting for middle aisle bargains, don't forget about Lidl's in-store bakery. It's renowned for its fresh, low-cost pastries, bread, and snacks. It's the perfect reward for a successful shopping trip.
