A Guide to This Week's Morrisons Offers and More Card

Published on October 09, 2024 in Shopping Guides

Morrisons sets itself apart with its unique "Market Street," an in-store area featuring specialist butchers, bakers, and fishmongers. This focus on fresh produce, coupled with a comprehensive loyalty program, provides multiple avenues for savings. This guide examines the Morrisons offers this week.

Leveraging the Morrisons More Program

The Morrisons More card is the central component for maximizing savings. While its core function is a points-based system, its greatest strength is the delivery of personalized digital offers via the Morrisons app.

A More Card Strategy

  • Pre-Shop Offer Activation: Before every shopping trip, consult the Morrisons app or website. You must activate your personalized offers to receive the associated benefits.
  • Consistent Scanning Protocol: Develop the habit of scanning your More card (either physical or digital) at every checkout to ensure you accumulate points and redeem your activated offers.
  • Strategic Point Redemption: Upon reaching 5,000 points, you are issued a "Morrisons Fiver" (£5 voucher). This can be applied to a future grocery bill.

The Value Proposition of Market Street

Market Street is a prime location for high-quality fresh products and some of the store's most compelling promotions.

  1. Butcher's Counter Deals: Be observant of multi-buy promotions, such as "3 for £12," on fresh meat and poultry prepared by the in-store butchers.
  2. Fishmonger Promotions: Many stores feature a "Catch of the Day" with preferential pricing on fresh fish, with promotions often concentrated towards the end of the week.
  3. Bakery Reductions: The in-store bakery frequently offers deals on fresh bread and often reduces prices on remaining stock later in the day.
