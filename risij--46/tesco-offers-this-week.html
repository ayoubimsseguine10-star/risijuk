Maximise Your Clubcard Points with Tesco's Weekly Offers

Published on July 26, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Programs

The Tesco Clubcard is one of the most powerful loyalty programs in UK retail. By strategically combining it with the weekly deals, you can achieve significant savings. The key is understanding how to leverage "Clubcard Prices" alongside the regular Tesco offers this week.

Clubcard Prices vs. Regular Offers

Tesco often has two tiers of discounts: general offers available to everyone, and exclusive "Clubcard Prices" for members. The latter often provide a much deeper discount. By planning your shop around these, you can reduce your bill substantially. Our listings on Risij.uk clearly highlight when a deal requires a Clubcard.

Tips for Tesco Savings

  1. Always Scan Your Card: It seems obvious, but always have your Clubcard (or the app) ready. You'll not only get the instant discounts but also collect points for future rewards.
  2. Check Your Coupons: Tesco regularly sends out personalised coupons based on your shopping habits. Check the app before you shop to activate them.
  3. Stack the Deals: Look for opportunities to use a coupon on an item that's already on a Clubcard Price offer. This "deal stacking" is where the biggest savings are found.
  4. Compare Big Brands: For branded items, it can be worth checking deals at other major supermarkets. For instance, the Sainsbury's offer this week might have a better price on your favourite cereal, even with a Clubcard discount at Tesco.
