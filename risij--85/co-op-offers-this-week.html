A Simple Guide to Co-op Offers This Week

Published on Jan 12, 2025 | In Saving Guides

Your local Co-op is perfect for grabbing things you need quickly, but did you know it's also great for saving money if you're a member? This guide explains how to get the most from the Co-op offers this week.

Why You Should Become a Co-op Member

For just £1, you can become a Co-op member, which is the best way to save. As a member, you get access to:

  • Member Prices: Look for the blue signs in-store. These are special discounts just for members on everyday items.
  • Offers in the App: Each week, you can choose two offers in the Co-op app for products you like to buy.

Plus, when you shop as a member, you're also helping to support local community projects, which is a nice extra bonus!

Other Ways to Find Value

  1. Look for Meal Deals: The Co-op is great for easy dinner solutions. Their pizza deals or freezer filler bundles are often cheaper than buying everything separately.
  2. Check for Reduced Items: Like most supermarkets, the Co-op reduces the price of items that are close to their sell-by date. You can find some great bargains if you shop at the right time of day.

We keep an eye on all the best Co-op deals on the main Risij.uk website, so you can always check before you head to your local store.
