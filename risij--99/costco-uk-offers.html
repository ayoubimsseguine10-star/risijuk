A Beginner's Guide to Shopping at Costco

Published on Jan 13, 2025 | In Savings Guides

Costco can feel a bit daunting at first, but it's a wonderful place to save money by buying in bulk. A membership is your ticket in, and this little guide will help you get the most value from the Costco UK offers.

Is Bulk-Buying Right for You?

The main idea behind Costco is that buying a larger amount of something works out cheaper per item. This is fantastic for things you use all the time and that last for a long while, like toilet paper, cleaning supplies, and store cupboard essentials.

Four Friendly Tips for Your First Costco Visit

  1. Check the Coupon Book: Costco sends out a booklet of special offers every month. It's a great idea to have a look through it to see what the best deals are. We always share the highlights on the Risij.uk site!
  2. Give the Kirkland Brand a Try: Kirkland is Costco's own brand, and it's known for being great quality at a fair price. It's often just as good as the big brands, but for less.
  3. Spot the Special Markdowns: A little insider tip! If you see a price that ends in .97, it usually means it's on clearance and won't be back in stock. It can be a great time to grab a bargain.
  4. Share With a Friend: Sometimes the packs at Costco can be very big! A lovely idea is to go with a friend or family member and split some of the bigger purchases. That way, you both get to enjoy the savings.

With these simple tips, we hope your Costco membership becomes a wonderful way to save money for your household.
