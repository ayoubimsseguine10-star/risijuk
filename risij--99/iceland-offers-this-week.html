A Guide to the Iceland Bonus Card

Published on Jan 15, 2025 | In Savings Guides

Iceland is fantastic for its multi-buy deals, but did you know there's a simple way to get even more value? It's called the Iceland Bonus Card, and it's a lovely way to get a little extra back. Here's our guide to the Iceland offers this week.

Your Little Helper: The Bonus Card

The Bonus Card is a bit different from other loyalty cards. It's a saving scheme that gives you a nice little bonus. For every £20 you save onto your card, Iceland adds an extra £1 for you. That's like getting a 5% bonus before you even start shopping! It's one of the most generous schemes out there.

A Simple Shopping Idea

The best way to shop at Iceland is to combine your Bonus Card savings with their other great offers:

  • Look for Multi-Buys: Keep an eye out for the big "3 for £10" and "3 for £5" deals. These are perfect for stocking up the freezer with pizzas, ice cream, and family meals.
  • Explore the £1 Aisles: Don't forget to have a wander down the £1 aisles. You can often find brilliant basics like frozen veg and chips for a great price.

So, a lovely way to shop is to save some money on your Bonus Card to get your £1 bonus, and then use that money to buy items that are already on a great multi-buy deal. We list all the current Iceland offers on Risij.uk to help you spot the best bargains.
