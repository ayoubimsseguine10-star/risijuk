A Shopper's Guide to Morrisons Offers This Week

Published on November 16, 2024 | Category: Strategy

Morrisons stands out with its "Market Street" fresh food counters and a strong digital rewards program. An effective shopping trip combines the quality of Market Street with the personalized savings of their app. This guide covers how to get the most from the Morrisons offers this week.

The My Morrisons App

Your primary tool for saving at Morrisons is the My Morrisons app. It provides personalized coupons, member-only prices, and basket bonuses. It's essential for any regular shopper.

Key Tactics for Saving at Morrisons:

  • Activate App Offers Before You Shop: Open the My Morrisons app and activate your personalized offers before you head to the checkout.
  • Check Market Street for Deals: The butcher, fishmonger, and baker counters often have special promotions that offer better value than pre-packaged alternatives.
  • Shop Later for Reductions: Morrisons is known for its "yellow sticker" clearance items. Visiting in the late afternoon or evening increases your chance of finding them.
  • Look for Fuel Promotions: Morrisons often runs deals where spending a certain amount on groceries gets you a voucher for money off fuel.

Always check and activate offers in the My Morrisons app before you start your shop. It's the easiest way to guarantee savings.
