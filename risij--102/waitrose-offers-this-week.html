Finding Quality Savings at Waitrose

Category: Shopping Tips

Waitrose is known for its high-quality food, but that doesn't mean you can't find great value. By using the myWaitrose loyalty scheme and shopping strategically, you can enjoy premium groceries without the premium price tag. Here’s how to approach the Waitrose offers this week.

Your Key Tool: The myWaitrose Card

The myWaitrose scheme isn't about collecting points. Instead, it gives you immediate, valuable perks and personalised discounts.

How to Use myWaitrose Effectively:

  • Choose Your Vouchers: Every week, check the Waitrose app or website to choose personalised vouchers for money off the products you buy most often.
  • Look for Member Prices: In-store, keep an eye out for special prices that are only available to myWaitrose members. Just scan your card to get the discount.
  • Enjoy the Perks: Don't forget the extra benefits, like a free newspaper when you spend £10, and 20% off the fish counter on Fridays.

Shop Smart with Waitrose's Own Brands

Waitrose has a fantastic range of own-brand products that can help you manage your budget. A smart strategy is to:

  • Stock up on 'Essential Waitrose': This range offers great quality on everyday items at a very competitive price.
  • Treat yourself with 'No.1 Waitrose': This is the premium range. Look for these items when they are part of the weekly offers for a taste of luxury at a lower price.

To plan your shop, check the latest promotions on the Waitrose page at Risij.uk. This will help you spot the best value before you go.
