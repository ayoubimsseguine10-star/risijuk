Our Mission: To Simplify Savings for Everyone.

We're dedicated to cutting through the noise of supermarket promotions to bring you clarity and real value.

The Idea Behind Risij.uk

In a world of rising costs, finding the best value on your weekly shop shouldn't be a challenge. That’s why we built Risij.uk.

We grew tired of visiting multiple websites and downloading different apps just to compare prices. We envisioned a single, streamlined platform where all UK supermarket offers were accessible and easy to compare. We are a team of developers and savvy shoppers committed to making that vision a reality.

The Role of This Blog

Our main website provides the raw data; this blog provides the strategy. While Risij.uk shows you the latest Lidl offers this week, this blog teaches you how to plan your visit. While the main site lists the current Tesco offers this week, here we explain the nuances of the Clubcard scheme.

We aim to provide:

  • Clear Guides: Simple, actionable advice for your next shopping trip.
  • Honest Analysis: We dig into which promotions offer genuine savings.
  • Long-Term Strategies: Helping you build a sustainable approach to managing your grocery budget.

Thank you for trusting us to be part of your savings journey. We're committed to helping UK households shop smarter and save more.
