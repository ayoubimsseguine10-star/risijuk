A Simple Guide to Nectar Savings at Sainsbury's

Simple Guide | Updated: October 2024

At Sainsbury's, your Nectar card is more than just for collecting points – it's the key to unlocking lower prices. To get the best value from the Sainsbury's offers this week, you just need to get into the habit of using your card every time.

The Three Parts of Nectar Savings

There are three easy ways your Nectar card helps you save:

  • Nectar Prices: This is the biggest benefit. You'll see items with two prices on the shelf – a regular price and a much lower one for Nectar members.
  • The Nectar App: The free app sends you special bonus offers every week based on what you normally buy, helping you collect points faster.
  • SmartShop: This is the in-store scanner (you can use a handset or your phone). It beeps to let you know about deals on things you're walking past, so you don't miss out.

Your 3-Step Sainsbury's Savings Plan

Just follow these simple steps to make sure you're getting the best value:

  1. Always Scan Your Nectar Card: This is the golden rule! If you don't scan your card or app at the till, you won't get the lower Nectar Prices.
  2. Check the App Before You Shop: Open the Nectar app before you go and save your weekly bonus offers. This gets them ready to use when you check out.
  3. Use the SmartShop Scanner: Using SmartShop is a great habit. It makes checkout quicker and points out deals you might have missed.
