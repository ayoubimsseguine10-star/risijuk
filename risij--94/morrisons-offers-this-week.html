The Morrisons More & Market Street Playbook

Published on Jan 17, 2025 | In Savings Guides

Morrisons offers a unique strategic advantage: its "Market Street" fresh counters. Combined with the Morrisons More loyalty program, this creates a powerful savings dynamic. This playbook will show you how to exploit both to win the Morrisons offers this week.

THE MORRISONS MORE CARD: YOUR KEY ASSET

The More card is your primary tool for unlocking value. It operates on two levels:

  • MORE CARD EXCLUSIVES: These are direct, member-only price reductions. Scanning your card or app is mandatory to access this lower pricing tier.
  • POINTS-TO-VOUCHER CONVERSION: Accumulate points on targeted products and fuel purchases. Reaching the threshold allows conversion to a £5 voucher, effectively a rebate on past spending.

YOUR OPERATIONAL DIRECTIVES

  1. PRIORITIZE MARKET STREET: The fresh food counters are a goldmine. Analyze their multi-buy deals on proteins and baked goods; they often provide superior value compared to pre-packaged equivalents.
  2. ACTIVATE PERSONALIZED OFFERS: The Morrisons More app delivers targeted offers based on your purchasing history. You must check the app and activate these offers before your shop to benefit from them.
  3. DEPLOY "TOO GOOD TO GO": Many Morrisons locations use the Too Good To Go app to offload surplus food at a significant discount. This is an excellent tactic for acquiring low-cost goods at the end of the day.
