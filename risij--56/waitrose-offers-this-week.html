How to Shop Smartly with Waitrose Offers This Week

Posted on October 16, 2023 in Quality Savings

Waitrose is known for its high-quality produce and premium shopping experience, but that doesn't mean you can't find a bargain. With the right approach and a myWaitrose card, you can enjoy quality for less. Let's dive into the Waitrose offers this week.

The myWaitrose Card: Your Key to Savings

The myWaitrose loyalty program is different from points-based schemes. It focuses on providing immediate, personalized value. Key benefits include:

  • Personalised Vouchers: Each week, you can choose two personalised vouchers from a selection based on your shopping history, giving you discounts on items you actually buy.
  • Exclusive Member Prices: Look for the myWaitrose signs in-store for special discounts on a wide range of products, from everyday essentials to fine wines.
  • Freebies and Perks: While the free coffee has changed, members still get access to perks like a free newspaper when they spend a certain amount.

Where to Find the Best Value

Beyond the myWaitrose card, there are other ways to save:

  1. Waitrose Essentials Range: This is Waitrose's own-brand value range. It offers hundreds of products at competitive prices without compromising on the quality and ethical standards Waitrose is known for.
  2. Dine-In Deals: Waitrose frequently runs excellent "Dine in for £X" meal deals, which include a main, side, dessert, and a bottle of wine or non-alcoholic alternative, offering fantastic value for a high-quality meal.
  3. Reduced to Clear Section: Don't forget to check the yellow-sticker section, usually at the end of aisles. You can find incredible bargains on products nearing their sell-by date.
