A Simple Guide to Waitrose Offers This Week

Published: October 15, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

Waitrose is known for its high-quality food, and with a few simple tricks, you can enjoy it without breaking the bank. The key is to use their free loyalty scheme. Let's take a look at the best Waitrose offers this week.

The myWaitrose Card: Your Best Friend for Savings

The free myWaitrose card is essential for getting good value. It gives you a few great perks:

  • Personalized Vouchers: This is the best bit. Every week, you can open the app and choose two vouchers for money off items you actually want to buy.
  • Member-Only Prices: Look for the green price tags around the store. These are special lower prices just for members on everything from wine to ready meals.
  • Little Extras: You also get a free monthly magazine and other nice surprises from time to time.

The Best 'Dine In' Deal Around

Many people agree that Waitrose's 'Dine In' meal deal is one of the best you can get. It offers a restaurant-quality meal for two—often with a main, side, dessert, and sometimes a drink—for a set price. It’s fantastic value for a special night in.

Shop Smart Across the Store

For your everyday basics, the "Essential Waitrose" range is excellent quality and very reasonably priced. By mixing these essentials with a few premium items using your myWaitrose vouchers, you can have a high-quality shop that still fits your budget.
