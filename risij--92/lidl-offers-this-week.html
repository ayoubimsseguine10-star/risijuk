Lidl Offers: How to Conquer the Middle Aisle

Published October 21, 2024 | In: Savings Guides

Lidl is famous for two things: great value groceries and the chaotic magic of the "Middle of Lidl." To master both, you need a strategy. Here's your simple tactical guide to the Lidl offers this week.

Tactic #1: Use the Lidl Plus App Religiously

This is your secret weapon. The Lidl Plus app is more than just a loyalty card. It's your key to unlocking extra savings. Before every single shop, you must:

  • Activate your coupons: Check the "Coupons" section and activate any deals you might use. They don't apply automatically!
  • Spin the wheel: After you shop, scan your app and you'll get a "spin the wheel" for a chance to win a prize. It's a small thing, but it adds up.

Tactic #2: Plan Your Middle Aisle Raid

The Middle of Lidl is where the best non-food bargains are, but it's a battleground. Here's how to win:

  • Know the theme: Each week has a theme (e.g., DIY, Kitchen, Fitness). Check the leaflet or Risij.uk on Sunday to know what's coming.
  • Go on Thursday: This is when the new stock drops. For popular items like the air fryer or the pizza oven, Thursday morning is your only realistic chance.
  • Be decisive: If you see something you want, grab it. It probably won't be there an hour later.
