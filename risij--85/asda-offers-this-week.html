A Simple Guide to Asda Offers This Week

Published on Jan 11, 2025 | In Saving Guides

Asda has some great ways to save money, but it helps to know what to look for. This simple guide will help you understand the Asda offers this week, focusing on their Rollbacks and the Asda Rewards app.

Two Main Ways to Save at Asda

  • Rollbacks: These are simply temporary price drops on items. You'll see green signs for these all over the store. They're an easy way to get a discount on popular products.
  • Asda Rewards App: This is Asda's loyalty program. You use an app on your phone to earn money back into a "Cashpot." You can do this by buying special "Star Products" or doing little "Missions" in the app.

Simple Tips for Your Asda Shop

  1. Check the App First: Before you shop, open the Asda Rewards app to see the latest "Star Products." If they're things you need, you can earn money back easily.
  2. Plan Meals Around Rollbacks: Have a look at the current Rollbacks on the Risij.uk site. Planning a few meals around discounted items is a great way to lower your bill.
  3. Use Your Cashpot Wisely: You can turn your Cashpot money into vouchers to spend in-store. It can be a good idea to save it up for a larger shop to make a real difference to your budget.

By using the app and keeping an eye out for green Rollback signs, you can make your money go further at Asda.
