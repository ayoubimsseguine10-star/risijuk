A Simple Guide to Asda's Rollback Deals

Published: October 28, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

Asda is famous for its promise of great value, and its "Rollback" deals are a huge part of that. But what does Rollback actually mean? This guide breaks down the Asda offers this week to help you understand how they work and how you can save.

What is a "Rollback"?

A "Rollback" is simply a price drop on an item that lasts for a while. It's not just a quick one-day sale; it’s a lower price on everyday products that you can count on for several weeks. It's Asda's way of showing you they're offering long-term value.

How to Save More at Asda

  • Look for Rollback Signs: It's easy to spot these deals in-store. Just look for the big signs showing that a price has been "rolled back" to a lower cost.
  • Stock Up on Your Favourites: When you see a Rollback on things you use all the time (like coffee, cereal, or cleaning supplies), it's a great time to buy a few to stock up your pantry.
  • Download the Asda Rewards App: This is a must-have for Asda shoppers. You earn "pounds, not points" by completing missions in the app. You build up a cashpot that you can turn into vouchers to spend on your shopping.

More Than Just Rollbacks

Keep an eye on the Asda Rewards app for special "Star Product" offers that give you bonus rewards. Combining these app deals with the in-store Rollbacks is a smart way to get the most value out of every trip to Asda, making it very competitive with other big stores like Morrisons.
