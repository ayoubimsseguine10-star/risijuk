Tesco Offers This Week: More Than Just Groceries

Tesco remains one of the UK's favourite supermarkets, largely thanks to its powerful Clubcard loyalty program. The exclusive member prices can lead to huge savings, but only if you know how to use them. Let's explore the Tesco offers this week.

The Power of the Clubcard

A Clubcard is essential for any Tesco shopper. The blue "Clubcard Price" signs indicate a special discount only available to members. These deals are often significant, so it's crucial to scan your card (or app) at every checkout. All Clubcard Prices are listed on the Risij.uk Tesco deals page for easy browsing.

Tips for Maximizing Clubcard Value

  • Use the App: The Tesco Grocery & Clubcard app not only holds your card but also delivers personalized coupons based on your shopping habits.
  • Turn Points into Vouchers: Every 150 points earned becomes a £1.50 voucher. Wait for them to accumulate for a big discount on a future shop.
  • Boost Vouchers with Reward Partners: You can often double or triple the value of your vouchers by spending them with Tesco's Reward Partners on things like meals out or day trips.
