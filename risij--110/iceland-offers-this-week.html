A Simple Guide to Saving at Iceland

Simple Guide | Updated: October 2024

Iceland is a go-to for filling the freezer, and they have a really simple way to help you save money. To get the best value from the Iceland offers this week, the key is to use their Bonus Card. It's like a special savings pot!

Iceland's Two Main Ways to Save

A smart Iceland shop is built on two simple things:

  • The Bonus Card: This is a brilliant and easy way to save. For every £20 you add to your Bonus Card, Iceland gives you an extra £1 for free. That's a guaranteed saving before you even start shopping.
  • Great Multi-Buy Deals: The store is famous for its multi-buy offers, especially the "3 for £10" deals on meat and other popular items. These are perfect for stocking up.

Your Easy Iceland Shopping Plan

Just follow these three simple steps every time you visit Iceland:

  1. Top Up Your Bonus Card First: Get into the habit of adding money to your Bonus Card before you shop. That way, you lock in your extra free £1 for every £20 you add.
  2. Plan Around the Multi-Buys: Before you go, check out the latest multi-buy deals. The Risij.uk Iceland page has a handy list. You can plan your meals around these offers to save even more.
  3. Check Out the Whole Store: Iceland is more than just frozen food now. They have a great range of fresh groceries and cupboard items that are often part of their weekly deals.
