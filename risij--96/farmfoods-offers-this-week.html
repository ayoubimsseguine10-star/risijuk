Farmfoods: The Brand Hunter's Guide to Smarter Shopping

Updated: July 2024 | Category: Store Guides

Farmfoods is where savvy shoppers go to hunt for big brands at low prices. It's a no-frills warehouse of bargains, specializing in frozen food and pantry staples. To win here, you need a focused strategy based on multi-buys and leaflet deals, all detailed in the Farmfoods offers this week.

Your Farmfoods Game Plan

This isn't your one-stop-shop for everything, and that's the point. Farmfoods is a targeted mission to stock your freezer and cupboards with brands you love, for less than you'd pay elsewhere.

Key Tactics for Brand Hunters:

  • The Leaflet is Your Map: The Farmfoods leaflet is everything. It's your guide to the hottest deals of the week. Grab one in-store or check it online before you go. Never shop blind.
  • Think in Multiples: The pricing is geared towards multi-buy deals (like "3 for £5" or "2 for £3"). The single-item price is often a decoy; the real value is in buying the bundle.
  • Get Vouchers Emailed to You: Sign up on the Farmfoods website. They'll email you money-off vouchers (£2 off £25, etc.) that you can print and stack on top of the in-store offers for a double-dip saving.

How to Fit Farmfoods into Your Routine

The pro move is to make Farmfoods your first stop. Load up on all your frozen essentials—from pizzas to peas—and grab your favorite branded cereals, sauces, and drinks. Then, head to another store for your fresh items. For example, after your Farmfoods run, check the Morrisons offers this week for deals on their fresh meat and veg counters.
