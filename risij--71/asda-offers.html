A Strategic Guide to Asda's Rollbacks & Rewards

Category: Shopping Guides | Published: October 2024

Asda's commitment to value is a cornerstone of its brand, with two primary tools for shoppers: the iconic "Rollbacks" and the modern Asda Rewards app. Understanding how to use them together is the key to unlocking significant savings on your weekly shop.

The Twin Pillars of Asda Savings

Asda's approach gives you two ways to save. Rollbacks offer immediate discounts at the shelf, while the Rewards app provides cashback on your purchases. A smart shopper uses both.

1. Leveraging Rollbacks

Rollbacks are temporary price reductions on a wide variety of products, from household staples to the latest tech. These are Asda's headline deals, clearly marked in-store. The best strategy is to check the Asda offers this week online before you shop and build your meal plan around these discounted items.

2. Maximizing Asda Rewards

The Asda Rewards app is a cashback system where you build a "Cashpot" to spend later. You earn by:

  • Buying "Star Products": Look for items with a star icon in the app and in-store to earn a percentage of their value back into your Cashpot.
  • Completing "Missions": The app offers challenges, like "spend £5 on fruit and veg," that add bonus cash to your pot.

The trick is to buy Star Products that are also on Rollback, effectively getting a double discount.

Find all the latest Rollbacks and Star Products by viewing the Asda offers for this week on our main site.

By paying attention to both instant reductions and long-term cashback, you can craft a shopping strategy that makes your Asda budget go further than ever before.
