Making the Most of Morrisons Offers This Week

Published on July 15, 2024 in Supermarket Guides

Morrisons stands out with its "Market Street," featuring in-store butchers, bakers, and fishmongers. This focus on freshness, combined with a powerful loyalty scheme, means there are plenty of ways to save. Let's dive into the Morrisons offers this week.

Unlocking Savings with the Morrisons More Card

The Morrisons More card is a points-based system that is essential for any regular shopper. You earn points on selected products and at the fuel station. The key feature, however, is the personalised offers.

How to Use the More Card Effectively

  • Activate Your Offers: Check the Morrisons app or website before you shop and activate personalised offers based on your shopping history. These could be for extra points or money off.
  • Scan at Checkout: Always scan your card (physical or in-app) to collect points and redeem offers.
  • Redeem Fivers: Once you reach 5,000 points, you'll receive a £5 voucher (a "Morrisons Fiver") to spend on your next shop.

Don't Miss Market Street Deals

Market Street is where Morrisons truly shines. You can often find deals here that aren't available in the pre-packaged aisles.

  1. Butcher's Bundles: Look for multi-buy deals on fresh meat, like "3 for £10" on chicken, mince, and sausages.
  2. Fish Friday: Many stores offer special deals on fresh fish every Friday.
  3. Freshly Baked Bread: The bakery often has offers on loaves and rolls, especially towards the end of the day.

"Magic Box" Food Waste Reduction

For a real bargain, check if your local Morrisons participates in the "Too Good To Go" app. You can buy a "Magic Box" of unsold food from the store at the end of the day for a fraction of its original price. It's a great way to save money and fight food waste.
