Iceland's Simple Secret: The Bonus Card Advantage

Published: October 19, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

Iceland is the king of frozen food, offering convenience and value for busy families. Their loyalty scheme, the Bonus Card, is refreshingly simple and one of the most direct ways to get more for your money. Let's look at how to leverage it with the Iceland offers this week.

How the Bonus Card Gives You Free Money

Forget complicated points. The Iceland Bonus Card works like a savings account that the supermarket tops up for you.

Here's how it works: You can add money to your Bonus Card to budget for your shopping. For every £20 you load onto the card, Iceland adds an extra £1 for free. That's a 5% return on your money before you've even bought anything. It's an excellent tool for saving up for a big monthly shop or for special occasions like Christmas.

Other Bonus Card Perks

  • Exclusive Deals: Cardholders often get access to special prices and promotions not available to other shoppers.
  • Free Delivery: In-store shoppers can get free home delivery on spends over £25 just by swiping their card.

Strategy: Combine with Multi-Buys

The best way to shop at Iceland is to combine the Bonus Card benefit with their famous multi-buy deals. They are well-known for "3 for £5" or "3 for £10" offers on big-name brands. By paying for these already-discounted items with your topped-up Bonus Card, you are effectively layering two types of savings.

It's Not Just Frozen Food

While famous for its freezers, Iceland has a growing range of fresh groceries, pantry staples, and household items. You can often do your entire weekly shop there, collecting Bonus Card rewards on everything you buy.
