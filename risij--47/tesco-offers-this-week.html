Mastering the Aisles: How to Snag the Best Tesco Offers

Published on July 26, 2024 in Loyalty Schemes

Tesco is a staple for many UK households, and its Clubcard scheme has become a game-changer for savvy shoppers. The yellow labels indicating 'Clubcard Prices' can lead to significant savings on your weekly shop. But are you using your Clubcard to its full potential? This guide covers how to make the most of the Tesco offers this week.

The Power of the Clubcard

The concept is simple: scan your Clubcard (or the app) at checkout to get instant discounts on hundreds of products. The difference between the regular price and the Clubcard price can be substantial, often making it one of the most effective loyalty schemes available.

How to Maximize Your Clubcard Savings:

  • Always Scan: This sounds obvious, but it's easy to forget. Make a habit of having your card or app ready at the till. No scan, no savings.
  • Use the App: The Tesco Grocery & Clubcard app not only holds your card digitally but also sends you personalised coupons based on your shopping habits. These can be stacked with regular Clubcard prices for extra savings.
  • Turn Points into Vouchers: For every pound you spend, you earn points. These are converted into vouchers which you can spend in-store or online. Alternatively, you can exchange them with Reward Partners for up to double their value on things like restaurant meals or days out.
  • Look for Bonus Points: Tesco often runs promotions offering bonus points on specific products or categories. Keep an eye out for these to boost your voucher balance faster.

Planning Your Shop

Before you go, it's worth checking the latest deals. On Risij.uk, we compile the highlights of the weekly offers, helping you build a shopping list around the best discounts. This is particularly useful for stocking up on pantry items or grabbing a deal on a more expensive purchase. It's also wise to compare with other stores, such as the Sainsbury's offers this week, which often compete directly with Tesco's promotions.
