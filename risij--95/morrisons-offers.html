The Morrisons Fresh Food Hack & App Guide

Strategy Update: October 19, 2024 | Category: Traditional Grocery

Unlock Value on "Market Street"

Morrisons' "Market Street" with its real bakers, butchers, and fishmongers is what makes it special. These counters are not just about quality; they're about value. Often, buying fresh from the counter is cheaper per kilo than the pre-packaged stuff. To find the best deals, check the Morrisons offers this week on Risij.uk, where we flag the top Market Street promotions.

How to Master Market Street:

  • Look for deals on "The Best" range to get premium quality for less.
  • Ask the staff for advice! They can help you choose the best cuts and reduce waste.
  • Keep an eye out for yellow-sticker deals on fresh items towards the end of the day.

Get Personalized Deals with the My Morrisons App

The "My Morrisons" app is your personal deal finder. Instead of confusing points, it gives you money-off coupons for the things you actually buy. The trick is to open the app and activate your offers before you start your shop. It’s like having a set of coupons tailored just for you, every single week.
