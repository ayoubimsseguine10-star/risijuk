Iceland: Cool Deals for Your Freezer

Updated: November 05, 2025 | Category: Savings Guides

Iceland is a hero for busy families, offering amazing value and convenience with its huge range of frozen food. Stocking your freezer here is a brilliant way to plan ahead and save money. The Iceland offers this week are always packed with deals to help you do just that.

The Famous Multi-Buy Deals

Iceland makes saving money easy with simple, clear multi-buy offers that give you real value.

1. The "3 for £10" Mix & Match

This is a classic Iceland deal that everyone loves. You can mix and match from a huge range of popular branded items like pizzas, chicken, fish, and ready meals. It's the perfect way to stock up on family favourites. We always list what's included in this deal on the Iceland offers page at Risij.uk.

2. The £1 Value Range

Iceland has a massive selection of products for just £1 each. From frozen veggies and chips to ice cream and garlic bread, you can get the basics for many meals at a fantastic price.

Get More with the Iceland Bonus Card

The Iceland Bonus Card is a very simple and clever way to save. It's not a points card, but a savings card. For every £20 you load onto your card, Iceland gives you an extra £1 for free. That’s an instant 5% saving on top of all the other offers in the store. It also unlocks exclusive prices just for cardholders.
