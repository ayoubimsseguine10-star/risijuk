The Iceland Bonus Card program operates on a clear and transparent value-add model. For every £20 of consumer capital allocated to the card, Iceland provides a £1 bonus, representing a guaranteed 5% return on investment. This mechanism serves as an effective budgeting tool for larger or seasonal expenditures. When this guaranteed return is combined with data from the Iceland offers this week, the total potential savings are significantly compounded.
