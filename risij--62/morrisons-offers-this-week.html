Unlocking Value at Morrisons: Offers This Week & My Morrisons

Morrisons stands out with its "Market Street" concept, offering fresh produce, a butchery, a fishmonger, and a bakery in most stores. To get the best value, savvy shoppers combine these fresh deals with the Morrisons offers this week and the My Morrisons loyalty program.

How to Save at Morrisons

Focus your efforts on these key areas for maximum savings:

  • Weekly Offers: Morrisons has a constantly rotating selection of deals, including multi-buys, half-price events, and special promotions on big brands and their own-label products.
  • My Morrisons App: This is your gateway to personalised offers. The app gives you money-off vouchers based on your shopping habits. The more you shop, the more tailored the deals become.
  • Market Street Deals: Keep an eye on the counters for daily specials. You can often find great prices on fresh meat, fish, and deli items, especially towards the end of the day.

Expert Shopping Tips

Make your next Morrisons shop more affordable with these tips:

  1. Check Risij.uk for the Latest Deals: Before you head out, browse our Morrisons offers page to see the top promotions of the week.
  2. Activate Your App Offers: Always check the My Morrisons app before you shop and activate the offers you want to use.
  3. Look for the "Magic Box": Morrisons is part of the "Too Good To Go" app, where you can buy a "Magic Box" of unsold food at a heavily discounted price to prevent food waste.

A Fresh Approach to Savings

Morrisons offers a great balance between the price-competitiveness of stores like ASDA and the focus on quality and provenance you might find at Sainsbury's. By using the loyalty app and planning around the weekly deals, you can enjoy high-quality fresh food without breaking the budget.
