The Perks of Iceland's Bonus Card

Published: October 21, 2024 | In: Loyalty Programs

Getting More with the Iceland Bonus Card

Iceland's Bonus Card stands out as one of the most user-friendly loyalty programs in the UK. The premise is simple: for every £20 you save to your card, Iceland adds an extra £1. It's an effective way to budget for larger shops or special occasions. When you combine this bonus with the already competitive Iceland offers this week, your savings potential grows significantly.

A Simple Bonus Card Strategy:

  • Add funds to your card with each shop to steadily build your bonus.
  • Watch for special promotions and exclusive deals for cardholders.
  • Plan to use your accumulated bonus for big events like Christmas or birthdays.

Access to Exclusive Brands

A key attraction at Iceland is their range of exclusive brand partnerships, including popular names like Greggs, TGI Fridays, and Slimming World. These products allow you to enjoy familiar favourites at home for a fraction of the cost and are often featured in Iceland's generous multi-buy deals.
