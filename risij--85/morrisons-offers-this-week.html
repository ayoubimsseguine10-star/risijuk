A Simple Guide to Morrisons Offers This Week

Published on Jan 17, 2025 | In Saving Guides

Morrisons is unique because of its "Market Street," where you can find fresh food from butchers, bakers, and fishmongers. This, combined with their loyalty card, offers some great ways to save. Here's a simple guide to the Morrisons offers this week.

How to Use the Morrisons More Card

The Morrisons More card is the key to getting the best prices. Here's how it helps you:

  • More Card Exclusives: Just like other supermarkets, you'll see special lower prices on items just for More card holders. Just scan your card or app to get the discount.
  • Earn Points for Vouchers: You can also earn points on certain items and when you buy fuel. When you get enough points, you can turn them into a £5 voucher to spend on your shopping.

Where to Find the Best Deals

  1. Check Out Market Street: The fresh food counters are a great place to find value. They often have multi-buy deals on fresh meat and fish that can be better than the pre-packaged options.
  2. Activate Offers in the App: Make sure to check the Morrisons More app before you shop. They give you personal offers based on what you usually buy, which can save you even more money.
  3. Look for "Too Good To Go" Bags: Many Morrisons stores sell "Magic Bags" on the Too Good To Go app. These are bags of surplus food that are perfectly good to eat, sold at a big discount at the end of the day.
