A Simple Guide to Your Tesco Clubcard

Simple Guide | Updated: October 2024

Your Tesco Clubcard is the most important tool for saving money when you shop there. It's no longer just for points; it's your key to getting the best prices. This guide explains the Tesco offers this week in a simple way.

The Three Ways Your Clubcard Saves You Money

Your Clubcard works in three simple ways:

  • 1. Clubcard Prices: This is the biggest and best perk. Many items in the store have a special, lower price just for people who scan their Clubcard.
  • 2. Points and Vouchers: This is the classic benefit. You collect points as you shop, and Tesco turns them into money-off vouchers to use on future shops.
  • 3. Reward Partners: You can often swap your vouchers for up to double their value with other companies, like restaurants and cinemas. This can be a fantastic way to get more from your points.

Your Easy Tesco Shopping Plan

To get the most out of every trip to Tesco, just follow these steps:

  1. Always Scan Your Clubcard: This is the number one rule. If you don't scan your card or app, you won't get the special Clubcard Prices. Make it a habit!
  2. Check the Deals Before You Go: Have a quick look online to see the best Clubcard deals for the week. A site like the Risij.uk Tesco page can help you write your shopping list.
  3. Use the Tesco App: The free Tesco Grocery & Clubcard app is really useful. It has your Clubcard on it, stores your vouchers, and sometimes gives you extra coupons.

The New Standard for Supermarkets

Tesco's idea of giving the best prices to members has become very popular. Other supermarkets, like Sainsbury's with its Nectar card, are doing the same thing. For smart shoppers, using a loyalty card is now the most important step to saving money.
