It's Not Just Frozen: A Guide to Iceland Offers This Week

Published on July 28, 2024 | Category: Budget Shopping

Iceland built its reputation on frozen food, and while it's still the king in that department, the store has expanded to offer a full range of groceries. The Iceland offers this week provide fantastic value, especially when you know about their famous multi-buys and loyalty scheme.

The Famous Multi-Buy Deals

Iceland is renowned for its aggressive multi-buy promotions, which are often advertised on TV. Deals like "3 for £10" on pizzas, meats, or ready meals are a constant feature. They also have many "£1" deals on everyday essentials. These offers are a simple and effective way to plan family meals on a budget, much like the deals you can find at Farmfoods.

The Iceland Bonus Card

The Iceland Bonus Card is one of the most straightforward and rewarding loyalty schemes available. It works like a savings card with a special perk:

  1. Top it up: You add money to your Bonus Card in-store or online.
  2. Get a bonus: For every £20 you load onto the card, Iceland gives you £1 for free. That's a 5% return, which is better than any savings account!
  3. Spend it: You can then use the card to pay for your shopping.

It's a brilliant way to budget for your shopping while earning a little extra. You also get access to exclusive cardholder competitions and occasional special offers.

Exclusive Brands

Iceland has popular partnerships with brands like TGI Fridays, Greggs, and Slimming World, offering exclusive frozen versions of their products. These often get included in weekly promotions, so keep an eye out.
