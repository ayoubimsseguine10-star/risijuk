How to 'Double Dip' on Savings at Asda This Week

Published: November 04, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

Asda offers a powerful two-pronged approach to saving money: instant price cuts and a cash-back app. By understanding how to use them together, you can significantly reduce your grocery bill. Let's explore how to get the most from the Asda offers this week.

Part 1: The Foundation - Rollbacks

Asda's "Rollbacks" are their well-known, straightforward price reductions. You'll see these clearly marked in-store on thousands of products, from big brands to everyday essentials. These form the first layer of your savings.

Part 2: The Accelerator - Asda Rewards

The Asda Rewards app is the key to maximising your savings. It's a digital wallet where you earn real money (a "cashpot") to spend on future shops. There are two primary ways to build your cashpot:

  • Star Products: Look for items in the app marked as "Star Products." Buying these gives you a percentage of their value back into your cashpot.
  • Missions: The app offers challenges, like "spend £5 on fruit and veg," that reward you with a cash bonus when completed.

The 'Double Dip' Strategy

The secret to expert-level saving at Asda is to find products that are both on Rollback AND are Star Products in the app. This means you get an instant discount at the till and you earn money back for later. It's the ultimate win-win.

Plan Your Shop for Success

Before heading to the store, open the Asda Rewards app and check the latest deals on a site like Risij.uk. Build your shopping list around items that offer a "double dip." This simple planning step is the difference between casual saving and strategic, significant reductions in your spending.
