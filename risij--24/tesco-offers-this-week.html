Unlocking Tesco's Clubcard Power: This Week's Guide

Published on July 26, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

Your Tesco Clubcard is one of the best ways to save money on your groceries, but many people don't use it to its full potential. This guide will help you make the most of the Tesco offers this week and turn your Clubcard into a real money-saver.

Clubcard Prices: The Key to Instant Savings

The most important thing to remember is to always scan your Clubcard. This unlocks "Clubcard Prices," which are special discounts available only to members. Not scanning your card is like leaving free money at the checkout.

Finding All The Best Deals

On top of Clubcard Prices, Tesco has other deals like multi-buys and clearance items. Keeping track of them all can be tricky. That's why at Risij.uk, we list all the current Tesco offers in one place, so you can plan your shop with confidence.

A Simple Checklist for Your Tesco Shop:

  • Check Risij.uk Before You Go: A quick look at our list of Clubcard Prices helps you build a smart shopping list and plan your meals around the best deals.
  • Look for Yellow Stickers: "Reduced to Clear" items with yellow stickers offer huge discounts. The best time to find these is usually in the evening.
  • Boost Your Vouchers: You can often double or triple the value of your Clubcard vouchers by swapping them with Tesco's "Reward Partners" for things like meals out or day trips.
  • Know the Competition: Having a quick look at what other stores, like the Aldi offers next week, have on sale ensures you're always making the smartest choice.
