A Simple Guide to Farmfoods Offers This Week

Published on Jan 14, 2025 | In Saving Guides

Farmfoods is a fantastic place to stock up on frozen food and save money. If you want to get the very best value, it helps to know how their deals work. Here's our simple guide to the Farmfoods offers this week.

How Farmfoods Helps You Save

Farmfoods is all about offering great prices on big brands, especially when you buy more than one item. Their best deals are usually multi-buys, which are perfect for filling up the freezer.

Three Simple Steps to Save at Farmfoods

  1. Get Their Vouchers: This is the most important tip! Go to the Farmfoods website and sign up for their emails. They'll send you vouchers for money off your shop, like £5 off when you spend £60. It's an easy way to save.
  2. Check the Weekly Leaflet: The leaflet shows you all the best multi-buy deals, like "3 for £10." You can plan what you want to buy before you even go to the store. We list these deals on Risij.uk too.
  3. Think About Stocking Up: To get the best value at Farmfoods, it's a good idea to buy in bulk. Think about items your family eats a lot of, like frozen chicken, chips, or vegetables, and buy them when they're on a great multi-buy offer.

By using a voucher from their website on top of the in-store deals, you can make some really big savings on your family's food budget.
