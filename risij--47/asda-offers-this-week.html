ASDA Rollbacks: More Than Just Low Prices

Published on July 25, 2024 in Store Guides

ASDA's "Pocket Tap" slogan is backed by its famous Rollback events, where prices on thousands of products are lowered for a limited time. These aren't just minor discounts; Rollbacks can offer significant savings on everyday essentials and big-ticket items alike. Understanding how they work is key to getting the most out of the Asda offers this week.

What Exactly is a Rollback?

A Rollback is a price reduction on a specific product that lasts for a set period. You can find them across the entire store, from fresh produce and pantry staples to electronics and clothing from their George line. They are easily identifiable by the prominent red and yellow "Rollback" signs on the shelves.

Strategies for Shopping the Rollbacks:

  • Check Online First: The ASDA website has a dedicated Rollback section. You can browse all the current deals before you even leave the house, allowing you to create a targeted shopping list.
  • Stock Up on Staples: Rollbacks are the perfect opportunity to stock up on non-perishable items you use regularly, like canned goods, pasta, cleaning supplies, and toiletries.
  • Don't Forget George at ASDA: The Rollback events often include fantastic deals on clothing, school uniforms, and homeware from the George at ASDA brand, offering excellent quality at even lower prices.
  • Use the ASDA Rewards App: While not a traditional loyalty card, the ASDA Rewards app allows you to earn "pounds" in a "Cashpot" by buying specific "Star Products" or completing "Missions". This can be used to get money off your shopping later.

Beyond Rollbacks

ASDA is also highly competitive on its base prices, frequently comparing them to other supermarkets. It's always a good idea to cross-reference deals with what's available elsewhere. For example, check the Morrisons offers this week to see who has the better price on fresh meat and produce.
