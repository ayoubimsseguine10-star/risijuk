Co-op Membership: Your Hack for Smarter Local Shopping

Updated: July 2024 | Category: Loyalty Schemes

Your local Co-op is more than just a convenience store; with a membership, it's a savings hub. The loyalty program is designed to give back to you and your community, giving you a real edge on the Co-op offers this week.

Unlocking Your Member Perks

Being a Co-op member is about more than just a plastic card. It's about activating a set of powerful perks that make your top-up shops cheaper and more rewarding.

Your Key Advantages:

  • Member-Only Prices: Look for the blue "Member Price" tags in-store. These are instant, exclusive discounts on everyday items. This is your number one reason to scan your card every time.
  • Pick Your Own Deals: The Co-op app is your friend. Every week, it lets you choose two personalized offers on things you actually buy. It's a simple way to guarantee savings.
