A Simple Guide to Shopping at Lidl

Simple Guide | Updated: October 2024

A trip to Lidl is always an adventure, with great prices on groceries and surprising finds in the middle aisle. To get the best value from the Lidl offers this week, it helps to know what to look for.

Where to Find the Best Deals at Lidl

There are three main places to find amazing value in every Lidl store:

  • The "Middle of Lidl": This is what Lidl is famous for! The middle aisles are full of brilliant non-food items that change every week, from DIY tools to kids' toys.
  • Themed Food Weeks: Most weeks, Lidl focuses on food from a different country, like Italy or Spain. It's a great chance to try new things at a low price.
  • The Lidl Plus App: This free app is your key to extra savings. It gives you special coupons for money off your shopping.

Your Easy Lidl Shopping Plan

Follow these three simple steps for a successful trip:

  1. Check the App and Website First: Before you go, have a quick look at the Lidl app or website to see what's in the Middle of Lidl that week and which coupons are available. The Risij.uk Lidl page also has a great summary.
  2. Activate Your Coupons: This is very important! You need to open the Lidl Plus app and tap to 'activate' your coupons before you get to the checkout. If you don't, they won't work.
  3. Be Quick for Popular Items: If you see a really popular item advertised for the Middle of Lidl, try to go early in the week. The best stuff sells out quickly!

A Fun Way to Shop
