A Guide to Morrisons Offers This Week

Published on January 3, 2025 | Category: Shopping Guides

Morrisons is unique among major supermarkets because of its "Market Street" fresh food counters. A smart way to shop the Morrisons offers this week is to combine deals from the main aisles with the great value you can find from the in-store butchers, fishmongers, and bakers.

How to Use the 'My Morrisons' App

The 'My Morrisons' loyalty app is very straightforward. Instead of making you collect points, it gives you personalised money-off deals directly in the app. It's a simple way to get discounts on the things you already buy.

'My Morrisons' Tips:

  • Activate Offers Before You Shop: Get into the habit of checking the app and activating your deals before you head to the checkout.
  • Join In-App Clubs: If you often buy specific things, like baby products, you can join clubs in the app to get extra, relevant offers.
  • Scan Your Card Every Time: To get your discounts, you have to scan your digital card at the till.

Finding Value on Market Street

While Morrisons has good deals on regular groceries, Market Street is where you can often find the best quality and prices for fresh food.

  • Ask at the Counters: The staff at the meat and fish counters are very helpful and can tell you about any daily specials or deals.
  • Check for Bakery Reductions: Towards the end of the day, you can often find freshly baked bread and other items at a reduced price.
  • Freshly Made Meals: The salad bar and fresh pizza counter are great, affordable options for a quick lunch or dinner.
