The Playbook: The Morrisons More Card Exploit

Published: November 05, 2025 | Category: Tactical Guides

The Morrisons More scheme is a multi-layered system designed for the savvy shopper to exploit. It offers three distinct channels for value extraction: instant price cuts, points accumulation, and app-based bonuses. Here’s how you leverage all three on the Morrisons offers this week.

Channel 1: More Card Exclusives (The Instant Win)

This is your baseline. Like a Tesco Clubcard, the More Card unlocks immediate lower prices on hundreds of items. Look for the "More Card Exclusives" signs. If you're not scanning your card on every single purchase, you're failing at step one.

Channel 2: The App (The Accelerator)

The physical card is for amateurs. The app is where you accelerate your gains. Before each shop, you must complete one critical action: open the app and activate all your personalised bonus point offers. This is the fastest way to stack points and unlock your £5 vouchers.

Channel 3: Fuel Points (The Long Game)

Don't forget the pump. Earning points on fuel is a slow burn, but it contributes directly to your next £5 voucher. It's a passive way to add value that most people ignore.
