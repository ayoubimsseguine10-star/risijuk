Your Guide to the Best Lidl Offers This Week

Published on July 21, 2024 in Supermarket Guides

Lidl has built a reputation for high quality at low prices, and savvy shoppers know how to make the most of it. To truly maximize your savings, you need to be strategic and keep an eye on the ever-changing Lidl offers this week. This guide will turn you into a Lidl pro.

The Magic of the "Middle of Lidl"

The legendary Middle of Lidl is an aisle of non-food treasures that changes twice a week, on Thursdays and Sundays. It’s where you'll find everything from power tools to paddleboards. The secret is to know what's coming and get there early, as the best items sell out in hours.

Unlocking Extra Savings with the Lidl Plus App

If you shop at Lidl, the Lidl Plus app is non-negotiable. It's packed with features to help you save:

  • Weekly Coupons: Every week, you get a new set of coupons for discounts on specific products. Activate them in the app before you scan your card.
  • Spend & Save: The more you spend in a month, the more coupons you unlock.
  • Digital Receipts: Keep track of your spending and make returns easier with digital receipts stored in the app.
  • Partner Offers: Get exclusive discounts from other companies, like cinemas and travel firms.

Don't Miss the Weekly Food Highlights

Alongside the Middle of Lidl, the supermarket features weekly food themes, such as Italian Week or French Week, offering specialities you won't find year-round. Also, look out for their "Pick of the Week" deals on fruit, vegetables, meat, and fish for some of the best fresh food bargains available.

The Lidl Bakery

Arguably one of Lidl's best features is its in-store bakery. Offering fresh bread, croissants, and pastries at incredibly low prices, it's the perfect way to start (or end) your shopping trip. Pro tip: look for the discount trolley in the evenings for even bigger reductions on bakery items.
