A Guide to Aldi's Specialbuys

Published on Jan 10, 2025 | In Savings Guides

The famous "middle aisle" at Aldi can be a lot of fun, filled with all sorts of unexpected treasures! To make sure you find the best bits, it helps to have a little plan. Here's our friendly guide to the Aldi offers for next week.

When to Find New Things

Aldi brings out its new Specialbuys on two days every week: Thursday and Sunday. These items are very popular and don't get restocked, so once they're gone, they're really gone! Knowing this is the secret to a successful trip.

Three Simple Tips for Your Visit

  1. Have a Little Peek Online First: Before you go, it's a great idea to look at the Aldi leaflet online. You can find it on their website or see a summary on our main Risij.uk site. This way, you can see if there's anything you're really excited about.
  2. An Early Trip Can Help: If you've seen something you really want, popping in on a Thursday or Sunday morning is your best bet to find it before it sells out.
  3. Don't Forget the Super 6! While you're there, be sure to check out the "Super 6" fruit and vegetable deals. They're a brilliant way to stock up on healthy essentials for less.

With these little tips, your Aldi shops can be fun, successful, and great for your budget!
