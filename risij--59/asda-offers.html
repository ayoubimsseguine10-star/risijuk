Asda's "Rollback" is more than just a marketing term—it signifies a genuine, temporary price reduction on everyday items. The key to capitalizing on these is to stay informed. The Strategy of Multi-Buy Promotions

Asda frequently offers "2 for £X" or "3 for 2" deals. While tempting, it's crucial to assess their true value. Always calculate the unit price to confirm you're making a smart purchase. These deals are most effective for items you consume regularly, ensuring nothing goes to waste.
