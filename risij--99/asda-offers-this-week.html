A Guide to Asda Rewards & Rollbacks

Published on Jan 11, 2025 | In Savings Guides

Shopping at Asda offers a couple of great ways to save: their famous "Rollbacks" and the handy Asda Rewards app. Here’s a simple guide to help you make the most of the Asda offers this week.

Two Great Ways to Save

  • Rollbacks: These are simply temporary price drops on everyday items. You can spot them by their green signs around the store. They're a great chance to grab your favorites for a little bit less!
  • Asda Rewards: This is a free app where you can build up a "Cashpot." You earn money in your pot by buying special "Star Products" or completing little "Missions" in the app. It's a fun way to save up for a future shop.

Our Friendly Checklist for Your Shop

  1. Check the App First: Before you head out, open the Asda Rewards app to see what the "Star Products" are for the week. It's always worth a quick look!
  2. Keep an Eye Out for Rollbacks: As you wander the aisles, look for those green signs. It's always a nice surprise to find something on your list has been reduced in price.
  3. Let Your Cashpot Grow: It can be tempting to use your Cashpot straight away, but it's really satisfying to let it build up and then use it to get a nice discount on a bigger shop.

By keeping these simple things in mind, you can make your Asda shopping experience even more rewarding.
