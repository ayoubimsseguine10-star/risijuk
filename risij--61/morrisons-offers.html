Fresh Deals and More: A Guide to Shopping at Morrisons

Published on October 19, 2024 in Shopping Guides

Morrisons stands out with its "Market Street," featuring specialist butchers, bakers, and fishmongers in-store, offering a traditional shopping experience with supermarket convenience. Combining this with their loyalty scheme is the key to unlocking great value on fresh food and groceries. Here's how to shop the Morrisons offers this week.

Get More with the Morrisons More Card

The Morrisons More card is a points-based loyalty scheme. You earn points on selected products and fuel, and once you reach 5,000 points, you get a £5 voucher. The app also features exclusive offers and discounts tailored to you.

Top Tips for a Morrisons Shop

  • Visit Market Street: Don't just stick to the aisles. The fresh meat, fish, and baked goods from the Market Street counters are often better value and higher quality.
  • Use the More App: Activate your personalized offers in the app before you shop to earn extra points and get money off your favourite items.
  • Look for "More Card Exclusives": Many of the best deals are reserved for More card holders. Look for the special signs to identify these offers.
  • Check the Seasonal Aisles: Morrisons does a great job with seasonal events, offering a wide range of products for occasions like Christmas, Easter, and Halloween at competitive prices.

Conclusion

Morrisons offers a unique blend of fresh quality and supermarket value. By making the most of the More card and exploring the Market Street counters, you can enjoy fantastic food at prices that won't break the bank.
