Waitrose's Quality Savings: The myWaitrose Guide

Category: Loyalty Schemes | Published: July 2024

Waitrose is synonymous with high-quality food and excellent service. While it has a reputation for being a premium supermarket, savvy shoppers can find fantastic value by leveraging the myWaitrose loyalty program and keeping a close eye on the Waitrose offers this week.

Making myWaitrose Work for You

The myWaitrose card is less about points and more about immediate, personalized value. It's designed to reward loyal customers with perks and tailored discounts.

Key Features of the Program:

  • Personalised Vouchers: The core of the program. Every week, myWaitrose members receive a selection of digital vouchers for money off specific items, based on their shopping history. Choosing these wisely is key to saving.
  • Fish Friday: A long-standing tradition where myWaitrose members get 20% off fresh fish from the counter every Friday.
  • Freebies & Perks: The program used to be famous for its free coffee. While that has changed, members still get access to a free newspaper with a minimum spend and other exclusive partner offers.
  • Waitrose Food Magazine: Members can pick up the highly-regarded Waitrose Food magazine for free in-store.

Finding Value in Quality

Beyond the loyalty scheme, Waitrose offers other avenues for savings. Their 'Essential Waitrose' range provides everyday items at competitive prices without compromising on the store's quality standards. They also run frequent multi-buy offers, especially on wine and fresh goods. It's always a good idea to compare their premium offers with those from other quality-focused retailers, like the Sainsbury's offer this week, to ensure you're getting the best deal.
