A Simple Guide to Iceland's Bonus Card

Published on January 2, 2025 | Category: Loyalty Programs

While Iceland is famous for frozen food, their best savings tool is actually their loyalty scheme: the Bonus Card. Understanding how it works is the key to getting the most value from the Iceland offers this week.

How the Bonus Card Gives You 5% Back

The Iceland Bonus Card is different from other loyalty cards. Instead of collecting points, you get an immediate saving. Here’s how it works:

  • Top Up Your Card: Before you shop, you add money to your Bonus Card.
  • Get an Instant Bonus: For every £20 you add, Iceland gives you an extra £1 for free.
  • This Means a 5% Saving: This simple process gives you a guaranteed 5% discount on your shopping, which you can spend on anything in the store.

Combine Your Bonus with Weekly Offers

The best part is that you can use your Bonus Card savings on top of Iceland's other promotions.

  • Look for Multi-buy Deals: Iceland is famous for its "3 for £10" offers on popular branded items like pizzas and ready meals.
  • Find Exclusive Brands: You can only find grocery ranges from brands like Greggs and Slimming World at Iceland.
  • Get Free Home Delivery: If you spend over a certain amount in-store (usually £25), you can get your shopping delivered to your home for free the same day.
