Farmfoods: Your Guide to Frozen Value Finds

Category: Store Guides | Published: July 2024

Farmfoods has carved out a niche as the go-to destination for frozen food bargains and brand-name pantry staples. It's a paradise for those who like to batch cook, meal prep, or simply keep a well-stocked freezer. Shopping smart at Farmfoods means focusing on their multi-buy deals and special promotions listed in the Farmfoods offers this week.

The Farmfoods Shopping Strategy

Farmfoods isn't a full-service supermarket, and that's its strength. It focuses on a core range and executes it with incredible value. Here's how to make the most of it.

Tips for the Savvy Shopper:

  • Leaflet is Law: Farmfoods is a leaflet-driven retailer. Pick one up in-store or view it on our main site before you shop. The best deals are always advertised there.
  • Think in Multiples: The store's pricing structure heavily favours multi-buy offers, such as "3 for £5" or "2 for £3". Factor this in when planning your shop to maximize savings. It's often cheaper to buy three items than two.
  • Brand-Name Bargains: Unlike discounters who focus on own-brands, Farmfoods is a great place to find big brands like Birds Eye, McCain, and Young's at low prices.
  • Sign Up for Vouchers: Check the Farmfoods website to sign up for their mailing list. They frequently send out money-off vouchers (e.g., £2 off a £25 spend) that can be printed and used in-store.

Complementing Your Shop

Farmfoods is an excellent primary stop for your frozen and pantry needs. You can easily round out your shop with fresh produce from another store. For example, after stocking up at Farmfoods, you could check the Lidl offers this week for their excellent value fruit and vegetables.
