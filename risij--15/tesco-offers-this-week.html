Tesco Clubcard Secrets: How to Maximize Your Savings

Published on July 29, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Programs

The Tesco Clubcard has transformed from a simple points card into a powerful savings tool. With the introduction of "Clubcard Prices," having a Clubcard is no longer optional—it's essential for anyone looking to save money at Tesco. Let's explore how to get the most out of the card and the Tesco offers this week.

Clubcard Prices: The Game Changer

The biggest benefit of the Clubcard is access to Clubcard Prices. These are instant discounts on hundreds of products across the store. The difference between the regular price and the Clubcard price can be substantial, often making Tesco the cheapest option for those specific items.

Tips for a Smarter Tesco Shop:

  1. Always Scan Your Card: This sounds obvious, but without a scan, you'll pay the full price. Use the app on your phone so you never forget your card.
  2. Plan Around Clubcard Prices: Before you shop, check the Tesco offers page on Risij.uk to see the current Clubcard Prices and plan your meals and shopping list accordingly.
  3. Turn Points into Vouchers: Every 150 points you collect is worth £1.50 in vouchers. These are sent out quarterly, but you can request them instantly via the app.
  4. Use Reward Partners: The best way to use your vouchers is with Tesco's Reward Partners. You can often exchange your vouchers for two or three times their value on things like restaurant meals, cinema tickets, and days out.

Is Clubcard the Best Loyalty Scheme?

With its instant discounts, the Tesco Clubcard is arguably one of the most powerful loyalty schemes, rivaling the Sainsbury's Nectar card. If you're a regular Tesco shopper, making full use of Clubcard Prices and Reward Partners is the fastest way to a cheaper grocery bill.
