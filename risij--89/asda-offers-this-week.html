Your Guide to ASDA's Offers & Rewards This Week

ASDA is famous for its great value, but are you using all the tools available to save? By combining their Rollbacks with the ASDA Rewards app, you can get even more from the ASDA offers this week. Here’s how to do it.

ASDA's Two Main Ways to Save

Understanding ASDA's savings system is simple. It's built on two key features:

  • Rollbacks: These are long-term price drops on everyday essentials. You can spot them easily by the green "Rollback" signs in-store, and they offer consistent savings on products you buy regularly.
  • ASDA Rewards App: This is the best way to earn money back. Use the app to buy special "Star Products" or complete "Missions" to build a "cashpot" that you can spend in-store.

Simple Tips for a Cheaper ASDA Shop

Use these strategies to get the most out of every trip:

  1. Plan Your Shop: Your first step should be to visit our ASDA offers page on Risij.uk. We highlight the best Rollbacks and Star Products so you can walk in with a clear plan.
  2. Scan the App Every Time: Make it a habit to scan your ASDA Rewards app at the checkout. It's the only way to earn cashpot rewards on your purchases.
  3. Look for Yellow Stickers: ASDA is great for "reduced to clear" items. Check the fresh and chilled sections in the afternoon or evening to find products with yellow stickers for the biggest bargains.
