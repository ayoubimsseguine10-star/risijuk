How to Get the Most from Your Tesco Clubcard This Week

Published on July 29, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Programs

The Tesco Clubcard is one of the most popular loyalty schemes in the UK, but are you using it to its full potential? The Tesco offers this week are great, but with a few simple tricks, your Clubcard can save you even more. Here’s how.

Think Beyond the Basic Scan

Simply scanning your card at checkout is just the first step. The real power of the Clubcard comes from combining its features. The "Clubcard Prices" are special discounts just for members, and they often provide the biggest savings in the store.

Where to Find the Best Clubcard Deals

Look for the yellow and blue Clubcard Price tags. You'll find the best value on:

  • Household Staples: Big savings on items you buy regularly, like coffee, pasta, and laundry detergent, really add up over time.
  • Tesco Finest Range: Clubcard Prices often make premium items much more affordable, so it's a great time to treat yourself.
  • Frozen Foods & Drinks: Multi-buy offers are common in these aisles, which is perfect for stocking up the freezer or preparing for a gathering.

Simple Tips to Maximize Your Savings

Here are a few easy ways to make your Clubcard work harder:

  1. Use the Tesco App: The Tesco Grocery & Clubcard App sends you personalized coupons based on what you usually buy. You can use these on top of the normal Clubcard Prices.
  2. Look for "Yellow Sticker" Deals: Tesco reduces prices on items nearing their expiry date. These discounts can be combined with your Clubcard, offering huge savings, especially in the evening.
  3. Use Reward Partners: Don't just spend your points in-store. You can often double or triple their value by exchanging them for vouchers with Reward Partners for things like restaurant meals or days out.
