How to Win at Weekly Shopping with the Lidl Plus App

Category: Shopping Guides | Published: October 2024

Lidl has earned a reputation for exceptional value, but the savviest shoppers know that the real key to unlocking its full potential is the Lidl Plus app. This digital companion transforms a standard shopping trip into a strategic money-saving exercise.

Your Digital Key to Lidl Savings

The Lidl Plus app is a free tool that provides a host of weekly benefits. The most important feature is the set of personalized coupons that refresh every Thursday. These offer direct discounts—like 15% off the bakery or £5 off a £40 spend—that you won't get without the app.

A Shopper's Checklist for Lidl

  • Activate Coupons Before You Shop: Open the app before you enter the store and tap 'Activate' on all the coupons you might use. They won't apply automatically unless you do.
  • Scan Your Digital Card: At the checkout, simply scan the QR code from your app to apply all your activated coupons instantly.
  • Explore the "Middle of Lidl": Just like Aldi, Lidl has a middle aisle with rotating non-food bargains. Check the Lidl offers this week online to see what themed deals are coming up.
  • Don't Forget the Bakery: Lidl's in-store bakery is a highlight, offering artisan-style bread and pastries at a fraction of the usual cost. It's a must-visit on every trip.

See this week's app coupons and Middle of Lidl themes by checking the Lidl offers for this week on our main site.

By integrating the Lidl Plus app into your routine, you can consistently save more on your groceries. It’s an essential tool for anyone serious about getting the best possible value from their Lidl shop.
