Making Sense of Asda's Deals

Updated: November 05, 2024 | Category: Shopping Guides

Asda has a few different ways to help you save, from their famous Rollbacks to special multi-buy deals. To get the most out of the Asda offers this week, it helps to know what to look for. Here's our simple breakdown for 2025.

The Main Ways to Save at Asda

Understanding the difference between Asda's deals is the key to a smarter shop.

1. Rollbacks: Longer-Lasting Low Prices

A "Rollback" isn't just a quick sale; it's when Asda lowers the price on everyday items for a longer period. This makes it a great, reliable way to save on your regular buys. We keep an eye on the best Rollbacks on our Asda offers page at Risij.uk for you.

2. Multi-buy Deals: Great for Stocking Up

Offers like "2 for £5" are perfect for buying things you use all the time. It's always a good idea to quickly check the price of a single item to see just how much you're saving.

Our Extra Money-Saving Tips

  • Look for Yellow Stickers: Items that are close to their use-by date get a yellow "reduced" sticker, often later in the day. This is a fantastic way to find a bargain!
  • Use the Asda Rewards App: This app is a game-changer. You can earn money in your "cashpot" by buying "Star Products" or completing little missions. It's a must-have for regular Asda shoppers.
  • Check the "Just Essentials" Range: Before you buy a branded item on offer, compare its price to the "Just Essentials" version. This budget-friendly range often offers the best value.
