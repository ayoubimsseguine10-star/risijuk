How to Make Your Tesco Clubcard Work for You

The Tesco Clubcard is one of the most popular loyalty cards in the UK, and for good reason. It’s a simple way to save money. By understanding how to use it with the Tesco offers this week, you can significantly lower your grocery bills.

The Three Parts of Clubcard Savings

Tesco's savings program is built on three simple features:

  • Clubcard Prices: This is the easiest way to save. Hundreds of items have a special, lower price only for Clubcard members. These instant discounts are the biggest benefit.
  • Points and Vouchers: You earn points on almost everything you buy. These points are then turned into money-off vouchers that you can spend on your future shopping.
  • Reward Partners: This is a smart trick to get more value. You can swap your vouchers with Reward Partners for up to double their value on things like family days out or magazine subscriptions.

Your Simple Plan for a Cheaper Tesco Shop

Follow these rules to get the most value from every trip:

  1. Always Scan Your Card: This is the most important rule. If you forget to scan your Clubcard or the app, you will pay a higher price for many items.
  2. Check Our Tesco Guide: We find the best Clubcard Prices each week and list them for you. A quick look at our Tesco page helps you plan your list.
  3. Use the App for Extra Points: The Clubcard app often has special coupons for bonus points on certain products. Check it before you shop to earn vouchers faster.
