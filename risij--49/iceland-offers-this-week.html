Iceland's Bonus Card Benefits and Weekly Deals

Published on: October 19, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Programs

Iceland is a leader in frozen food, but its value extends beyond the freezer aisles. The key to unlocking maximum savings is the Iceland Bonus Card. Let's break down the benefits and the best Iceland offers this week.

How the Iceland Bonus Card Works

The Bonus Card is a savings card, not a points card. You can top it up with money to spend in-store, and for every £20 you add, Iceland gives you an extra £1. This is effectively a 5% bonus on your shopping before you even start buying things. It's a simple, guaranteed way to save.

Tip 1: Use it for Budgeting

The Bonus Card is an excellent budgeting tool. Decide on your weekly or monthly grocery budget, load it onto the card, and get an immediate bonus. This helps you stick to your spending limit while getting a little extra for free.

Tip 2: Look for the Famous "£1" Deals

Iceland is famous for its £1 deals on a huge range of products, from frozen vegetables to brand-name ice creams. These, combined with their extensive 3 for £5 or 3 for £10 multi-buy offers, make it a top destination for family shoppers. These straightforward deals are often easier to manage than the points systems at other stores, like Sainsbury's.

Free Home Delivery

Another major perk is Iceland's free home delivery service for in-store shops over a certain amount (usually £25). This is a great convenience, especially when you're stocking up on heavy, bulky frozen items. It's a service that sets them apart from discounters like Farmfoods.
