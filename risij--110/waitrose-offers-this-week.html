A Simple Guide to Saving at Waitrose

Simple Guide | Updated: October 2024

Waitrose is known for its high-quality food, and while it can be a bit pricier, there are still smart ways to save. The key is to make the most of their loyalty scheme. This guide explains how to find value in the Waitrose offers this week.

Three Easy Ways to Save at Waitrose

A smart Waitrose shop focuses on three simple things:

  • The myWaitrose Card: This is a free loyalty card that's essential for saving. It unlocks special member-only prices on lots of items throughout the store.
  • Personalised Vouchers: This is the best tool. Every week, you can go online and choose from a list of digital vouchers that give you money off things you actually buy.
  • Smart Product Choices: For everyday items, the 'Essential Waitrose' range is great value. And keep an eye out for special offers on their premium 'No.1' range.

Your Easy Waitrose Shopping Plan

To get great quality food at a better price, just get into these habits:

  1. Choose Your Vouchers Online (Very Important!): Before every shop, you must log in to your myWaitrose account online or in the app and select the vouchers you want to use. They don't apply unless you choose them first!
  2. Look for "Yellow Sticker" Deals: Waitrose often has great reductions on items that are near their sell-by date. Look for the yellow stickers, usually later in the day, to find some amazing bargains.
  3. Check for the "Dine In" Offer: Waitrose often has a "Dine In" deal, which includes a high-quality main course, side, dessert, and often a drink for a set price. It's one of the best value deals of any supermarket.

To get a look at the best weekly deals before you go, you can check the summary on the Risij.uk Waitrose page.

A Different Way to Save

Saving at Waitrose is more about being selective than at a discount store like Lidl. It rewards a little bit of planning. If you value high-quality ingredients, using the myWaitrose card is the perfect way to balance quality and cost.
