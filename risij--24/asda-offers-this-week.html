A Simple Guide to Asda's Rollbacks & Rewards

Published on July 29, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

Shopping at Asda is all about finding consistent value. The key is to understand their two main saving tools: the famous "Rollbacks" and the powerful Asda Rewards app. This guide will show you how to use both to get the most from the Asda offers this week.

What Are Asda Rollbacks?

Unlike a typical weekly sale, a Rollback is a longer-term price cut on everyday items. Asda "rolls back" the price on hundreds of products, keeping them low for weeks or even months. This makes it easier to plan your budget and stock up on essentials.

How to Master Asda Rewards

The Asda Rewards app is a must-have. It works on a simple "pounds, not points" system. You build up a cash pot by buying "Star Products" and completing "Missions" in the app, which you can then spend in-store. It's one of the most straightforward loyalty schemes out there.

Top Tips for Your Next Asda Shop:

  • Check Risij.uk for Current Offers: Before you shop, visit Risij.uk to see a clear list of the best Asda offers this week, including top Rollbacks.
  • Always Scan Your Rewards App: Make it a habit. You'll be surprised how quickly your cash pot grows just from your regular shopping.
  • Look for "Star Products": These are highlighted in the app and in-store. Buying them gives you an instant boost to your cash pot.
  • Keep an Eye on Missions: The app will offer you personalized missions, like "buy 3 pieces of fruit," that add extra pounds to your pot when completed.
