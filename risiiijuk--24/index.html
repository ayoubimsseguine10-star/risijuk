Tesco Clubcard: Your Secret Weapon

16 Oct 2023 | Shopping Tips

Every shopper in the UK needs a Tesco Clubcard. Full stop. It's a powerhouse loyalty scheme that turns the Tesco offers this week from merely good to unmissable.

How Clubcard Prices Work

It's dead simple. You'll see two prices on loads of items: the normal one and a cheaper Clubcard Price. Scan your Clubcard at the checkout, and you get the lower price. The savings are often massive, sometimes up to 50% off.

It's More Than Just Discounts

You also earn points as you shop. These points turn into vouchers you can spend in-store or, even better, swap with Reward Partners for up to double their value on meals out, cinema tickets, and more. It's a game-changer.

Ready to unlock huge savings? Check out the latest Tesco offers this week on Risij.uk.
