A Simple Guide to Iceland Offers This Week

Published on Jan 15, 2025 | In Saving Guides

Iceland is a great place to find value, especially with its famous multi-buy deals. But there's a simple trick to save even more: the Iceland Bonus Card. This guide will explain how to make the most of the Iceland offers this week.

Your Secret Weapon: The Bonus Card

The Iceland Bonus Card is a brilliant way to get extra value. It's not a points card, it's a savings card. Here's how it works: for every £20 you load onto your card, Iceland gives you an extra £1 for free. That's like getting 5% off your shopping before you even start! It's one of the best and simplest loyalty schemes around.

How to Find the Best Deals

Once you've got your Bonus Card, look out for these classic Iceland offers:

  • The Famous Multi-buys: Iceland is known for its "3 for £10" or "3 for £5" deals on things like pizzas, ready meals, and frozen meat. These are a great way to stock up.
  • The £1 Aisles: Don't forget to check out the sections where everything is just £1. It's perfect for grabbing basics like frozen veg and chips.

The best way to shop is to load money onto your Bonus Card to get your free £1, and then use that money to buy items that are already on a great multi-buy deal. We list all the current Iceland offers on Risij.uk to make it easy to plan.
