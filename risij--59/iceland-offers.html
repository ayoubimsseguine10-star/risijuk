Iceland's Bonus Card Benefits

Posted on: October 21, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Programs

Unlocking Extra Savings with the Bonus Card

The Iceland Bonus Card is one of the most straightforward and rewarding loyalty schemes available. For every £20 you save on the card, Iceland gives you £1 back. It's a simple way to build up a fund for a big shop or to treat yourself. Combine this with the fantastic Iceland offers this week, and the savings quickly multiply.

Bonus Card Strategy:

  • Load money onto your card regularly to build up your bonus.
  • Look for cardholder-exclusive deals in-store and online.
  • Use your bonus to pay for your Christmas shop or a special occasion.

Exclusive Brands and Partnerships

Iceland is renowned for its exclusive partnerships with brands like TGI Fridays, Greggs, and Slimming World, allowing you to enjoy restaurant-quality food at home for less. These ranges are often included in their famous "3 for £X" deals, offering even greater value.
