Finding Value in Co-op's Offers This Week

Published on July 26, 2024 | Category: Shopping Tips

Often seen as just a convenience store, your local Co-op can be a goldmine for great deals if you know what to look for. This guide will help you navigate the Co-op offers this week and get the most out of every shop.

The Power of a Co-op Membership

The single best way to save at Co-op is by becoming a member. For just £1, you unlock a range of benefits:

  • Member Prices: Look for the blue shelf tickets for exclusive discounts on dozens of everyday items. These savings can be substantial.
  • Personalised Offers: In the Co-op app, you can choose two new offers each week tailored to your shopping habits.
  • Community Support: 2p for every £1 you spend on Co-op own-brand products goes to your chosen local cause.

Other Ways to Save

Even without a membership, you can find great value:

  1. Meal Deals: Co-op often has excellent meal deals, such as their "Freezer Fillers" or pizza deals, which are perfect for an easy dinner.
  2. Reduced to Clear: Like all supermarkets, Co-op reduces items nearing their expiry date. The time varies by store, but late afternoon is often a good time to look.
  3. Focus on Own-Brand: The Co-op's own-brand range, especially the "Irresistible" line, offers excellent quality and is often part of their weekly promotions.
