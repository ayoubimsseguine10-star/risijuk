Waitrose: Quality on a Budget with myWaitrose

Strategy Update: October 15, 2024 | Category: Premium Supermarkets

Your Tool for Affordable Quality: myWaitrose

Shopping at Waitrose doesn't have to break the bank. The myWaitrose loyalty program is your secret weapon for getting that famous quality for less. It gives you personalized money-off vouchers and member-only prices. Our analysis of the Waitrose offers this week on Risij.uk always flags where these member deals can give you the biggest savings.

How to Be a Savvy Waitrose Shopper:

  • Always check your myWaitrose app before you shop and activate your personal vouchers.
  • Don't forget the freebies! The complimentary newspaper or coffee adds real value to your shop.
  • Look for the special myWaitrose prices in-store to get exclusive discounts.

The Smart Shopping Strategy

The key to budget-friendly shopping at Waitrose is a two-part plan. First, build the foundation of your shopping list from the "Essential Waitrose" range – it's great quality at a fair price. Then, top up your trolley with special items from the weekly promotions. This way, you get the best of both worlds: everyday value and high-quality treats.
