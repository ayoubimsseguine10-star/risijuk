Finding Value in Morrisons' Weekly Offers

Published on October 20, 2024 | Category: Retail Analysis

Morrisons' unique selling proposition is its "Market Street," an integrated suite of specialist counters for fresh produce. This feature, combined with their loyalty program, creates specific avenues for value extraction. A successful shop hinges on monitoring the Morrisons offers this week across these key areas.

Maximizing Market Street

Market Street provides opportunities for savings not typically found in pre-packaged supermarket aisles. Engage with the specialist staff to identify the day's best value propositions.

  • Butcher Counter: Multi-buy deals on items like mince and chicken are common. These are ideal for bulk purchasing and freezing.
  • Bakery: Freshly baked goods are often subject to price reductions later in the day to clear stock. Timing your visit can lead to significant savings.
  • Fish Counter: The designated "Catch of the Day" is typically priced competitively and represents good value.

Leveraging the Morrisons More Program

The modern Morrisons More card has shifted from a simple points system to a platform for personalized, instant discounts. The critical step for any shopper is to check the app *before* shopping to activate relevant offers. This simple action compounds savings made elsewhere in the store and should be a mandatory part of your pre-shop routine.
