Unlocking Savings with Nectar at Sainsbury's

Your Nectar card is the key to getting great value at Sainsbury's. By combining the weekly Sainsbury's offers this week with your Nectar card, you can get instant discounts and earn valuable points. This guide will show you how.

Your Sainsbury's Savings Toolkit

Sainsbury's makes saving simple with these three tools:

  • Nectar Prices: This is the big one. Hundreds of products have special lower prices just for Nectar card holders. Always look for the Nectar Price labels on the shelf to see your exclusive discount.
  • The Nectar App: The app gives you personalized bonus point offers every week. These are based on your shopping habits, so you get rewarded for buying your favourite items.
  • SmartShop: Use the scanners in-store or the app on your phone to scan items as you shop. It helps you keep track of your spending and points out Nectar deals you might have missed.

How to Shop Smarter at Sainsbury's

Follow these simple rules to make sure you never miss a deal:

  1. Always Scan Your Nectar Card: This is the most important rule. If you don't scan your card or app at the checkout, you won't get the Nectar Price discounts.
  2. Check the App Before You Shop: Open the Nectar app before you go to the store to see your weekly bonus offers. You may need to "save" them to get the points.
  3. Let SmartShop Be Your Guide: Using the scanner makes it easy to spot Nectar Prices as you walk through the aisles, so you never miss a chance to save.
  4. Get a Quick Update from Risij.uk: We list the best Nectar Prices and promotions each week. Visit our Sainsbury's page for a quick overview.
