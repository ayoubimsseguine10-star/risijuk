A Simple Guide to Waitrose Offers This Week

Published on Jan 21, 2025 | In Saving Guides

Waitrose is known for its high-quality food, but that doesn't mean you can't find great value there. With the free myWaitrose card and a few simple tips, you can enjoy premium groceries for less. Here’s our guide to the Waitrose offers this week.

Get a myWaitrose Card

The myWaitrose card is the best way to save. It's free to join, and it gives you access to special deals:

  • Personal Vouchers: This is the best part! Every week, you can go into the app and choose two vouchers for money off certain types of products, like "20% off bakery items." Pick the ones that are most useful for your shop.
  • Member-Only Prices: In the store, look for the green signs that say "myWaitrose price." These are instant discounts just for members.

More Ways to Find Value

  1. Check Out the Dine-In Deal: Waitrose often has a great "Dine In" meal deal that includes a main, a side, a dessert, and a bottle of wine. It's usually fantastic value for a high-quality meal.
  2. Try the Essentials Range: The "Essential Waitrose" brand includes thousands of everyday items that have the quality you expect from Waitrose but at a more affordable price.
  3. Look for Reduced Items: The reduced-to-clear section is a great place to find a bargain, especially on high-quality ready meals or fresh meat. Try looking in the evening for the best selection.

You can see all the best Waitrose offers on the main Risij.uk site, which makes it easy to plan which vouchers to choose.
