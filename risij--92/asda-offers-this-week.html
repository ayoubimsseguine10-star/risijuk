ASDA Offers: Your Ultimate Checklist

Published October 25, 2024 | In: Savings Guides

Saving money at ASDA is all about using their two main tools: Rollbacks and the ASDA Rewards app. Nail these, and you'll cut your grocery bill. Here’s your simple checklist for tackling the ASDA offers this week.

Your Pre-Shop Checklist

A few minutes of prep makes all the difference. Before you leave the house, do this:

  1. Check for Rollbacks: These are ASDA's big, simple price drops. Look at the weekly offers list on a site like Risij.uk and add any essentials to your list. This is your chance to stock up.
  2. Scan for Star Products: Open the ASDA Rewards app. See what the current "Star Products" are. These give you bonus pounds in your Cashpot. If they're on your list anyway, it's free money.
  3. Look for Missions: Still in the app, check if there are any "Missions" you can complete, like "spend £5 on fruit and veg." Activate any that fit your shopping plan.

In-Store Execution

You've done the prep, now it's time to shop. Keep your phone handy and your list in front of you. Focus on grabbing the Rollback items and Star Products you identified. At the checkout, make sure you scan your ASDA Rewards app to collect your Cashpot earnings. It's that simple.
