Saving Big at Your Local Co-op

Updated: November 05, 2024 | Category: Shopping Guides

Your local Co-op is perfect for when you need to quickly grab a few things. While it's known for convenience, you can also find some fantastic savings in the Co-op offers this week, especially if you have a membership card.

Your Best Friend: The Co-op Membership Card

Becoming a Co-op member is a brilliant investment. For just £1, you unlock some great ways to save:

  • Special Member Prices: Look out for the blue shelf tickets to get exclusive discounts on all sorts of products.
  • Offers Just for You: The Co-op app sends you two personalized offers every week, based on what you usually buy.
  • Support Your Community: When you buy Co-op's own-brand items, they give back to local causes, which is a lovely extra bonus!

How to Spot the Best Deals

1. Check Out the Meal Deals

Co-op's "Frozen Deals" and other meal bundles are a fantastic way to get a whole meal for a great price. They're perfect for a quick and easy dinner. We always list the latest meal deals on our Co-op page on Risij.uk.

2. Scan the Ends of the Aisles

When you walk into the store, have a quick look at the entrance and the end of each aisle. This is where you'll usually find the best promotions of the week.
