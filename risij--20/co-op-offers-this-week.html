Co-op: Big Savings at Your Local Shop

Updated: November 05, 2025 | Category: Savings Guides

Your local Co-op is perfect for those times you need to quickly grab a few things. But did you know it’s also a great place to find deals? The Co-op offers this week can help you save on everyday items, especially if you know a few simple tricks.

Your Best Friend: The Co-op Membership

If you shop at Co-op, getting a membership is a must! For just £1, you unlock the best savings in the store:

  • Member Prices: Look for the special blue shelf tickets to get exclusive discounts on all sorts of products.
  • Personalised Offers: The Co-op app gives you two offers each week, chosen just for you based on what you buy.
  • Supporting Your Community: When you buy Co-op's own-brand products, a portion of what you spend goes to local charities and causes. It's saving money and doing good at the same time!

Finding the Best Deals

1. Check Out the Meal Deals

Co-op is famous for its "Frozen Deals," where you can get a main, a side, and a dessert (like pizza, chips, and ice cream) for one low price. They're perfect for a quick and easy dinner. We list these great bundles on our Co-op offers page on Risij.uk.

2. Look for Deals at the Front

The entrance and the ends of the aisles are where you'll usually find the best weekly promotions on fresh food and bakery items. A quick look around these areas when you walk in is always a good idea.

Popping to the shops? Take a quick look at the latest Co-op offers this week on Risij.uk before you go!
