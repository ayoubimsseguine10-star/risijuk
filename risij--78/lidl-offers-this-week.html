The Two Keys to Saving Money at Lidl

Published: October 21, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

Lidl offers a fantastic shopping experience with its high-quality groceries, fresh bakery, and the legendary "Middle of Lidl." To ensure you're getting the absolute best value, you need to master two key tools. This is your guide to the Lidl offers this week.

Key 1: The Lidl Plus App

If you shop at Lidl even occasionally, the Lidl Plus app on your smartphone is essential. It's a hub of savings that goes far beyond a simple loyalty card.

  • Weekly Coupons: Every Thursday morning, you'll receive a fresh batch of coupons. Activate the ones you want in the app and they'll apply automatically when you scan your digital card at the till.
  • Spend & Save: The "Coupon Plus" feature rewards you with money-off coupons for hitting spending targets throughout the month.
  • The Digital Scratchcard: After every shop, you get a digital scratchcard for a chance to win a prize, from a free croissant to money off your next shop.

Key 2: The "Middle of Lidl" Strategy

The middle aisle is where Lidl stocks its non-food special offers, changing themes every Thursday and Sunday. These items are limited and in high demand.

The strategy is simple: check the upcoming themes in the app or online leaflet. If you see something you want, be prepared to visit the store on the launch day, as popular items like DIY tools or kitchenware can sell out in hours. Don't Forget the Basics

While the app and the middle aisle are exciting, don't overlook the consistent value in Lidl's core range. Their fresh bakery, award-winning wines, and high-quality fruit and vegetables are always priced competitively.
