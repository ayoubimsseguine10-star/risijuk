More Than a Supermarket: A Guide to Morrisons Offers This Week

Published on July 28, 2024 | Category: Supermarket Guides

Morrisons stands out from its competitors with its strong focus on fresh food, largely thanks to its "Market Street" concept. When looking for the best Morrisons offers this week, it pays to look beyond the standard aisles and explore everything the store has to offer.

The Value of "Market Street"

Market Street is Morrisons' collection of fresh food counters, including a butcher, a fishmonger, a baker, and a deli. This is where you can often find high-quality products and great deals.

Highlights of Market Street:

  • Pick and Mix Salad Bar: A healthy and affordable lunch option.
  • Fresh Pizza Counter: Get a custom-made pizza, often with a "2 for £X" deal.
  • Butcher's Deals: Look for deals like "3 packs of meat for £10," which are great for stocking up.

My Morrisons Rewards

The "My Morrisons" loyalty program has moved away from points and now focuses on giving you personalized offers and money-off coupons. You simply scan your card or app at the checkout, and the discounts are applied. It's crucial to check the app before you shop to activate the offers that are relevant to you. These can range from money off your total shop to discounts on specific products you buy frequently.

This system is similar to what you might find with the Sainsbury's Nectar offers, focusing on tailored discounts rather than universal points.
