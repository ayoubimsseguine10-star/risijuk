A Guide to Optimizing Morrisons' Weekly Offers (2025)

Published: December 2024 // Category: Retail Guide

Morrisons sets itself apart with its "Market Street," a collection of in-store counters staffed by specialists like butchers, bakers, and fishmongers. The key to maximizing value is balancing the quality of these fresh products with app-based savings. Here’s our guide to the Morrisons offers this week.

The Core Value: Market Street

The specialist counters on Market Street are where Morrisons truly shines. Watch for multi-buy deals on high-quality fresh products, such as "2 for £8" on butcher-prepared meats, which allow you to enjoy premium quality at a more accessible price point.

Your Digital Tool: The "My Morrisons" App

The My Morrisons app uses a personalized model to deliver savings. To use it effectively, you need to be proactive:

  • Activate Your Offers: The app provides money-off vouchers based on your shopping habits. You must tap to activate these offers in the app *before* you get to the checkout.
  • Look for a "Basket Bonus": After you shop, you might be surprised with a "Basket Bonus" in the app—a special treat or money-off voucher for your next visit.
  • Join Specialist Clubs: If you're a parent or student, you can join subgroups like the "Baby Club" to receive extra, targeted discounts.

View All Current Morrisons Promotions

To see the latest Market Street deals and ensure you've activated all your My Morrisons offers, visit the official Morrisons offers this week page on the Risij.uk platform.

Expert Tip

Connect with the "Too Good To Go" app. Morrisons participates in the program, offering "Magic Bags" of surplus food at a deep discount, which helps reduce food waste and saves you money.
