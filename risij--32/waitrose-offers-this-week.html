Finding Value at Waitrose

Updated: November 05, 2024 | Category: Shopping Guides

Waitrose is known for its high-quality food, and while it can be pricier, it's definitely possible to find great value there. The secret is to make the most of the Waitrose offers this week and the myWaitrose loyalty scheme.

The myWaitrose Card: Your Key to Savings

The myWaitrose card is a brilliant tool for saving money. It's not about collecting points; it's about getting instant, personalized discounts:

  • Vouchers Just for You: The scheme learns what you buy and sends you personalized digital vouchers for money off your favourite items.
  • Exclusive Member Prices: Look out for special discounts that are only available to myWaitrose members.
  • Little Perks: You can also get a free newspaper when you spend a certain amount, which is a nice little extra.

How to Spot the Best Deals

1. "Dine In" Meal Deals

Waitrose often has a "Dine In" meal deal that includes items from its premium "No.1" range. This is a fantastic way to enjoy a high-quality meal for two at a much lower price.

2. Look for the Yellow Tags

The yellow price tags are your sign for a weekly offer. A quick scan of the aisles for these yellow tags is a simple way to see what's on promotion.

We round up the best of these offers on our Waitrose offers page at Risij.uk to save you time.

3. Don't Forget "Essential Waitrose"

Before you pick up a branded item, check the price of the "Essential Waitrose" version. This own-brand range is known for its great quality and is often the best value choice.
