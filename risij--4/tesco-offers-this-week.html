Maximise Your Tesco Shop with Clubcard Prices

Published on: October 24, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Schemes

The blue Tesco Clubcard is one of the most powerful tools in a UK shopper's arsenal. While many people simply scan it for points, its real value lies in the instant discounts available through Clubcard Prices. Combining these with the regular Tesco offers this week can lead to substantial savings.

Clubcard Prices vs. Regular Offers

It's important to understand the difference:

  • Weekly Offers: These are standard promotions available to all customers, like "2 for £5" or half-price deals.
  • Clubcard Prices: These are exclusive, lower prices on specific items available only when you scan your Clubcard or app. They are often significant reductions.

The key is to look for items that have both a weekly offer and an even lower Clubcard price, although this is rare. The main strategy is to build your shopping list around the best Clubcard Prices.

How to Get the Most from Your Clubcard

1. Always Scan Your Card (or App)

This sounds obvious, but it's the first step. Make sure you have the Tesco Grocery & Clubcard app for easy scanning and to see personalised coupons.

2. Check Risij.uk for the Best Clubcard Deals

We highlight the very best Clubcard Prices within our list of the Tesco offers this week. This saves you from having to hunt for the yellow labels in-store. Plan your meals around these deeply discounted items.

3. Turn Points into Vouchers

Don't let your points expire! Convert them into vouchers for money off your shopping or, for even better value, exchange them for 2x value with Tesco's Reward Partners for things like meals out or subscriptions.
