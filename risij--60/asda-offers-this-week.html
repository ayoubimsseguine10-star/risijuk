Your Guide to Asda's Weekly Deals and Rollbacks

Published on July 26, 2024 | Category: Shopping Tips

Asda is a go-to supermarket for many families thanks to its wide range of products and competitive prices. To get the most bang for your buck, it's essential to understand how to find the best Asda offers this week. This guide will walk you through their key saving opportunities.

Understanding Asda's Offers

Asda has several types of promotions that run throughout the store:

  • Rollbacks: These are Asda's signature deals. A Rollback is a price reduction on a product for a limited time. Look for the distinctive green "Rollback" signs.
  • Multi-buy Deals: Offers like "2 for £X" are common, especially on snacks, drinks, and household items. These are great for stocking up on items you use regularly.
  • Asda Rewards: By using the Asda Rewards app, you can earn pounds (not points) in your "Cashpot" on specific "Star Products" and complete "Missions."

Strategies for Saving at Asda

  1. Plan Your Meals Around Rollbacks: Before you shop, check the latest Rollbacks online. Planning your weekly meals around these discounted items can significantly cut your bill.
  2. Use the Asda Rewards App: Always scan your app at checkout. The Cashpot can build up surprisingly quickly and can be converted into vouchers to spend in-store.
  3. Compare Prices: While Rollbacks are great, always compare the unit price (price per 100g/ml) to ensure you're getting the best deal, especially compared to Asda's own-brand "Just Essentials" range.
