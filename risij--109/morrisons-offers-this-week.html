Morrisons Report: Integrating App & 'Market Street' Deals

Published on January 3, 2025 | Category: Retail Analysis

Morrisons differentiates itself within the competitive UK grocery market through its unique "Market Street" concept of in-store specialists (butchers, bakers, fishmongers). An effective savings strategy involves integrating the standard Morrisons offers this week with targeted use of the 'My Morrisons' loyalty app.

The 'My Morrisons' App: A Digital Coupon Model

Unlike traditional points-based systems, the 'My Morrisons' app operates on a direct discount model. It provides users with personalized, digital, money-off coupons based on their purchasing history.

Maximizing App Utility:

  • Pre-Shop Offer Activation: Users must manually activate their desired offers within the app before reaching the checkout.
  • Sub-Program Enrollment: The app contains specialized "Clubs" (e.g., Baby Club) that provide access to additional, category-specific discounts.
  • Consistent Scanning: The digital card must be scanned at the point of sale to apply the activated discounts to the final bill.

Strategic Purchasing at Market Street

The Market Street counters provide opportunities for value that extend beyond pre-packaged goods.

  • Engage with Counter Staff: Staff can provide information on daily specials and fresh inventory, which may not be advertised store-wide.
  • End-of-Day Reductions: Visiting the store in the late afternoon or evening often yields significant "yellow sticker" discounts on fresh items.
  • Utilize Ancillary Services: The salad bar can be a cost-effective option for single-serving meals or sides.
