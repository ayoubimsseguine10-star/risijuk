Is a Costco Membership Worth It?

Updated: November 05, 2024 | Category: Shopping Guides

Costco is famous for its bulk-buy deals, but with a yearly membership fee, you might be wondering if it's really worth it. The Costco UK offers can be amazing, but it works best for certain types of shoppers. Let's break it down.

Will a Membership Save You Money?

A Costco membership is most likely to be a good investment for you if:

  • You have a large family: The more mouths you have to feed, the more you'll benefit from buying in bulk.
  • You have plenty of storage space: You'll need a big pantry or an extra freezer to store all your bulk buys!
  • You can stick to a shopping list: Costco is full of temptations! A list will help you buy what you need and avoid impulse purchases.
  • You're a driver: The petrol at Costco is often much cheaper, which can help you make back your membership fee faster.

Tips for Smart Costco Shopping

1. Check the Coupon Book

Costco's monthly coupon book is your best friend. It shows you all the upcoming discounts so you can plan your shopping trips. We always share the highlights on our Costco offers page at Risij.uk.

2. Look for the Star (*)

If you see a little star or asterisk (*) on a price tag, it means that item won't be restocked. So if it's something you love, now's the time to grab it!

3. Don't Forget the Kirkland Brand

Kirkland Signature is Costco's own brand, and it's known for being great quality at a fantastic price. It's often just as good as the big-name brands, but for much less money.
