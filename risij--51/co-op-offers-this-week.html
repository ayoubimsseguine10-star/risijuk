Unlocking Value with Co-op Offers This Week

Published: October 24, 2023 | Category: Shopping Tips

Often seen as just a convenience store, your local Co-op can be a goldmine for savings if you know where to look. Beyond its accessibility, Co-op provides significant value through its membership program and targeted weekly deals. Let's dive into the best Co-op offers this week.

The Power of Co-op Membership

The single most important tip for saving at Co-op is to become a member. For a one-time £1 fee, you unlock a host of benefits:

  • Member Prices: Look for the blue shelf tickets. These are exclusive discounts for members that can offer substantial savings on everyday items.
  • Personalised Offers: In the Co-op app, you can choose two new personalised offers each week, giving you discounts on products you actually buy.
  • Community Rewards: A portion of what you spend on Co-op branded products goes back to local community causes, so you're saving money and supporting your neighbourhood.

Tip: Check the App Before You Shop

Always open the Co-op app before you head to the store. Activate your two weekly offers and check for any additional member-only deals. This small habit can lead to significant savings over time.

Meal Deals and Freezer Fillers

Co-op is also fantastic for its meal deals, particularly the "Freezer Fillers" offer, which typically includes several main items and sides for a set price (e.g., £5). These are perfect for easy weeknight dinners and represent excellent value compared to buying the items individually.
