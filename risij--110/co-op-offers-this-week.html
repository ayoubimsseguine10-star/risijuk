A Simple Guide to Co-op Membership Savings

Simple Guide | Updated: October 2024

Your local Co-op is perfect for grabbing things you need quickly, and with their membership, you can save money at the same time. For just a one-off £1, you can unlock some great deals. This guide explains the Co-op offers this week in simple terms.

How to Save with a Co-op Membership

Becoming a Co-op member is easy and gives you a few great perks:

  • Member Prices: Look for the blue signs in the store! These are special lower prices on everyday items that only members get.
  • Choose Your Own Offers: In the Co-op app, you can pick two offers each week that are tailored to you, helping you save on the things you buy most often.
  • Get Money Back: You get 2p back for every £1 you spend on Co-op's own-brand food. It adds up in your account and you can spend it later!

Your Easy Co-op Shopping Plan

Just remember these three simple steps:

  1. Check the App at Home: Before you pop to the shop, open the Co-op app and choose your two special offers for the week.
  2. Look for the Blue Signs: When you're in the store, keep an eye out for the blue member price signs to find the best deals.
  3. Always Scan Your Card: This is the key! Make sure you scan your membership card (or the app on your phone) at the checkout to get all your discounts and rewards.

If you want to see which member deals are available before you go, you can check the Risij.uk Co-op page for a quick summary.

Perfect for Top-Up Shops

A Co-op membership is ideal for those smaller, in-between shops. It works perfectly alongside your main weekly shop at a bigger supermarket like ASDA, helping you save money every day of the week.
