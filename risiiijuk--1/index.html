Costco UK: The Art of Buying in Bulk

Published on October 23, 2024

Costco is a whole different animal. It's a members-only warehouse where buying a giant pile of something means you pay less for each one. If you're smart about the Costco UK offers, you can save some serious cash over time.

Is the Membership Worth It?

If you've got a big family or lots of storage space, the annual fee usually pays for itself. The trick is to buy stuff you use all the time and that won't go bad.

  • Stick to the Basics: Think toilet paper, cleaning supplies, and stuff for the pantry.
  • Kirkland Signature Brand: Their own brand is as good as the big names but way cheaper. It's a no-brainer.
  • Cheap Gas: The gas at Costco is almost always the cheapest around, which can make up for the membership fee all by itself.

Figure out if buying in bulk is for you. See the latest Costco UK offers on Risij.uk.

The Lowdown on Tesco Clubcard: Your Secret Weapon for Savings

Published on October 16, 2024

The Tesco Clubcard is probably one of the best loyalty cards in the UK, for sure. It's the only way to get the best deals from the Tesco offers this week.

How Clubcard Prices Work

It's super simple. Things on sale have two prices on the shelf: the regular one, and a cheaper Clubcard Price. The discount happens automatically when you scan your Clubcard. The savings can be huge, sometimes even half price.

It's More Than Just Discounts

You also get points for every pound you spend. These points turn into vouchers you can use in the store, or you can trade them with "Reward Partners" for up to double their value on things like restaurants and days out. It's a double-double!

It's free to join, so what are you waiting for? Check the latest Tesco offers this week on Risij.uk.
