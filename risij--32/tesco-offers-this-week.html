Getting the Best Tesco Clubcard Deals

Updated: November 05, 2024 | Category: Shopping Guides

At Tesco, having a Clubcard is the secret to unlocking the best prices. To really make your budget stretch, you need to know how to use it properly. The Tesco offers this week are all built around their loyalty scheme, and this guide will show you how to get the most out of it.

The Most Important Thing: Clubcard Prices

Your Clubcard isn't just for collecting points anymore – it's a key that unlocks a special, lower set of prices. This is the biggest and best way to save money at Tesco.

How it Works:

  • Look for the Blue Tags: In the aisles, you'll see items with a blue Clubcard Price tag. This is the lower price you'll pay.
  • Scan Your Clubcard: Just scan your Clubcard (the physical card or on your phone app) at the till, and the price will drop automatically.
  • We Find the Best Deals: To help you plan, we search for the best Clubcard Prices each week and list them on our Tesco offers page at Risij.uk.

Other Smart Ways to Save

1. Look for the Aldi Price Match

Tesco matches the price of hundreds of basic grocery items to Aldi. When you see the "Aldi Price Match" logo, you can be sure you're getting a really competitive price on that item.

2. Check the Ends of the Aisles

You'll often find other weekly deals that aren't Clubcard-exclusive on the end of the aisles. It's always worth having a quick look as you walk around the store.
