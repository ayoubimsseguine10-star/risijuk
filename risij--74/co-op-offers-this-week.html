A Simple Guide to Your Co-op Membership

Published: Oct 24, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Guides

Shopping at the Co-op is a bit different because being a member really pays off. It's about getting great deals and helping your community. Let's look at how to get the most from the Co-op offers this week.

It All Starts with a £1 Membership

For just £1, you can become a Co-op member and open up a whole world of savings. The biggest benefit is getting special member-only prices on everyday items, which makes shopping at your local Co-op even better value.

Tip #1: Spot the Blue "Member Price" Signs

When you're in the store, keep an eye out for the bright blue "Member Price" signs. These show you where the exclusive deals are. Just remember to scan your card at the till to get the discount!

Tip #2: Choose Your Own Offers on the App

This is a really neat feature. The Co-op app lets you pick two special offers each week that are just for you, often based on what you usually buy. It's a great way to save on your favourites. It’s more personal than the general deals you might find in the SPAR offers next week.

Save Money and Help Your Community

Here's a lovely bonus: when you buy Co-op's own brand products, they give money back to local charities and causes. So, when you save with the Co-op offers this week, you're also doing a little bit of good for your area.

Top Tip: Check the Co-op app before you shop to activate your two personalised offers. It's a simple step that guarantees extra savings.
