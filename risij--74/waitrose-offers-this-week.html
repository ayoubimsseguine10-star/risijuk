A Guide to Shopping Smartly at Waitrose

Published: Oct 15, 2024 | Category: Shopping Guides

Waitrose is known for its wonderful, high-quality food, but many people think it's expensive. With a few simple tricks, you can enjoy all the lovely things Waitrose has to offer and still be a savvy shopper. Let's look at the Waitrose offers this week.

Your Best Friend at Waitrose: The myWaitrose Card

The myWaitrose card is your key to saving. It's not about collecting points; it's about getting instant perks. Here's what it gives you:

  • Weekly Vouchers for You: Check the app to choose from a list of weekly discount vouchers on items you might like.
  • A Free Cup of Tea or Coffee: One of the most loved perks! Get a free hot drink with any purchase when you bring a reusable cup.
  • Special Member Prices: Keep an eye out for exclusive discounts for myWaitrose members right at the shelf.

Tip #1: Don't Overlook the "Essential" Range

The "Essential" Waitrose range is their own brand, and it's fantastic. The quality is excellent, and the prices are very competitive, often similar to what you'd find at Morrisons or Sainsbury's. It's a great way to fill your basket with staples.

Tip #2: Look for Yellow Stickers in the Evening

Waitrose is a brilliant place to find reduced items later in the day. You can often find delicious ready meals, fresh bread, and sushi with yellow stickers. It's a perfect way to try something a bit special for a much lower price.

It's About Value, Not Just Price

The best way to shop at Waitrose is to look for offers on things where the quality really shines through, like a special cut of meat or a delicious artisan cheese. Often, the Waitrose offers this week let you buy a superior product for the price of a standard one elsewhere. That's what smart shopping is all about!

Top Tip: The free myWaitrose newspaper is full of lovely recipes and often has exclusive coupons inside!
