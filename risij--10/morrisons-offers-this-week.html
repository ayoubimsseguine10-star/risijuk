Fresh Savings: A Guide to Morrisons Offers This Week

Published on: 24th July 2024 | Category: Shopping Strategy

Morrisons sets itself apart with its unique "Market Street," which features skilled in-store butchers, bakers, and fishmongers. This emphasis on freshness, paired with a rewarding loyalty program, creates multiple avenues for savings. Here’s your guide to leveraging the Morrisons offers this week.

Maximize Savings with the Morrisons More Card

The "Morrisons More" loyalty program is a straightforward and effective way to get more value from your shopping. It allows you to earn points and get instant discounts.

More Card Key Benefits:

  • More Card Exclusives: Get immediate discounts at the checkout on hundreds of specially marked products simply by scanning your card.
  • Earn Points: Collect points on fuel and selected products in-store, which are then converted into money-off vouchers.
  • App-Based Bonuses: Download the app to access personalised coupons and bonus point offers, helping you earn rewards faster.

Discover the Top Deals! See all current promotions and More Card Exclusives on the Morrisons Offers This Week page on Risij.uk.

Explore Market Street for Quality and Value

The Market Street counters are a goldmine for savvy shoppers. The expert staff can provide custom cuts of meat or specific fish fillets, which helps reduce food waste and often proves more cost-effective than buying pre-packaged options. Also, keep an eye out for "when it's gone, it's gone" (WIGIG) deals across the store for limited-time bargains.

Your Morrisons Savings Blueprint

Before you shop, browse the weekly offers online and check the Morrisons More app for any personalised coupons to activate. Always scan your More card to secure exclusive prices and earn points. A visit to the Market Street counters can also yield high-quality, fresh products at excellent prices, making your shop both delicious and economical.
