A Simple Guide to Sainsbury's Offers This Week

Published on Jan 18, 2025 | In Saving Guides

Sainsbury's has made saving money really simple with one great tool: Nectar Prices. If you have a Nectar card, you can get some fantastic discounts. This guide will explain how to find the best Sainsbury's offers this week.

Nectar Prices = Instant Savings

Nectar Prices are special discounts just for Nectar members. When you're shopping, you'll see items with two prices: a regular price and a lower Nectar Price. To get the lower price, all you have to do is scan your Nectar card or app at the checkout. It's that simple!

A Simple Plan for Saving at Sainsbury's

  1. Look for Nectar Prices: When you're making your shopping list, check the current Nectar Prices on the Risij.uk site first. These are where you'll find the biggest savings.
  2. Check the Nectar App: The Nectar app sometimes gives you personal coupons for extra savings on things you buy often. It's always worth a quick look before you shop.
  3. Spot the Aldi Price Match: For basic items like bread and milk, look for the red "Aldi Price Match" signs. This means Sainsbury's has matched its price to Aldi's, so you know you're getting a good deal on your essentials.

By always scanning your Nectar card and keeping an eye out for these three things, you can easily lower the cost of your weekly shop.
