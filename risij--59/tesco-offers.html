Tesco Clubcard: Beyond the Points

Posted on: October 16, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Programs

The Game-Changer: Clubcard Prices

The Tesco Clubcard has evolved from a simple points-based system into an essential tool for saving money. "Clubcard Prices" offer instant, often substantial, discounts on a massive range of products, from everyday groceries to Finest* range treats. Shopping at Tesco without a Clubcard is like leaving money on the table. We curate the top deals from the Tesco offers this week on Risij.uk.

Your Clubcard Checklist:

  • Always have your Clubcard or app ready to scan.
  • Check the Tesco app for personalized coupons before you shop.
  • Build your meal plan around items with exclusive Clubcard Prices.

Multiplying Value with Reward Partners

The real secret to the Clubcard's power lies in its Reward Partners. You can exchange your points for vouchers that are worth double their value at a huge range of restaurants, cinemas, and attractions. This is the best way to maximize the value of the points you earn on your weekly shop, turning groceries into family days out or meals with friends.
