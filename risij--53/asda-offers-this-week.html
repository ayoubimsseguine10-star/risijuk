ASDA Savings Strategy: Rollbacks and Rewards

Updated: November 01, 2024 Category: Family Savings

ASDA has long been a destination for families seeking value. Their approach to savings is twofold: immediate price cuts through "Rollbacks" and long-term value via their ASDA Rewards app. Understanding how to use these tools in tandem is key to unlocking the full potential of the ASDA offers this week.

The Power of the Rollback

A "Rollback" is ASDA's signature promotion—a straightforward price reduction on everyday items. These aren't temporary sales but longer-term price drops. They provide consistent, predictable savings on hundreds of products, from pantry staples to household necessities.

Supercharging Savings with ASDA Rewards

The ASDA Rewards app is where the real strategy comes in. It allows you to build up a "Cashpot" by completing missions and buying "Star Products."

  • Star Products: Look for the star icon on products in-store and online. Buying these adds a percentage of their value back into your Cashpot.
  • Missions: Complete simple challenges, like spending a certain amount in a category, to earn extra pounds for your Cashpot.
  • Cash Out with Vouchers: Once you've built up your Cashpot, you can easily convert it into vouchers to spend on your shopping. It's a fantastic way to pay for a future shop.

A Holistic Approach

The savviest shoppers combine these tools. They stock up on Rollback items while also targeting Star Products to build their Cashpot for later. It’s a powerful combination of immediate savings and future discounts. Don't forget to also keep an eye on competitors, like the latest Iceland offers this week, for a complete savings picture.
