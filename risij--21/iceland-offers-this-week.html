A Simple Guide to Iceland's Offers This Week

Published: October 20, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

Iceland is the place to go for amazing frozen food deals, and they have a fantastic loyalty card that's a bit different from the rest. Let’s look at how to get the most from the Iceland offers this week.

The Iceland Bonus Card: Your Secret Weapon

This is one of the best loyalty schemes around. It’s not about collecting points; it's about getting free cash to spend. Here's how it works:

  • Load It Up: Decide on your shopping budget and load that amount onto your Bonus Card. You can do this in-store or online.
  • Get a £1 Bonus: For every £20 you load, Iceland gives you £1 for free. It’s like getting a 5% discount on your entire shop before you even start!
  • Get Special Prices: Just by having the card, you’ll get access to exclusive lower prices on lots of items every week.

Look for the Famous Multi-Buys

Iceland's "3 for £10" deals are a great way to stock up the freezer with family meals, pizzas, or party food. When you pay using the money on your Bonus Card, you're saving even more on these already great Iceland offers this week.

Enjoy Free Home Delivery

If you spend over a certain amount online (usually £40), Iceland will deliver your shopping to your door for free. It’s a great way to do a big shop without the heavy lifting.
