Fresh Deals on Morrisons' Market Street

Updated: November 05, 2025 | Category: Savings Guides

Morrisons feels like a traditional market right inside a supermarket, with its wonderful 'Market Street' of butchers, bakers, and fishmongers. This focus on fresh food, plus some great weekly deals, means the Morrisons offers this week are always full of tasty savings.

Unlock Savings with the "My Morrisons" App

The "My Morrisons" app is the best way to get extra discounts. It’s free and gives you:

  • Offers Just for You: Get money-off coupons based on what you usually buy. It could be for a specific product or money off your total shop!
  • Exclusive Member Prices: Look for special prices around the store that are only available when you scan your app at the checkout.
  • Little Surprises: Sometimes you'll find a free treat or a special bonus just for you in the app.

Don't Miss the Market Street Deals

Market Street is where you can often find the best value on fresh food.

1. Fresh Meat and Fish Offers

Keep an eye out for multi-buy deals on fresh chicken, mince, and fish. The "2 for £8" on selected meat packs is a very common and popular deal. Don't be shy to ask the butcher or fishmonger what's best on the day!

2. Bakery and Deli Goodies

Check for deals on freshly baked bread, cakes, and cooked meats from the deli counter. The Salad Bar is also a brilliant, cost-effective way to grab a healthy and delicious lunch.
