A Guide to Morrisons More & Market Street

Published on Jan 17, 2025 | In Savings Guides

One of the things we love about Morrisons is their "Market Street," with all its lovely fresh food! When you combine that with their More card, you can find some really great value. Here's our friendly guide to the Morrisons offers this week.

Using Your Morrisons More Card

The More card is a great little helper for your Morrisons shop. It helps you save in two main ways:

  • More Card Exclusive Prices: Keep an eye out for special prices that are just for More card holders. All you need to do is scan your card or app to get the discount.
  • Earning Points for Vouchers: You can collect points on things like fuel and selected items in-store. Once you have enough points, you can turn them into a £5 voucher to spend on your shopping!

Our Top Tips for Shopping at Morrisons

  1. Visit Market Street: The fresh food counters are wonderful. You can often find great multi-buy deals on meat, fish, and bakery items that are better value than the pre-packaged options.
  2. Check Your App for Offers: The Morrisons More app often has special offers just for you, based on what you usually buy. It's always worth a quick look before you shop to see if there are any extra savings to be had.
  3. Try the "Too Good To Go" App: Lots of Morrisons stores partner with an app called Too Good To Go. It's a way for them to sell 'magic bags' of surplus food at the end of the day for a very low price. It's a great way to save money and prevent food waste!
