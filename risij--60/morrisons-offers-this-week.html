A Shopper's Guide to Morrisons Offers This Week

Published on July 26, 2024 | Category: Shopping Tips

Morrisons stands out with its "Market Street," featuring in-store butchers, bakers, and fishmongers. This focus on fresh food, combined with a great loyalty scheme, provides many opportunities to save. Here's how to make the most of the Morrisons offers this week.

Leverage the Morrisons More Card

The Morrisons More card is your key to unlocking the best value. The scheme has evolved, and here's how it works now:

  • More Card Exclusives: Look for the special prices marked for More card holders throughout the store. These are instant discounts, similar to a Tesco Clubcard.
  • Earn Points: You earn More Points on selected products and at the fuel station. 5,000 points gets you a £5 voucher (a "Morrisons Fiver").
  • Personalised Offers: Check the app for offers tailored to your shopping habits. Make sure to activate them before you shop.

Don't Miss These Morrisons Deals

  1. Market Street Offers: Keep an eye on the counters. You'll often find deals like "2 for £8" on fresh meats or fish, which offer excellent quality and value.
  2. Multi-save Deals: Morrisons runs many multi-buy offers across the store on big brands and their own-label products.
  3. "Magic Bags": Through the "Too Good To Go" app, many Morrisons stores offer "Magic Bags" of unsold food at the end of the day for a heavily discounted price. It's a great way to save money and reduce food waste.

By combining More Card deals with the weekly offers, you can significantly reduce your grocery bill. Risij.uk helps by bringing all the top Morrisons offers together in one place for easy viewing.
