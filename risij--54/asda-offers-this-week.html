Your Guide to ASDA's Weekly Deals

Published on July 28, 2024 | Category: Shopping Tips

ASDA is a go-to supermarket for many families thanks to its competitive pricing and wide range of products. Understanding how to navigate the ASDA offers this week can lead to significant savings. Let's break down their main promotions.

Key ASDA Promotions

ASDA's offers generally fall into a few key categories that are worth looking out for during your weekly shop.

Rollbacks

This is ASDA's most famous promotion. "Rollback" means ASDA has lowered the price on a product for a limited time. These are genuine temporary discounts and are great for stocking up on household staples. You'll see the yellow Rollback signs throughout the store.

Multi-buy Offers

Look for deals like "2 for £X" or "3 for 2." These are common on items like drinks, snacks, and toiletries. Before you buy, always check if you'll actually use the extra items to ensure it's a worthwhile saving.

Just like ASDA, other big stores like Tesco and Sainsbury's also have extensive multi-buy deals, so it's always worth comparing.
