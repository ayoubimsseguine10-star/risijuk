Costco Warehouse Hack: How to Shop Like a Pro

Updated: July 2024 | Category: Store Guides

Costco isn't a supermarket; it's a warehouse playground for savers. But to truly win, you need to know the secret language of the warehouse and have a game plan. This is your hack sheet for making your membership pay for itself with the Costco UK offers.

Hack #1: Decode the Price Tags

Costco's price tags are talking to you, if you know how to listen. They contain secret codes that tell you if you're getting a good deal or a once-in-a-lifetime bargain.

Your Price Tag Cheat Sheet:

  • Price ends in .97: This is a store manager's markdown. It's a good deal, specific to that warehouse, to clear stock.
  • An asterisk (*) in the corner: The "death star." This item is being discontinued. If it's something you love, buy it now because it won't be back.
  • Price ends in .00: This is often the lowest possible price, a final clearance to get it out the door. Grab it.

Hack #2: Kirkland Signature is Your Best Friend

Costco's own brand, Kirkland Signature, is a massive hack. These products are often made by the same companies as the big brands, just with different packaging and a much lower price tag. From coffee to vodka, batteries to olive oil, Kirkland is almost always a smart buy.

Hack #3: Plan Around the Coupon Book

The monthly coupon book (delivered to your door and available online) is your treasure map. These are the official, planned discounts. Don't make a big purchase, like tyres or a TV, without checking if it's in the coupon book first. Timing your buys around these deals is fundamental to maximizing your Costco UK offers.
