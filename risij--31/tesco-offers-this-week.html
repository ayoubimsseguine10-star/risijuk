A Simple Guide to Tesco Offers This Week

Published: October 16, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

Shopping at Tesco is all about the Clubcard. It's the key to unlocking almost all of their best deals. This simple guide will show you how to use it to get the most from the Tesco offers this week.

Rule #1: Always Scan Your Clubcard

It's as simple as that. "Clubcard Prices" give you big, instant savings on hundreds of items every week. The price difference for members is often huge, so there's no reason to ever pay full price. The card is free, so if you don't have one, it's worth getting one today.

Get More Value from Your Points

For every £1 you spend, you get one Clubcard point. Every few months, Tesco turns your points into vouchers to spend on your shopping. But here's the clever bit: you can often double their value with Tesco's Reward Partners.

  • Swap Vouchers for Treats: You can exchange your vouchers for deals on things like cinema tickets, restaurant meals, or even a Disney+ subscription.
  • Check for Deals: Before your vouchers arrive, have a look at the Reward Partners website to see where you can get the most bang for your buck.

What Else to Look For

Alongside the member deals, keep an eye out for Tesco's popular "Dine In" meal deals and their "Low Everyday Prices" on hundreds of basic items. These add even more value to the weekly Tesco offers this week.
