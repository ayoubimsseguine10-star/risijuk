Tesco Clubcard: How to Level Up Your Savings Game

Updated: July 2024 | Category: Loyalty Schemes

The Tesco Clubcard is more than a loyalty card; it's a power-up for your shopping. With "Clubcard Prices" offering instant discounts, it's become essential. But the real pros know how to play the long game with points and partners. Here's how you extract maximum value from the Tesco offers this week.

Level 1: Master the App & Instant Wins

The first level of the game is simple: use the Tesco Grocery & Clubcard app every single time. This is your key to unlocking the instant discounts of Clubcard Prices. Never shop without it. The app also serves up personalized coupons based on what you buy, so check it before you shop to clip extra savings.

Your Core Moves:

  1. App is Always On: Your digital card lives in the app. Use it for every transaction, no matter how small.
  2. Clip Your Coupons: Check the "Coupons" section in the app before every shop. It's targeted, so it's usually stuff you actually want to buy.
  3. Scan Everything: Even if you don't think an item has a Clubcard Price, scan your card. You'll still collect points.

Level 2: The Reward Partner Endgame

This is where you go from amateur to pro. Collecting points is one thing, but how you spend them is what matters. Using your vouchers in-store is fine, but the real cheat code is using them with Tesco's Reward Partners. You can often double or even triple the value of your points, turning £5 in vouchers into £10 or £15 for meals out, days out, or subscriptions.

Always check the Reward Partner section of the Clubcard site before cashing in your vouchers. For a quick price check against the competition, see how the Sainsbury's offers this week stack up with their Nectar Prices.
