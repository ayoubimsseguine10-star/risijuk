Finding Value with Waitrose Offers This Week

Published on July 11, 2024 in Supermarket Guides

Waitrose & Partners has a reputation for high-quality products and excellent service. While it may be perceived as more expensive, smart shoppers know how to find fantastic value by taking advantage of the Waitrose offers this week and the myWaitrose loyalty program.

Unlocking Savings with myWaitrose

The myWaitrose card is free to sign up for and offers a range of benefits that go beyond simple points collection. It’s all about personalised value and rewarding experiences.

Key myWaitrose Benefits

  • Personalised Vouchers: Every week, you can choose two vouchers from a personalised selection for money off specific products you love.
  • Free Hot Drink: When you make a purchase in-store, you can get a free cup of coffee or tea from the self-serve machine. Don't forget to bring your own reusable cup!
  • Exclusive Member-Only Prices: Look for special discounts across the store that are only available to myWaitrose members.

Top Deals to Look For

Beyond the loyalty card, Waitrose has several regular promotions that offer great value.

  1. Dine In For Two: A popular deal that typically includes a main, a side, a dessert, and a bottle of wine or non-alcoholic alternative for a set price, offering a significant saving.
  2. No.1 Range Offers: Look for multi-buy deals on their premium "No.1" range, allowing you to sample high-end products at a lower price.
  3. Essential Waitrose: This is Waitrose's extensive own-label range, offering everyday items at competitive prices without compromising on the quality and ethical standards the brand is known for.

Reduced to Clear Bargains

Like other supermarkets, Waitrose reduces items nearing their sell-by date. These are often high-quality products, from fresh fish and meat to ready meals and desserts, available at a fraction of their original price. The best time to look is usually in the evening.
