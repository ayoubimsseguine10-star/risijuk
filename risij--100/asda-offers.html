Intel Drop: October 25, 2024 | Target: Major Supermarkets

Cracking the 'Rollback' Code

Asda's "Rollbacks" are designed to create a sense of urgency, but not all price cuts are created equal. The key is to separate the real deals from the noise. The weekly intel feed of Asda offers this week on the Risij.uk platform is your decoder ring, highlighting the price drops that actually matter to your bottom line.

Three Rules for Hacking Rollbacks:

  • Cross-reference the Risij.uk intel with your shopping list. Hunt for your essentials.
  • Go long on shelf-stable goods. When a staple item gets a deep discount, stock up.
  • Look for "deal stacking" opportunities where Rollbacks can be combined with other promos.

The Multi-Buy Trap

Multi-buy offers like "2 for £5" can be a trap for the undisciplined shopper. The question is never "is it a good deal?" but "do I actually need two?". Only pull the trigger on these offers for high-consumption items you'd be buying anyway. Otherwise, you're just spending more, not saving.
