Morrisons More Card Advantages & Market Street Deals

Category: Loyalty Schemes | Published: July 2024

Morrisons stands out with its 'Market Street' concept, featuring in-store butchers, bakers, and fishmongers that give it a traditional market feel. Combining this focus on freshness with the revamped Morrisons More loyalty program is the key to unlocking the best value from the Morrisons offers this week.

The Modern More Card

The latest iteration of the Morrisons More card has shifted from a simple points system to a model of instant rewards and exclusive prices, making it more dynamic and user-friendly.

How to Make the More Card Work for You:

  • Look for "More Exclusives": Similar to Tesco's Clubcard Prices, these are instant discounts applied at the checkout when you scan your More card or app. These offers change regularly.
  • Personalised Offers: Check the app for offers tailored to your shopping habits. Activating these before you shop can lead to significant savings on your favourite items.
  • Points on Fuel: A major perk is earning points on fuel purchases at Morrisons petrol stations, which can then be converted into money-off vouchers for your grocery shop.
  • More Card Events: Look out for special events like "More Points Week" where you can earn bonus points on your entire shop, accelerating your savings.

The Market Street Advantage

Don't overlook the fresh food counters. The quality is often excellent, and you can buy exactly the amount you need, reducing waste. The counters frequently have their own special offers that aren't available on the pre-packaged equivalents. Always compare with other quality-focused stores, like the Waitrose offers this week, to ensure you're getting the best deal on premium goods.
