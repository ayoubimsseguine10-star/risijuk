Getting the Most from Iceland Offers This Week

Posted on October 21, 2023 in Loyalty Cards

Iceland is a titan of the frozen food world, but their offers extend far beyond the freezer aisles. To truly benefit, you need to get to grips with their Bonus Card and weekly promotions. Let's break down the Iceland offers this week.

Your Secret Weapon: The Bonus Card

The Iceland Bonus Card is one of the most straightforward and rewarding loyalty schemes. For every £20 you save onto the card, Iceland gives you £1 back. That's a 5% return, which is better than most savings accounts! You can then spend this bonus in-store or online whenever you like.

Key Deals to Watch For

  • £1 Frozen Favourites: Iceland is famous for its extensive range of £1 items, perfect for stocking up on essentials and treats.
  • Multi-buy Deals: Look for the "3 for £5" or similar offers on big-name brands and Iceland's own exclusive ranges, like TGI Fridays and Slimming World.
  • Online Exclusives: The Iceland website often features special deals and bigger packs that you won't find in-store, with free delivery over a certain spend.

Planning Your Iceland Haul

Before you shop, load some money onto your Bonus Card to earn that extra cash back. Check the website for the latest multi-buy offers and plan your meals around them. Combining a big online shop with your Bonus Card savings is the ultimate Iceland savings strategy.
