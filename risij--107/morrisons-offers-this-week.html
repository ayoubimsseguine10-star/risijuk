A Tactical Guide to This Week's Morrisons Promotions

Posted on November 16, 2024 in "Retail Strategy"

Morrisons' unique "Market Street" fresh counters provide a key tactical advantage. Optimal savings are achieved by combining these fresh deals with the "My Morrisons" app. This guide outlines how to exploit the best Morrisons offers this week.

The Keys to Value Extraction at Morrisons

Your two primary assets as a Morrisons shopper are the rewards app and the fresh food counters. The "My Morrisons" app delivers personalized discounts, while Market Street provides access to high-quality fresh goods, often at a lower cost than their pre-packaged equivalents.

Top Tactics for Your Morrisons Mission:

  • Activate "My Morrisons" Offers Pre-Shop: Before entering the store, you must open the app and activate your personalized offers. This is the only protocol to ensure these discounts are applied at checkout.
  • Conduct Reconnaissance of Market Street: The butcher, fishmonger, and bakery sections frequently run exclusive promotions. A sweep of this area is a mandatory part of any in-store operation.
  • Execute Late-Day "Yellow Sticker" Sweeps: Morrisons is known for aggressive price reductions on near-expiry items. A visit in the late afternoon or evening can yield high-value targets.
  • Monitor for Fuel Promotions: Periodically, Morrisons issues vouchers for fuel discounts based on in-store spend. Be aware of these high-value secondary objectives.

For any regular Morrisons operative, the "My Morrisons" app is not optional. It is an essential component of your cost-saving toolkit.
