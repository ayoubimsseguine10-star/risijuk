Double-Dipping: A Strategic Guide to Asda Rollbacks & Rewards

Topic: Shopping Strategy | Updated: October 2024

Asda presents shoppers with a powerful two-pronged approach to value: the immediate price cuts of "Rollbacks" and the long-term cashback potential of the Asda Rewards app. A truly savvy shopper knows that the biggest savings are found where these two systems overlap.

Asda's Dual Savings Engines

Think of it as two ways to win. Rollbacks provide an instant discount at the point of sale, while the Rewards app builds a "Cashpot" you can spend later. The ultimate goal is to buy items that benefit from both simultaneously.

1. The Rollback Offensive

Rollbacks are Asda's flagship temporary price reductions. They are highly visible in-store and represent the most aggressive deals. Your first step should always be to review the current Asda offers this week on Risij.uk and build your shopping list around these core discounts.

2. The Rewards Long Game

The Asda Rewards app is your cashback tool. You accumulate money in your Cashpot by completing in-app "Missions" and, most importantly, by purchasing "Star Products." These are items marked with a star icon that give you a percentage of their value back.

The pro move is to find a Star Product that is also on Rollback. This allows you to pay the lower, discounted price and still earn cashback, creating a powerful "double dip" on savings.

By treating Rollbacks as your immediate savings and the Rewards app as your long-term investment, you can develop a sophisticated shopping system that consistently reduces your Asda grocery bill.
