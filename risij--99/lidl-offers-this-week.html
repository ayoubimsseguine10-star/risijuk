A Guide to Lidl Plus & the Middle Aisle

Published on Jan 16, 2025 | In Savings Guides

A trip to Lidl is always an adventure! There are two main parts to it: using the fantastic Lidl Plus app and, of course, exploring the treasures in the "Middle of Lidl." Here's our friendly guide to making the most of the Lidl offers this week.

Part 1: Using the Lidl Plus App

The Lidl Plus app is a brilliant way to get a little extra off your shop. Here are a few simple tips:

  • Activate Your Coupons: Before you start shopping, remember to open the app and tap to 'activate' your weekly coupons. You need to do this to get the discounts.
  • Earn Extra Rewards: The more you shop, the more you save! The app tracks how much you spend each month, and you can unlock extra coupons when you reach certain amounts.
  • Scan Your Card at the Till: This is the most important part! Just scan your digital card from the app at the checkout to apply all your lovely discounts.

Part 2: Exploring the "Middle of Lidl"

The middle aisle is where all the exciting, non-food items are. You can find anything from DIY tools to kitchen gadgets! The stock changes every Thursday and Sunday.

  1. Get a Sneak Peek: You can see what's coming up in the weekly leaflet, which we always feature on Risij.uk. It's a fun way to see if there's anything you fancy.
  2. Be Quick for Popular Items: If you see something you love, it's best to pop in on the Thursday or Sunday it's released, as the most popular items can sell out fast.

We hope these tips help you have a fun and budget-friendly shop at Lidl!
