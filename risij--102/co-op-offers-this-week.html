The Co-op Member Price Advantage

Category: Loyalty Programmes

Shopping at Co-op is different when you're a member. The loyalty scheme is designed to give you direct savings while also helping local causes. Here’s a quick look at how to get the most from the Co-op offers this week.

How Your Membership Saves You Money

The benefits of a Co-op membership are simple and rewarding. It costs just £1 to join, and you start saving immediately.

Key Member Benefits:

  • Member Prices: Look for the blue signs in-store. These are special lower prices on everyday items, exclusively for members.
  • Personalised Offers: Use the Co-op app to choose two offers you want each week, so you save money on the things you buy most often.
  • Rewards for You and Your Community: When you buy Co-op branded products, you get 2p back for every £1 spent, and Co-op donates another 2p to a local cause you care about.

Making Co-op Work for You

Co-op is perfect for those top-up shops during the week. By using your membership, you can get great value on your daily essentials without having to travel to a larger supermarket. To see how these deals stack up against others, you can always visit the main hub on Risij.uk.
