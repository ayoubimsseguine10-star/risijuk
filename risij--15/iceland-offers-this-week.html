Iceland Offers & The Bonus Card: A Genius Savings Hack

Published on July 29, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Programs

Iceland is the undisputed king of the frozen aisle, but the secret to truly unlocking its value lies in a small piece of plastic: the Bonus Card. This isn't just another loyalty card; it's a powerful savings tool. Let's explore how to combine it with the Iceland offers this week for maximum effect.

The 5% Back Trick: How the Bonus Card Works

The Iceland Bonus Card is brilliantly simple. For every £20 you load onto the card, Iceland instantly adds an extra £1. That's a 5% bonus. You're essentially getting paid to plan your shopping. You can then spend this balance in-store or online whenever you like.

How to Be an Iceland Pro:

  1. Budget, Load, Save: Before you do a big shop, load your estimated spend onto your Bonus Card to instantly claim your bonus.
  2. Stack the Savings: The bonus you earn is on top of any existing promotions. Pay for the famous "3 for £10" frozen food deals using your Bonus Card balance to get a discount on top of a discount.
  3. Unlock Free Delivery: Using your Bonus Card in-store also qualifies you for free home delivery on shops over £25.
  4. Enjoy Exclusive Perks: Cardholders are often entered into special competitions and get exclusive birthday treats.

What Should You Buy?

Beyond the amazing multi-buy deals on frozen goods, keep an eye out for Iceland's exclusive product ranges from brands like Greggs, TGI Fridays, and Slimming World. The value is incredible compared to buying them from the restaurant. Also, don't sleep on their grocery aisles, which are often just as cheap as their main rival, Farmfoods.
