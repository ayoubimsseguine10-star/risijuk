Maximizing Morrisons' Weekly Deals

Published on: October 21, 2024 | Category: Shopping Guides

Morrisons stands out with its 'Market Street' concept, featuring in-store butchers, bakers, and fishmongers that offer a fresh, traditional shopping experience. This focus on freshness, combined with strong weekly promotions, means the Morrisons offers this week are full of opportunities for savvy shoppers.

Leverage the "My Morrisons" App

The "My Morrisons" loyalty program is your gateway to the best savings. It's a free app that provides:

  • Personalized Offers: Get money-off coupons tailored to your shopping habits. These can be for specific products or for a total spend (e.g., £5 off a £40 shop).
  • Exclusive Member Prices: Look for special pricing in-store available only when you scan your app at checkout.
  • Surprise Bonuses: Occasionally, you might find a free item or extra points added to your account.

Don't Miss Market Street Deals

Market Street is the heart of Morrisons. You'll often find the best value here.

1. Fresh Meat and Fish Offers

Look for multi-buy deals on fresh chicken, mince, sausages, and fish. The "2 for £8" on selected meat packs is a common and excellent value offer. Ask the fishmonger or butcher about the day's specials.

2. Bakery and Deli Promotions

Check for deals on fresh bread, cakes, and cooked meats. The "Salad Bar" can also be a cost-effective way to get a healthy lunch.
