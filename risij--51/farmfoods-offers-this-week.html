Farmfoods: More Than Just Frozen Deals

Published: October 22, 2023 | Category: Shopping Tips

While Farmfoods built its reputation on fantastic value in frozen food, savvy shoppers know it's a treasure trove for all sorts of grocery bargains. From branded goods to fresh essentials, understanding how to shop at Farmfoods can lead to substantial savings. Let's explore the best Farmfoods offers this week.

The Weekly Flyer is Your Best Friend

Unlike some larger supermarkets, Farmfoods relies heavily on its physical and digital weekly flyers to announce the top deals. This is your primary tool for planning your shop.

  • Find it Online: Before you go, always check the latest flyer on the Farmfoods website or at Risij.uk.
  • Look for Vouchers: Occasionally, the flyers will contain spend-and-save vouchers (e.g., £5 off a £50 shop). These are pure gold.
  • Multi-buy Mania: Farmfoods excels at multi-buy offers, especially on frozen items. Think "3 for £10" on pizzas or "2 for £5" on ready meals.

Tip: Stock Up Your Freezer

The core strength of Farmfoods is still its frozen section. When you see a great multi-buy deal on items your family regularly eats—like chicken, fish fingers, or vegetables—it's wise to stock up. This is where having a chest freezer pays dividends.

Beyond the Freezer Aisle

Don't just stick to the frozen section. Farmfoods offers competitive prices on a range of ambient goods, including cereals, canned foods, drinks, and snacks. Often, they have big-brand items at prices that beat the major supermarkets, so it's always worth a browse.
