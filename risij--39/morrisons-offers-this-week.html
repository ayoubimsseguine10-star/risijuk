Your Guide to Morrisons More Card Savings

Published: October 28, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Guides

Morrisons is well-known for its "Market Street," with fresh meat, fish, and baked goods made in-store. To get the best value, you'll need their "Morrisons More" loyalty card. This guide will show you how to use it to make the most of the Morrisons offers this week.

How Does the Morrisons More Card Work?

The More card is simple. It gives you "More Card Exclusives," which are instant discounts on items, just for members. You can also earn points on certain products and at the petrol station, which turn into money-off vouchers.

Simple Tips for Saving at Morrisons

  • Use the App for Extra Offers: The Morrisons More app is the key. You need to open it and activate your personalized offers before you shop to get the bonus points.
  • Look for "More Card Exclusives": These are instant discounts you get just by scanning your card. They are clearly marked on the shelves and offer the biggest savings.
  • Check Out Market Street Deals: The fresh food counters often have great promotions. It's a fantastic way to buy high-quality meat, fish, and bread for less.
  • Earn Points on Fuel: If you fill up your car at a Morrisons petrol station, remember to scan your card. The points you earn on fuel can add up to useful discount vouchers.
