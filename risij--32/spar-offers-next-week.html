Convenient Savings at Spar

Updated: November 05, 2024 | Category: Shopping Guides

Spar is the perfect place for grabbing what you need quickly and easily. While you pay for convenience, you can still find some excellent bargains by keeping an eye on the Spar offers next week. This guide will show you what to look out for.

How to Save Money at Spar

Because Spar shops are often run by local people, the deals can sometimes be different from store to store. However, they always have national offers that are great value.

1. Look for the "Mega Deals"

Every week, Spar has "Mega Deals" on popular branded snacks, drinks, and treats. These are usually priced at £1 or on a great multi-buy offer, making them perfect for stocking up on your favourites.

2. Check Out the Meal Deals

Many Spar stores have great-value deals for an easy evening meal, like a pizza and a drink for a set price. These can be a much cheaper and quicker alternative to a takeaway. We list the latest national deals on our Spar offers page at Risij.uk.

3. Try Spar's Own Brand

Spar's own-brand range has won awards for being both tasty and great value. Choosing Spar's own products for everyday items is a simple way to lower the cost of your shop without compromising on quality.
