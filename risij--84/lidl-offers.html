Your Digital Key: A Masterclass in the Lidl Plus App

Topic: Shopping Strategy | Updated: October 2024

While Lidl is already known for its incredible baseline value, the Lidl Plus app is the tool that separates casual shoppers from master savers. This free digital companion unlocks an extra layer of discounts that are unavailable to anyone else.

Unlocking a New Tier of Savings

The core of the Lidl Plus app's power lies in its weekly coupons. Every Thursday morning, a new set of personalized discounts goes live, offering significant savings on specific product categories or your total spend. Using the app effectively is a non-negotiable for any serious Lidl shopper.

The Lidl Shopper's Digital Checklist

  • Pre-Shop Activation is Crucial: Open the app before you head to the store and tap 'Activate' on every coupon. They will not be applied at the checkout unless you have manually activated them first.
  • Scan Your Digital Card at Checkout: The final step is to present the QR code from your app at the till. One scan applies all your activated coupons.
  • Strategize Around the "Middle of Lidl": This central aisle features weekly themed non-food deals. Check the Lidl offers this week online to see if the upcoming themes (like DIY, kitchen, or garden) align with your needs.
  • Leverage the Bakery: Lidl's in-store bakery offers exceptional quality at low prices. It's a simple way to add a touch of luxury to your shop for less.

By making the Lidl Plus app an integral part of your shopping routine, you can consistently achieve a level of savings that goes far beyond their already competitive shelf prices.
