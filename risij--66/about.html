Our Charter

Our Founding Principle

The core philosophy of Risij.uk is that financial prudence in grocery shopping should be accessible to all, not just the most diligent. In an increasingly complex market, our objective is to serve as a beacon of clarity, providing UK households with the intelligence required to navigate their weekly shop effectively.

Our Methodology

Each week, our focus is the systematic collection, verification, and organization of thousands of offers from every major UK supermarket. From the tactical advantages of the Lidl offers this week to the strategic planning required for the Aldi Specialbuys, we transform raw data into actionable insights on our main platform. This journal serves to elaborate on the strategies behind the data.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing an impartial, accurate, and freely accessible resource. Risij.uk exists to simplify the process of saving money, removing the burden of manual deal comparison. We aim to foster a community of informed consumers who can confidently manage their household budgets with greater efficiency.
