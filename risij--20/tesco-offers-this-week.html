Tesco: Get the Most from Your Clubcard

Updated: November 05, 2025 | Category: Savings Guides

Tesco has made its Clubcard the most important tool for saving money in its stores. If you shop at Tesco, having a Clubcard is a must! The Tesco offers this week are almost all based on these exclusive deals, which give you big, instant savings.

Clubcard Prices: Your Key to Lower Prices

It's free to sign up for a Clubcard, and it immediately gives you access to cheaper prices on hundreds of products all over the store.

How It Works:

Just scan your Clubcard (the physical card or on the app) when you pay. Any items with a Clubcard Price will automatically be discounted. The savings can be huge – sometimes as much as half price!

At Risij.uk, we always list the best Clubcard Prices each week, so you can plan your shopping list around the biggest and best savings.

More Ways to Save at Tesco

1. Aldi Price Match

Tesco wants to be competitive on your everyday essentials. They match the prices of hundreds of their products to Aldi, including basics like bread, milk, eggs, and fresh vegetables. Just look for the "Aldi Price Match" logo on the shelf to be sure you're getting a great deal.

2. Great Weekly Deals

On top of the Clubcard Prices, Tesco also has special deals on a few key products each week. You can usually find these in the promotional aisles at the front of the store or at the end of the aisles.
