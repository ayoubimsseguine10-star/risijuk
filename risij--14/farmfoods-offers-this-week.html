Your Guide to the Best Farmfoods Offers This Week

Published: October 21, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

Farmfoods is a budget shopper's paradise, offering incredible value on frozen food and big brands. The key to maximizing savings here is stacking their two main types of deals. This is your guide to the best Farmfoods offers this week.

Your First Move: Get the Vouchers

Before you even think about visiting, sign up for the Farmfoods newsletter on their official website. They regularly email money-off vouchers (e.g., "£5 off a £60 spend"). This is a free, instant discount on your total shop, so it's an essential first step.

Mastering the Multi-Buy Aisle

Farmfoods is the undisputed champion of multi-buy deals. You’ll find "3 for £5" or "2 for £10" offers on a huge range of products, from branded pizzas to frozen vegetables.

The Multi-Buy Game Plan:

  • Assess Your Space: The golden rule of bulk buying: it's only a deal if you have the freezer space to store it properly.
  • Mix & Match for Variety: Many offers let you mix and match different items within the same deal, so you don't have to buy three of the same thing.
  • Target Big Brands: Farmfoods often has better prices on well-known brands like Birds Eye and McCain's than the larger supermarkets.
