Your Smart Guide to Lidl's Weekly Finds

Updated: October 28, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

The "Middle of Lidl" is famous for a reason—it's a treasure hunt for amazing bargains that changes every week. But it can be a bit chaotic! The Lidl offers this week are full of great deals, and this simple guide will help you find the best ones without the stress.

How to Shop the Middle Aisle

Lidl's special non-food items, or "Finds," arrive every Thursday and Sunday. Stock is limited, so once an item is gone, it's usually gone for good. This means a little planning goes a long way. Here’s how to get ahead.

A Simple Shopping Plan

  • Check Online First: The best place to start is online. Look at the weekly leaflet on Risij.uk to see what's coming and decide what you're interested in before you go.
  • Shop on Thursday or Sunday Morning: The most popular items can sell out fast. Visiting on the day they arrive gives you the best chance to get what you want.
  • Use the Lidl Plus App: This is a must-have. You can get extra coupons for more savings, and it keeps your receipts digitally, which makes returns easy.
