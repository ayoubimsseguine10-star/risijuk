A Guide to Co-op Member Deals

Published on Jan 12, 2025 | In Savings Guides

Your local Co-op is fantastic for quick top-up shops, and with their membership, it can be great for savings too! For just £1, you can become a member and unlock some lovely deals. Here's our guide to the Co-op offers this week.

Why the £1 Membership is Worth It

Becoming a Co-op member is a lovely way to save. Once you've joined, you get access to special prices that are just for members.

  • Member Prices: Keep an eye out for the blue signs in-store. These show you the special, lower price you get just for being a member.
  • Choose Your Own Offers: Each week, you can open the Co-op app and choose two personalised offers. It's a nice little bonus!

What's extra special is that when you shop as a member, you're also helping to support good causes in your local community. It feels great to know your shopping is doing a little bit of good!

Other Little Tips for Your Co-op Shop

  1. Check Out the Meal Deals: The Co-op often has great meal deals, like their pizza and ice cream bundles. They can be a simple and affordable option for dinner.
  2. Look for End-of-Day Reductions: If you pop in towards the end of the day, you might find some items with yellow stickers, which means they've been reduced for a quick sale.
