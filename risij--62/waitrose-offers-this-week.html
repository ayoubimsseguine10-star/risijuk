Finding Value at Waitrose: Offers This Week & myWaitrose

Waitrose & Partners is synonymous with high-quality produce, excellent customer service, and a premium shopping experience. While it may not be known as a budget supermarket, smart shoppers can find fantastic value by using the Waitrose offers this week and the myWaitrose loyalty scheme.

How to Shop Smart at Waitrose

Saving money at Waitrose is all about a targeted approach:

  • myWaitrose Card: This is the cornerstone of saving. It's free to join and gives you access to member-only prices, personalised vouchers, and freebies like a complimentary coffee with any purchase (check local store for availability).
  • Weekly Offers: Waitrose has a great selection of weekly offers, often featuring significant discounts on high-quality items like wine, meat, and their premium "No.1" range.
  • Essential Waitrose Range: Don't overlook the own-brand "Essential" range. It offers Waitrose quality on hundreds of everyday items at competitive prices.

Pro Tips for Your Waitrose Visit

Combine quality with value using these tips:

  1. Check Your Vouchers: Before you shop, log into your myWaitrose account online or in the app to select your personalised money-off vouchers for the week.
  2. Look for Yellow Stickers: Waitrose has a generous reduction policy. Look for items with yellow stickers, especially in the evenings, for big discounts on food nearing its use-by date.
  3. Plan Around the "Dine In" Deal: The Waitrose "Dine In" for two is a popular offer that includes a main, side, dessert, and wine or non-alcoholic alternative for a set price, offering excellent value.
  4. Consult Risij.uk: We highlight the best of the weekly offers on our dedicated Waitrose page, saving you the time of searching through them all.

Affordable Quality

Shopping at Waitrose doesn't have to break the bank. By being a savvy, organised shopper and taking full advantage of the myWaitrose benefits, you can enjoy premium products at a surprisingly affordable price, offering a different kind of value compared to discounters like Lidl.
