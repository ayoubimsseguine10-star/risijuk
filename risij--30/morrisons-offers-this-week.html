Morrisons: Market Street Value Extraction

Updated: November 05, 2024 | Category: Strategic Guides

Morrisons' unique 'Market Street' concept provides access to fresh, specialist-prepared goods within a standard supermarket framework. This creates unique opportunities for value extraction within the Morrisons offers this week, particularly when combined with their digital loyalty system.

System Optimization: The "My Morrisons" App

The "My Morrisons" application is the primary tool for unlocking personalized discounts. Its key functions include:

  • Targeted Vouchers: The system generates money-off coupons based on an analysis of your purchasing habits.
  • Exclusive Member Pricing: Access to a tier of lower prices on select items, activated by scanning the app at checkout.
  • Randomized Bonuses: Occasional deployment of surprise rewards, such as a free item or bonus discount.

High-Value Target Zones: Market Street

Market Street is the primary zone for acquiring high-quality fresh goods at competitive prices.

1. Fresh Protein Acquisition

Monitor this zone for multi-buy promotions on fresh meat and fish, such as the frequently deployed "2 for £8" deal on protein packs. Direct engagement with the butcher or fishmonger can yield intelligence on the day's best value items.

2. Bakery and Deli Assets

Promotions on freshly baked goods, charcuterie, and cheeses are common. The Salad Bar offers a high-utility, cost-effective solution for creating customized, healthy meals.
