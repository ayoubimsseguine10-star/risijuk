Combining Asda Rollbacks and Rewards

Category: Savings Guides | Published: October 2024

Asda provides two primary ways to save money: "Rollback" price reductions for instant savings, and the "Asda Rewards" app for building a cashback balance. Using both together is the most effective way to lower your bill.

The Two Components of Asda Savings

Think of it as a two-step process. Rollbacks reduce the price you pay at the checkout today. The Rewards app accumulates a "Cashpot" that you can use to reduce the cost of a future shop.

Step 1: Focus on Rollbacks

Rollbacks are temporary price cuts on various products. Before you shop, it's a good idea to check the current Asda offers this week on a site like Risij.uk to identify the best deals for your shopping list.

Step 2: Use the Rewards App for "Star Products"

Within the Asda Rewards app, certain items are designated as "Star Products." When you buy these, you earn cashback into your Cashpot. You can also complete "Missions" in the app to earn more.

The most effective strategy is to buy a Star Product that is also on Rollback. This allows you to pay the lower price while also earning cashback, maximizing your total savings.

Actionable Tip: Before your next shop, identify potential savings by reviewing the full list of Asda offers for this week on our main website.

By consistently using Rollbacks for immediate discounts and the Rewards app to build long-term value, you can make your grocery budget go further at Asda.
