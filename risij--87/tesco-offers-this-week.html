How to Make Your Tesco Clubcard Go Further This Week

Published on July 29, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Programs

The Tesco Clubcard is a brilliant little tool for saving money, but are you getting the most out of yours? The Tesco offers this week are always a good start, but with a few friendly tips, your Clubcard can unlock even more savings. Let's dive in!

It’s More Than Just a Scan

Getting into the habit of scanning your card is great, but the real magic happens when you know what to look for. The special "Clubcard Prices" are your best friend here—they offer instant discounts just for members and are often the best deals in the whole shop.

Where to Spot the Best Clubcard Deals

Keep an eye out for those cheerful blue and yellow Clubcard Price tags. You'll often find the best value on:

  • Everyday Essentials: Big savings on things you buy all the time, like coffee, cereal, and cleaning supplies, can make a real difference to your weekly budget.
  • A Little Treat: Clubcard Prices often make the Tesco Finest range much more affordable. It's the perfect excuse to treat yourself!
  • Stocking Up: The frozen food and drinks aisles are great places to find multi-buy offers, perfect for filling the freezer or planning a get-together.

Simple Tips to Boost Your Savings

Here are a few easy ways to make that little blue card work even harder:

  1. Use the Tesco App: The Tesco Grocery & Clubcard App is wonderful. It sends you personalized coupons based on your shopping habits, which you can use on top of the regular offers.
  2. Look for "Yellow Sticker" Bargains: Tesco reduces the price of items near their use-by date. These discounts are a fantastic way to save, especially in the evenings, and you still get your Clubcard points!
  3. Use Reward Partners for Vouchers: Before you spend your points in-store, check the Reward Partners section in the app. You can often double or even triple the value of your points for things like family days out or restaurant meals.
