Decoding Morrisons' Weekly Offers

Published on July 28, 2024 | Category: Shopping Tips

Morrisons stands out with its "Market Street," offering fresh meat, fish, and baked goods prepared in-store. To save money, it's crucial to understand how to combine their loyalty program with the weekly Morrisons offers this week.

The My Morrisons Loyalty Scheme

The "My Morrisons" app is central to saving money here. It has moved away from a points-based system to one focused on instant, personalized discounts.

  • Personalized Offers: The app provides you with money-off vouchers based on your shopping history. For example, you might get 50p off your favourite brand of coffee.
  • Basket Bonus: After you scan your card at the till, you might get a surprise "Basket Bonus," which could be anything from £5 off your next shop to a free bakery item.
  • Clubs: Join specific clubs like the "NHS Club" or "Teacher's Club" for extra discounts if you are eligible.

In-Store Promotions

Beyond the app, Morrisons runs standard promotions like multi-buys and price cuts. Their "Price Lock" events, where they freeze the price of hundreds of staples for a set period, are particularly good for budget planning. These are often as competitive as the ASDA offers this week.
