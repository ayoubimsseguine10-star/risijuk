A Simple Guide to Farmfoods Offers This Week

Published: October 21, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

Farmfoods is a hidden gem for budget shoppers, especially if you love a well-stocked freezer. The trick to getting amazing value is to combine their two best features: vouchers and multi-buys. Here’s a simple look at the best Farmfoods offers this week.

First Things First: Get Your Vouchers!

Before you go, make sure to sign up for the Farmfoods email newsletter on their website. They send out money-off vouchers (like "£2 off a £25 spend") that give you an instant discount on your entire shop. It’s free money!

Become a Multi-Buy Master

Farmfoods is famous for its "3 for £5" or "2 for £10" deals on everything from big-brand pizzas to bags of frozen veg.

A Few Friendly Tips:

  • Check Your Freezer Space: A great deal is only great if you have room to store it. A quick check before you leave home can save you a headache later.
  • Mix It Up: Many deals let you mix and match products, so you can try different things instead of buying three of the same item.
  • Look for Famous Brands: You can often find brands like Birds Eye or McCain's for much cheaper at Farmfoods than in other supermarkets.
