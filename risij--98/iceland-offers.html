How the Iceland Bonus Card Works

Category: Savings Guides | Published: October 2024

The Iceland Bonus Card is one of the most simple and effective loyalty schemes available. It functions like a savings card that rewards you for planning your grocery spending in advance.

A Guaranteed 5% Return on Savings

The system is very straightforward. The Bonus Card acts as a digital wallet for your Iceland shopping. For every £20 you load onto your card, Iceland adds a £1 bonus. This is equivalent to a 5% return on your money.

By loading your shopping budget onto the card before you pay, you effectively give yourself a 5% discount on everything you buy.

A Simple Strategy for Saving at Iceland

  • Load Your Card First: The most important step is to add funds to your Bonus Card before you head to the checkout. This simple habit ensures you always save.
  • Combine with Multi-Buy Deals: The 5% saving is applied on top of any other offers. Use your bonus-loaded card to pay for Iceland's well-known multi-buy deals for the best value. Check the latest Iceland offers this week to see what's available.
  • Take Advantage of Free Delivery: For larger shops, Iceland provides free home delivery from the store, allowing you to stock up on frozen goods without the difficulty of transport.

Actionable Tip: Identify the best multi-buy deals to purchase with your Bonus Card savings by viewing the full list of Iceland offers this week on our main site.

The Iceland Bonus Card is a valuable tool for any budget-conscious shopper due to its simplicity and guaranteed return on savings.
