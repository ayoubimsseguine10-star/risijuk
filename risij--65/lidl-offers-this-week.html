How to Conquer the "Middle of Lidl" and More

Published: October 21, 2023 | Category: Shopping Tips

Lidl has become a firm favourite for UK shoppers, offering a unique combination of high-quality groceries and an eclectic, ever-changing selection of non-food items. To get the best value, you need a strategy that covers both the food aisles and the legendary "Middle of Lidl." Here’s how to make the most of the Lidl offers this week.

Embrace the Lidl Plus App

If you shop at Lidl, the Lidl Plus app is non-negotiable. It's your key to unlocking the best savings:

  • Weekly Coupons: Every week, you get a new set of coupons for discounts on specific products. Activate the ones you need before you get to the checkout.
  • Partner Offers: The app includes exclusive discounts with other companies, like cinemas and travel firms.
  • Digital Receipts: Keep track of your spending and make returns easier with digital receipts stored in the app.
  • Coupon Plus: The more you spend each month, the more coupons you unlock for future shops.

Tip: The Middle of Lidl

The infamous middle aisle is where Lidl stocks its limited-time non-food items, from power tools to kitchen gadgets. New themes launch every Thursday and Sunday. If you see something you want, grab it. It likely won't be there on your next visit. This week, Lidl offers this week are featuring amazing deals on garden supplies in the middle aisle.

Don't Miss the Bakery

One of Lidl's standout features is its in-store bakery. Offering fresh bread, pastries, and savoury snacks at incredibly low prices, it's the perfect place to pick up a treat. The selection is baked throughout the day, so it's always fresh.
