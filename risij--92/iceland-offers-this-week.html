Iceland Offers: The 5% Bonus Trick

Published October 19, 2024 | In: Savings Guides

If you shop at Iceland without using the Bonus Card trick, you're leaving free money on the table. It’s the single most powerful way to save on all the Iceland offers this week. Here’s exactly how it works.

How the Trick Works

The Iceland Bonus Card isn't a normal loyalty card. It's a savings card that gives you a guaranteed bonus. The magic formula is:

For every £20 you load onto the card, Iceland instantly gives you £1 for free.

That’s a 5% return on your money before you've even bought anything. No other supermarket offers a guaranteed return like this.

Your Simple 2-Step Plan

Using this trick is incredibly easy.

  1. Load Before You Shop: Before you start filling your trolley, decide on your budget. Go to the cashier and ask to top up your Bonus Card with that amount (in multiples of £20 to get the bonus). If you plan to spend £40, load £40 and you'll instantly have £42 to spend.
  2. Pay with the Card: After you've done your shopping, simply pay with your Bonus Card balance at the checkout. You've just given yourself a 5% discount on your entire shop.

Pro Tip: Stack it Online

This trick works online too. Load up your card, then use it to pay for your online shop. You can stack the 5% bonus on top of Iceland's online-exclusive deals and free home delivery for maximum savings. Check all the latest deals on Risij.uk to plan your attack.
