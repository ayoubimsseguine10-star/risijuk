Lidl Offers This Week: A 2025 Guide to App & Aisle Savings

Published on January 16, 2025 | Category: Saving Strategies

Shopping at Lidl is a two-front battle for savings: mastering the digital coupons in the Lidl Plus app and navigating the treasure hunt of the "Middle of Lidl." This guide gives you the strategy to win on both fronts and conquer the Lidl offers this week.

Weaponize the Lidl Plus App

The Lidl Plus app is an essential tool for any serious saver. Don't just scan it; manage it:

  • Activate Coupons Pre-Shop: Before you even enter the store, open the app and activate all the weekly coupons. They don't apply automatically.
  • Track Your 'Coupon Plus' Progress: The app rewards spending milestones throughout the month with extra coupons. Know your target and plan a larger shop if you're close to the next reward.
  • Spin the Wheel: After every shop, spin the digital wheel for a chance to win a free item on your next visit. It's a small but satisfying bonus.

Navigating the "Middle of Lidl"

This legendary central aisle is where Lidl's most exciting non-food bargains live. New themes drop every Thursday and Sunday.

  1. Consult the Leaflet: The weekly leaflet (available on Risij.uk) is your map. It reveals the upcoming themes, allowing you to decide if a special trip is warranted for "DIY Week" or "Kitchen Gadgets."
  2. Act Fast on High-Demand Items: Popular electronics, branded tools, or seasonal items can sell out within hours of the store opening. If it's a must-have, be there on Thursday morning.
  3. Check for Leftover Bargains: Sometimes, unsold items from previous weeks are consolidated into a clearance section, offering even deeper discounts.

By combining digital discipline on the app with strategic strikes on the middle aisle, you can ensure you never miss a Lidl bargain.
