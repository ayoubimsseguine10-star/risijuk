ASDA's Rollback Deals: A Deep Dive into Real Savings

Published on July 29, 2024 | Category: Deal Analysis

ASDA's "Rollback" is one of the most recognizable promotions in UK supermarkets. But what does it really mean for your wallet? Are these long-term price drops genuine bargains? We're taking a closer look at the ASDA offers this week to see if Rollbacks live up to the hype.

Understanding ASDA Rollback

Unlike a typical weekly sale, a Rollback is a price reduction that lasts for a longer period—often several weeks or even months. ASDA positions these as a commitment to long-term value. This is great for staple items you buy regularly, as you can count on the lower price for a while.

How to Make the Most of Rollbacks

  • Stock Up on Staples: When you see a Rollback on non-perishable items like pasta, canned goods, or cleaning supplies, it's a great opportunity to stock up.
  • Check the "Before" Price: ASDA usually displays the previous price. This helps you gauge the actual saving. Risij.uk helps you track these price drops over time.
  • Don't Assume It's the Cheapest: While a Rollback is a price cut, it's still wise to compare. The Sainsbury's offers or Aldi's base prices might still be lower on certain items. A quick cross-reference is key.
