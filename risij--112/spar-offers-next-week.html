Making the Most of Spar Offers Next Week

Published on July 14, 2024 in Supermarket Guides

Spar is the friendly face of convenience shopping in neighbourhoods across the UK. While you might pop in for a pint of milk or a newspaper, looking out for the Spar offers next week can lead to some surprisingly good deals on everyday items.

Finding Value at Your Local Spar

Because Spar stores are independently owned, offers can sometimes vary from one location to another. However, there are common types of deals to look for that are promoted nationally.

Types of Deals to Spot

  • "Tonight's Tea" Deals: Many Spar stores run promotions on evening meal solutions, bundling a main and a side for a set price.
  • Wine and Beer Offers: Spar is often competitive on alcohol prices, with deals like "2 for £12" on bottles of wine or multi-buy offers on cases of beer.
  • Big Night In Bundles: Look for deals that combine snacks, soft drinks, and a pizza or ready meal - perfect for a weekend treat.

The Spar Own Brand

Don't overlook the Spar own-brand range. It has been developed to offer great quality at a lower price point than the big brands it sits alongside.

  1. Value on Essentials: Products like bread, milk, cheese, and pasta from the Spar brand are often priced very competitively.
  2. Award-Winning Products: Spar's own-brand range has won numerous awards, especially their wines and ready meals, proving that value doesn't mean compromising on quality.
  3. Seasonal Specials: The own-brand range often includes seasonal items, like BBQ foods in the summer or festive treats at Christmas, which are usually part of a promotion.
