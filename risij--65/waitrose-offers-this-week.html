How to Find Value with Waitrose Offers This Week

Published: October 15, 2023 | Category: Shopping Tips

Waitrose is known for its high-quality produce and premium products, but that doesn't mean it's out of reach for savvy shoppers. By leveraging the myWaitrose loyalty program and keeping an eye on specific promotions, you can enjoy the quality of Waitrose on a budget. Let’s explore the Waitrose offers this week.

Make myWaitrose Work for You

The myWaitrose card is free to join and is your ticket to the best savings in the store:

  • Personalised Vouchers: Waitrose sends out weekly vouchers based on your shopping habits, offering money off specific items or your total shop. Check your emails or the app.
  • Exclusive Member Prices: Look for the myWaitrose price tickets in-store for instant discounts on a wide range of products.
  • Freebies and Perks: While the famous free coffee has changed, myWaitrose still offers perks like a free newspaper when you spend a certain amount and discounts at the fish and meat counters on specific days.

Tip: The Essential Waitrose Range

Don't dismiss Waitrose's own-brand range. The "Essential Waitrose" line offers hundreds of everyday items at competitive prices. It's an easy way to lower the cost of your basket without sacrificing the quality Waitrose is known for.

Look for Yellow-Sticker Reductions

Like all supermarkets, Waitrose reduces items that are near their sell-by date. The times for these reductions vary by store, but they often happen in the evening. This is a fantastic way to pick up high-quality ready meals, meat, and baked goods for a fraction of their original price.
