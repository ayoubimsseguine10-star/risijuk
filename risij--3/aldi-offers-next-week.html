How to Prepare for the Aldi Offers Next Week

Published on July 28, 2024 | Category: Shopping Strategy

Aldi shoppers know that the early bird gets the worm, especially when it comes to the highly anticipated Specialbuys. Planning ahead is crucial, and knowing the Aldi offers next week can give you a significant advantage. Here's our guide to getting ahead of the crowds.

Understanding the Aldi Specialbuys Cycle

Aldi Specialbuys are unique, limited-stock items that go on sale every Thursday and Sunday. They can be anything from electronics and homeware to clothing and toys. The key is that they are not regular stock, so when they sell out, they are usually gone for good.

Where to Find a Preview

  1. The Aldi Leaflet: You can pick one up in-store or view it online. It typically shows the items for the upcoming week.
  2. Risij.uk: We consolidate this information, making it easy to see what's coming up. Check our Aldi offers page for the latest updates.
  3. Aldi's Website: They often have a "Coming Soon" section for the next wave of Specialbuys.

The Super 6: Fresh Deals

Alongside the Specialbuys, Aldi offers its "Super 6" every fortnight. This is a selection of six fruits and vegetables available at incredibly low prices. Planning your meals around the Super 6 is a fantastic strategy for cutting down your grocery expenses.

Top Tips for Aldi Shopping

  • Set a reminder: If there's a must-have Specialbuy, know whether it's a Thursday or Sunday release and plan your trip accordingly.
  • Be flexible: Sometimes the item you want may be sold out. Have a backup plan or be prepared to visit another store.
  • Check online first: Some Specialbuys are available online before they hit stores, which can be a great way to secure a popular item.
