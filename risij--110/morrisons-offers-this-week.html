A Simple Guide to Saving at Morrisons

Simple Guide | Updated: October 2024

What makes Morrisons special is its 'Market Street', with fresh food counters for meat, fish, and bread, just like a traditional market. The best way to save is by combining these fresh deals with the offers in the app. This guide explains how to find the best Morrisons offers this week.

The Two Best Ways to Save at Morrisons

A smart Morrisons shop uses two simple tools:

  • The My Morrisons App: This is a free app that sends you special money-off coupons based on what you usually buy. The more you use it, the better the offers get!
  • Market Street Deals: The fresh food counters often have their own special offers that are better value than the pre-packaged options. It's always worth a look.

Your Easy Morrisons Shopping Plan

Follow these three simple steps for a great shop:

  1. Activate Offers in the App: Before you shop, open the My Morrisons app and activate any coupons you want to use. You have to do this yourself for them to work at the till.
  2. Visit Market Street First: When you get to the store, have a walk along the fresh food counters to see if there are any good deals on meat, fish, or bread for your meals this week.
  3. Look for Other Deals: As you do the rest of your shopping, keep an eye out for the usual weekly deals, like half-price items and multi-buys, around the store.

To see what the main weekly offers are before you go, you can check the summary on the Risij.uk Morrisons page.
