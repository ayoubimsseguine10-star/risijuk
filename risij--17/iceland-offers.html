Iceland's Bonus Card: The Coolest Way to Save This Week

Iceland is famous for its vast frozen food selection, but savvy shoppers know the real key to savings is the Bonus Card. This powerful loyalty program can significantly boost your savings. This guide will show you how to make the most of the Iceland offers this week.

How the Bonus Card Works

The Iceland Bonus Card is a unique loyalty scheme. Instead of collecting points, you load money onto the card to spend later. For every £20 you load, Iceland gives you £1 for free. That's a guaranteed 5% return, which is better than any points-based system. You can see all the latest product deals to spend your bonus on at the Risij.uk Iceland page.

Maximizing Your Iceland Shop

  • Budget with the Bonus Card: Use the card to set your weekly shopping budget. Loading it up at the start of the month is a great way to control spending and get your bonus.
  • Look for the Big Multi-Buys: Iceland is famous for its "3 for £10" or similar deals on big-name frozen brands. These are perfect for stocking the freezer.
  • Enjoy Free Delivery: Iceland offers free next-day delivery on online orders over a certain amount, which is great for large, bulky frozen shops.
