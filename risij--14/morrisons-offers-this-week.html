How to Maximize the Morrisons Offers This Week

Published: October 19, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

Morrisons offers a unique blend of fresh "Market Street" quality and savvy savings opportunities. To get the most out of your shop, you need to engage with their loyalty program and know where to look for bargains. Here’s your guide to the best Morrisons offers this week.

Unlocking Value with the Morrisons More Card

The Morrisons More card is essential. It provides two key benefits:

  • More Card Exclusives: Just like other schemes, this gives you instant lower prices on a wide range of items. Look for the special price tags in-store.
  • Points for Pounds: You earn points on fuel and selected products. Once you hit 5,000 points, you get a £5 voucher (a 'Fiver') to spend on anything you like.

The Art of Yellow Sticker Hunting

Morrisons is renowned for its excellent "yellow sticker" reductions on items nearing their expiry date. The best time to hunt for these is typically in the evening. You can find incredible bargains on fresh bread, meat from the butcher counter, and ready meals.

Don't Miss the 'Dine In' Deals

For an easy, high-quality meal, Morrisons' "Dine In" deals are hard to beat. They typically include a main, side, dessert, and a drink for a set price, offering fantastic value compared to a takeaway. These are perfect for a weekend treat.

By using your More Card for every shop and keeping a sharp eye out for reductions, the weekly Morrisons offers this week can lead to substantial savings.
