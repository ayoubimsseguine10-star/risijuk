Iceland's Best Deals This Week

Published on July 28, 2024 | Category: Shopping Tips

While Iceland is the undisputed king of frozen food, many shoppers overlook its growing range of fresh groceries, pantry items, and household goods. The key to unlocking the best Iceland offers this week is to combine their famous multi-buys with their loyalty program.

How to Save at Iceland

Iceland's pricing strategy is simple and effective, focusing on clear, upfront value for customers.

Three for £X Deals

This is Iceland's signature offer. You'll find deals like "3 for £5" on frozen chicken, "3 for £3" on pizzas, and similar offers across a massive range of products. This is one of the most consistent ways to save and is often better value than buying individually.

The Iceland Bonus Card

This is a unique loyalty scheme. It's not a points card, but a savings card. For every £20 you load onto your Bonus Card, Iceland adds £1 for free. This is effectively a 5% discount on your shopping. You can use the saved money to pay for your groceries at any time. The card also gives you access to exclusive prices on certain items and free home delivery.

When looking for frozen deals, it is always a good idea to compare with the Farmfoods offers this week, as their promotions are often very similar.
