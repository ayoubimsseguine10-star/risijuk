Our Mission: Make Saving Simple.

Why we built Risij.uk

Our Obsession

We're obsessed with one thing: helping you save money on your groceries. We got tired of endless searching across different supermarket websites and apps, trying to find the best deals. We knew there had to be a smarter way to see all the offers this week in one simple place.

What We Built

Our main platform, Risij.uk, is the solution. It's a powerful tool that gathers and organizes the weekly flyers from every major UK supermarket. From the must-have Lidl offers this week to the forward-looking Aldi offers next week, we put the most current savings right at your fingertips.

This blog is where we go deeper. It’s an extension of our main site where we share expert shopping strategies, review deals, and give you the insider tips you need to become a pro saver.

Our Promise

We promise to keep our service fast, free, and easy to use. Our team works around the clock to ensure all offers are accurate and up-to-date. We believe saving money on your food bill should be effortless, and we're here to make that a reality.
