A Guide to Tesco Clubcard Prices

Published on Jan 20, 2025 | In Savings Guides

Your Tesco Clubcard is a fantastic little tool for saving money. Shopping without it means you could be missing out on some brilliant deals! This friendly guide is here to explain how you can use your Clubcard to make the most of the Tesco offers this week.

What are Clubcard Prices?

Clubcard Prices are special, lower prices on all sorts of items, just for people with a Clubcard. It's Tesco's way of saying thank you for being a loyal customer. To get these special prices, all you have to do is scan your Clubcard (or the app on your phone) when you're at the checkout. It's the most important step to saving money at Tesco!

Three Simple Tips for Your Tesco Shop

  1. Plan Your Shop Around Clubcard Prices: The best way to get a big discount is to see what's on a Clubcard Price deal before you go. We keep a full, up-to-date list on our main Risij.uk website.
  2. Look for the Aldi Price Match: For your everyday essentials, Tesco matches its prices to Aldi on hundreds of items. Keep an eye out for these signs to know you're getting a great deal on the basics.
  3. Keep an Eye Out for Yellow Stickers: If you're shopping later in the day, you might be lucky enough to spot some items with yellow "reduced to clear" stickers. This is a brilliant way to grab a real bargain!

By making your Clubcard a key part of your shopping routine, you can turn a regular grocery trip into a great savings opportunity.
