Iceland's Bonus Card Benefits: A Cool Way to Save

Category: Loyalty Schemes | Published: July 2024

Iceland is the undisputed king of the frozen aisle, but their savings strategy extends far beyond just low prices. The Iceland Bonus Card is a unique loyalty scheme that acts more like a savings wallet, helping you budget and earn rewards. Understanding it is crucial to making the most of the Iceland offers this week.

How the Bonus Card Works

Unlike a points-based system, the Bonus Card is beautifully simple. You load money onto the card, and Iceland gives you a bonus for doing so. It's a way to pre-pay for your shopping while getting rewarded.

Key Features and Tactics:

  • The £1 Bonus: For every £20 you load onto your Bonus Card, Iceland instantly adds an extra £1. That's a guaranteed 5% return on your money before you even start shopping. It's one of the most generous loyalty rewards on the market.
  • Budgeting Made Easy: By loading a set amount onto your card each payday, you create a dedicated grocery budget that's easy to stick to.
  • Exclusive Cardholder Deals: Keep an eye out for special promotions and discounts available only to Bonus Card holders. These are often advertised in-store and online.
  • Free Home Delivery: Bonus Card holders often get access to perks like free home delivery on online orders over a certain spend, which is a significant saving in itself.

Beyond Frozen

While famous for its frozen selection, Iceland has significantly expanded its range of fresh and grocery items. The same multi-buy offers and low prices often apply to their fresh meat, dairy, and produce, making it a viable option for a full weekly shop.
