A Guide to Saving at Your Local Spar

Published on Jan 19, 2025 | In Savings Guides

Your local Spar is so handy for picking up essentials, but it can also be a good place to find a bargain! Here's our friendly guide to making the most of the Spar offers for next week.

What Kind of Deals Can You Find?

Spar is great for offers that help with quick and easy meals. You'll often find:

  • Easy Meal Ideas: Look out for deals like "Tonight's Tea" bundles, which give you everything you need for a simple dinner at a nice price.
  • Discounts on Essentials: They often have weekly deals on everyday items like bread, milk, and coffee, which is great for a quick top-up.
  • Multi-buys on Treats: It's always a good place to find multi-buy offers on snacks, chocolate, and drinks.

Our Top Tips for Your Spar Visit

  1. Pick Up a Leaflet: The best way to see what's on offer is to grab one of the free leaflets in-store. It's a great way to see what's coming up.
  2. Check the Windows: The biggest and best deals are usually advertised on posters in the shop window, so you can spot them before you even go inside!
  3. Try the Spar Brand: For everyday items, the Spar own-brand is a fantastic way to save a little extra. They're often part of the best weekly deals, too.

While some offers might be a little different from shop to shop, we keep track of the big national deals on the Risij.uk website to give you a heads-up.
