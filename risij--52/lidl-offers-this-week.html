A Treasure Hunter's Guide to Lidl Offers This Week

Published on October 30, 2023 | Category: Smart Shopping

Shopping at Lidl is an experience, thanks in large part to the legendary "Middle of Lidl". This central aisle is home to a rotating selection of amazing bargains, from power tools to paddleboards. But the savings don't stop there; mastering the Lidl offers this week across the whole store is key to a successful shop.

Conquering the Middle of Lidl

The Middle of Lidl products are released every Thursday and Sunday. Just like Aldi's Specialbuys, these are limited-stock items. The best strategy is to check the Lidl website or app beforehand to see what's coming and plan your visit accordingly.

Lidl Savings Strategy:

  • Use the Lidl Plus App: This is non-negotiable for Lidl regulars. The app gives you exclusive weekly coupons, a digital scratch card for prizes, and a "Coupon Plus" feature that rewards you for spending thresholds.
  • Don't Miss the Themed Weeks: Lidl regularly hosts themed weeks (e.g., Italian Week, Greek Week) featuring special food and drink items that offer great quality and value.
  • Check the "Too Good to Waste" Boxes: Look for £1.50 boxes near the checkout containing fruit and vegetables that are still perfectly good but not cosmetically perfect.
  • Visit the Bakery: Lidl's in-store bakery offers fresh, delicious, and incredibly cheap bread and pastries.
The Lidl Plus app is the single most powerful tool for saving money at Lidl. Make sure you scan it every time.
