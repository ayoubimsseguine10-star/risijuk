How to Capitalise on Iceland's Offers This Week

Published on July 26, 2024 | Category: Shopping Tips

Iceland is a family favourite for its vast range of frozen foods, convenient meals, and great value deals. Shopping smart at Iceland means knowing how to spot the best promotions. This guide breaks down how to make the most of the Iceland offers this week.

Key Deals to Look For

Iceland's promotions are usually very clear and offer significant savings:

  • Multi-buy Deals: The most famous Iceland offer is often a "3 for £10" (or similar) on a huge range of frozen meats, fish, and ready meals. This is an excellent way to stock the freezer.
  • £1 Items: Iceland always has a selection of items for just £1, from frozen vegetables to ice cream and pizzas.
  • Big Brand Collaborations: Iceland partners with brands like TGI Fridays, Greggs, and Slimming World to offer exclusive frozen versions of popular products.

The Iceland Bonus Card Advantage

The Iceland Bonus Card is a fantastic loyalty scheme that helps you save even more:

  1. Get £1 for every £20 you save: For every £20 you load onto your Bonus Card, Iceland adds £1 extra. It's a free 5% bonus on your shopping.
  2. Exclusive Offers: Cardholders get access to special competitions and exclusive deals.
  3. Free Home Delivery: Get free home delivery when you spend £25 or more in-store, a great perk if you're doing a big freezer stock-up.

By combining the weekly multi-buy deals with the Bonus Card savings, you can make your money go much further. We always feature the best Iceland deals on Risij.uk to help you plan your shop.
