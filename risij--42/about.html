About This Project

We are the creators of Risij.uk, a platform designed to aggregate and simplify UK supermarket offers. This blog was established as a companion to that service, with a distinct and important objective: to elevate the discourse around grocery shopping from simple deal-hunting to informed decision-making.

Our Guiding Principle

The modern retail environment is intentionally complex. Loyalty schemes, price-matching initiatives, and rotating promotions create a landscape that can be difficult to navigate. Our purpose is to provide clarity. We analyze the weekly offers, from the Tesco offers this week to the Morrisons offers this week, with a critical eye.

This blog serves as our forum for sharing these insights. We do not merely list promotions; we examine their underlying mechanics and their practical implications for the consumer. Our goal is to equip you with the understanding necessary to derive maximum value from your weekly shop, whether you are evaluating the Costco UK offers or planning a family budget.

An Invitation

We consider our main site, Risij.uk, to be the source of market data. This blog is where that data is transformed into knowledge.

We appreciate your readership and invite you to engage with our analyses.
