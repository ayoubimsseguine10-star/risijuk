Iceland's Bonus Card: The Easiest Way to Save

Category: Loyalty Programmes

In a world of complicated loyalty schemes, the Iceland Bonus Card is refreshingly simple. It’s not about collecting points; it’s a savings tool that gives you a guaranteed bonus. Here’s how it works and why it’s a great way to save on the Iceland offers this week.

How the Bonus Card Works

The concept is incredibly straightforward. You add money to your Bonus Card to spend at Iceland later, and Iceland rewards you with a bonus for doing so.

The Key Benefit: A Guaranteed Bonus

For every £20 you load onto your card, Iceland gives you a £1 bonus. That’s a 5% return, which is a fantastic rate. You are essentially getting paid to budget for your groceries.

Other Perks for Cardholders:

  • Budgeting Made Easy: Use the card to set your grocery budget for the month. Load it up at the start and spend only what's on the card.
  • Exclusive Deals: Bonus Card holders get access to special member-only prices on certain products.
  • Free Delivery: When you shop online, using your Bonus Card often gets you free delivery on your order.

Combine the Bonus Card with Iceland's famous multi-buy deals (like "3 for £10" on frozen goods) for maximum savings. You can see all the current multi-buy promotions on the Iceland page on Risij.uk.
