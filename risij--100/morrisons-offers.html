Morrisons: Unlocking Market Street's Secret Value

Intel Drop: October 19, 2024 | Target: Traditional Grocery

The Market Street Advantage

Morrisons' "Market Street" fresh counters are their secret weapon, and most shoppers don't use them correctly. The price-per-kilo at the butcher and fishmonger is often cheaper than the pre-packaged stuff. Your mission is to check the Morrisons offers this week on Risij.uk to see which counter has the best deals, and build your meal plan around that.

Market Street Tactical Protocol:

  • Compare counter prices to packaged prices. The savings are often hiding in plain sight.
  • Talk to the staff. They know what's good and can give you exactly the amount you need, reducing waste.
  • Hit the store late. The end-of-day yellow sticker deals on fresh counter items are legendary.

The "My Morrisons" App: Your Personal Deal Engine

The "My Morrisons" app isn't about collecting points; it's about getting personalized price cuts sent directly to you. It learns what you buy and sends you money-off coupons for those exact items. The trick is to always check the app and activate your offers *before* you start shopping. Forgetting this one step is like leaving free money on the table.
