Your Guide to This Week's Co-op Offers and Member Savings

Posted on October 24, 2023 in Community Savings

Co-op Food stores are at the heart of many communities, offering convenience and a commitment to ethical sourcing. While often seen as a top-up shop, savvy shoppers know there's great value to be found in the Co-op offers this week, especially for members.

The Power of Co-op Membership

Becoming a Co-op member for just £1 is one of the best investments you can make. Members get access to exclusive pricing on a range of products, which is a key way to save. These member-only deals are clearly marked in-store and can offer substantial discounts compared to the non-member price.

Furthermore, every time you buy selected Co-op branded products and services, 2p for every £1 spent goes into your membership account, and Co-op gives the same to community organisations and local causes.

What to Look For This Week

  • Member Prices: Always scan the shelves for the blue member price signs. These are your biggest source of savings.
  • Meal Deals: Co-op often has excellent meal deals, such as their "Freezer Fillers" or pizza deals, which are perfect for a quick and easy dinner.
  • Wine Offers: Look out for promotions on wine and spirits, where buying multiple bottles can lead to big discounts.
