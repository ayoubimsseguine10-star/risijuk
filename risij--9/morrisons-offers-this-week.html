Unlocking Value with the Morrisons More Card

Published: October 28, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Programs

Morrisons sets itself apart with its "Market Street" concept, offering in-store butchers, bakers, and fishmongers. Their savings strategy is built around the "Morrisons More" loyalty program, which offers points, exclusive prices, and targeted offers. Let's break down the Morrisons offers this week.

How the Morrisons More Card Works

The More card is a hybrid system. It offers "More Card Exclusives" (instant discounts similar to Clubcard/Nectar Prices) and also allows you to earn points on selected products and fuel, which can be redeemed for vouchers.

Your Morrisons Savings Strategy

  • Activate App Offers: The Morrisons More app is your primary tool. You must activate personalized offers within the app before you shop to be eligible for the bonus points.
  • Look for "More Card Exclusives": These are instant price reductions at the shelf edge for members. They offer the most immediate and significant savings.
  • Capitalize on Market Street Deals: Keep an eye on promotions from the fresh food counters. These often represent the best quality and value in the store.
  • Fuel Points: If you use a Morrisons petrol station, the points earned on fuel can add up quickly, providing a regular stream of discount vouchers.

The Freshness Advantage

Morrisons' focus on fresh food provides a unique saving opportunity. By planning meals around the weekly Market Street offers, you can eat high-quality, fresh meals for less. This is a different approach to the pre-packaged value offered by stores like Farmfoods.
