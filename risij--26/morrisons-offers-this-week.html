How to Save with Morrisons Offers This Week

Published on: 24th July 2024 | Category: Shopping Strategy

Morrisons distinguishes itself with its unique "Market Street," featuring in-store butchers, bakers, and fishmongers. This focus on freshness, combined with a robust loyalty program, opens up multiple avenues for savings. Here’s your guide to leveraging the Morrisons offers this week.

Maximizing the Morrisons More Card

The "Morrisons More" loyalty program is a straightforward and powerful tool for getting extra value from your shopping. It provides a simple way to earn points and secure instant discounts.

Key Benefits of the More Card:

  • More Card Exclusives: Get instant price reductions at the checkout on hundreds of specially marked products just by scanning your card.
  • Points on Fuel & More: Collect points on fuel purchases and selected in-store products, which are then converted into money-off vouchers for your groceries.
  • Exclusive App Bonuses: Download the app to access personalized coupons and bonus point offers, helping you earn rewards even faster.

Find This Week's Top Deals! See all current promotions and More Card Exclusives on the Morrisons Offers This Week page on Risij.uk.

Discover Value on Market Street

The Market Street counters are a hidden gem for savvy shoppers. The expert staff can provide custom cuts of meat or specific fish fillets, which helps reduce food waste and can be more cost-effective than buying pre-packaged alternatives. Also, keep an eye out for "when it's gone, it's gone" (WIGIG) deals throughout the store for limited-time bargains.

Your Morrisons Savings Blueprint

Before you shop, browse the weekly offers online and check the Morrisons More app to activate any personalized coupons. Always scan your More card to get exclusive prices and earn points. A visit to the Market Street counters can also yield high-quality, fresh products at excellent prices, making your shop both delicious and economical.
