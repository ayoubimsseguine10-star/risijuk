Strategy Update: October 16, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Programs

Clubcard Prices: Your Instant Discount Code

Shopping at Tesco without a Clubcard is like leaving money on the table. "Clubcard Prices" give you huge discounts on hundreds of items, right at the till. It's no longer just about collecting points; it's about saving money on every single shop. At Risij.uk, we scan the Tesco offers this week to find the Clubcard deals that save you the most.

Your Clubcard Must-Do List:

  • Make scanning your Clubcard a habit. It's the most important step of your shop!
  • Use the Tesco app to get personalized coupons for extra savings on top.
  • Plan your meals around the best Clubcard Price offers to maximize your discount.

Double Your Money with Reward Partners

This is the ultimate Clubcard hack. Don't just spend your points in-store. Instead, exchange them for vouchers with Tesco's Reward Partners. You can often double their value, turning £5 in points into a £10 voucher for a restaurant, a day out, or even a holiday. It's the smartest way to use your points.
