A Simple Guide to Saving at Farmfoods

Category: Savings Guides | Published: October 2024

Farmfoods is a popular choice for stocking up on frozen goods and pantry staples. To get the best possible value, it's important to use their two main savings methods in combination: money-off vouchers and multi-buy deals.

The Two-Step Farmfoods Savings Method

The most effective way to shop at Farmfoods involves a simple, two-step process. The goal is to "stack" a general discount on top of their already competitive product offers.

Your Farmfoods Shopping Plan

  • Step 1: Get Vouchers from the Website. This is the most critical step. Before you go shopping, visit the Farmfoods website and sign up to receive their emails. They regularly send out printable money-off vouchers (e.g., £2 off a £25 spend).
  • Step 2: Focus on the Multi-Buy Offers. In-store, look for the prominent multi-buy deals, such as "3 for £10." Base your shop around these offers to maximize value. You can see the latest Farmfoods offers this week on our main site.
  • Look for Brand Names: Farmfoods stocks many popular brands like Birds Eye and McCain at lower prices than other supermarkets, especially on multi-buy deals.
  • Check the Dry Goods Aisles: In addition to frozen food, Farmfoods often has excellent deals on items like drinks, biscuits, and cleaning supplies.

Actionable Tip: Plan your shop in advance by checking the current multi-buy deals on the Farmfoods offers this week page on Risij.uk.

By using a money-off voucher on a shopping basket filled with multi-buy deals, you can achieve significant savings and stock your freezer for a fraction of the usual cost.
