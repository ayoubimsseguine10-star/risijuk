How to Maximize Savings with Morrisons Offers This Week

Published on October 31, 2023 | Category: Shopping Tips

Morrisons stands out with its "Market Street," featuring in-store butchers, bakers, and fishmongers. This focus on freshness, combined with a strong loyalty program, means there are plenty of ways to save. Let's dive into the best Morrisons offers this week.

Leveraging My Morrisons Rewards

The My Morrisons app is your gateway to savings. It provides personalised offers based on your shopping habits, as well as exclusive discounts and basket bonuses. It's a must-have for any regular Morrisons shopper.

Strategies for a Cheaper Morrisons Shop:

  • Activate Your App Offers: Before you shop, always open the My Morrisons app and activate your personalised offers.
  • Shop the Market Street Deals: Look for special promotions at the butcher, fishmonger, and bakery counters. These often provide better quality and value than pre-packaged alternatives.
  • Look for the Yellow Stickers: Morrisons is excellent for "reduced to clear" items, especially later in the day. You can find amazing bargains on products nearing their sell-by date.
  • Take Advantage of Fuel Savings: Morrisons frequently runs promotions where spending a certain amount in-store earns you a voucher for discounted fuel.
By combining app-based offers with the fresh deals on Market Street, you can significantly reduce your grocery bill at Morrisons.
