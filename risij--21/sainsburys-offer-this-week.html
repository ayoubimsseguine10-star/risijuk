A Simple Guide to Sainsbury's Offers This Week

Published: October 18, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

Sainsbury's is a favourite for its quality food, and with a Nectar card, it can be great value too. To get the best deals, you just need to know how to use their loyalty program. Here’s how to make the most of the Sainsbury's offers this week.

Nectar Prices are Everything

This is the golden rule of shopping at Sainsbury's: always, always scan your Nectar card. "Nectar Prices" are special lower prices on hundreds of items that you only get if you're a member. The savings can be huge, and the card is free, so it's a no-brainer!

Use the SmartShop App for Extra Deals

The Sainsbury's SmartShop app (or the handsets in store) is your next step to saving. The app gets to know what you like to buy and sends you personalized coupons for money off those items. It's a good idea to check the app for new deals before you start your shop.

Other Ways to Save

  • "Taste the Difference" Deals: Keep an eye out for special offers on Sainsbury's premium "Taste the Difference" range. It's a great chance to try something a bit fancy for less.
  • Clothing & Home Sales: The Tu clothing and homeware sections have big clearance sales at the end of each season, where you can find some real bargains.

By always using your Nectar card, you can be sure you're getting the best possible value on the Sainsbury's offers this week.
