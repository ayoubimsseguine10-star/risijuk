A Simple Guide to Aldi Offers Next Week

Published on Jan 10, 2025 | In Saving Guides

The famous Aldi "middle aisle" is where you'll find their amazing Specialbuys. But if you want to get the best deals, a little planning goes a long way. Here’s our simple guide to making the most of the Aldi offers for next week.

Know the Specialbuys Schedule

Aldi releases new Specialbuys every Thursday and Sunday. These are limited-stock items, so when they're gone, they're gone! Knowing this schedule is the first step to finding a great bargain.

Three Easy Tips for Aldi Shopping

  1. Check the Leaflet Online: Before you go, take a peek at the Aldi leaflet on their website or on Risij.uk. This lets you see what's coming up so you can decide if you want to make a special trip.
  2. Shop Early: If there's something you really want, try to go on Thursday or Sunday morning. This is the best way to get what you want before it sells out.
  3. Remember the Super 6: It's not just about the middle aisle! Aldi's "Super 6" deals on fruit and veg change every couple of weeks and are a fantastic way to save on healthy essentials.

With these simple tips, you can feel more prepared for your next Aldi trip and confident that you're getting the best value.
