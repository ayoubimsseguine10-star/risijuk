How to Find the Best Lidl Offers This Week

Published on November 15, 2024 | Category: Smart Shopping

Lidl is famous for its "Middle of Lidl," a central aisle of ever-changing bargains. However, the deepest savings come from combining these finds with the powerful Lidl Plus app. This guide covers the essential strategies for making the most of the Lidl offers this week.

Your Essential Lidl Toolkit

The Middle of Lidl products are released every Thursday and Sunday and are only available while stocks last. The best way to approach this is to know what's coming and to use the app for extra discounts on your grocery shop.

A 4-Point Strategy for Lidl:

  1. Use the Lidl Plus App Religiously: This is the most important tool. Activate your weekly coupons before you shop to get discounts on groceries. Scan the app at checkout to earn rewards.
  2. Preview the Middle Aisle Online: Check the Lidl website or app at the start of the week to see what non-food items will be available.
  3. Look for Themed Food Weeks: Lidl's special culinary weeks (e.g., Italian Week) are a great time to buy authentic, high-quality products at low prices.
  4. Check the In-Store Bakery: Lidl's bakery offers fresh, high-quality bread and pastries at a fraction of the price of traditional bakeries.

The Lidl Plus app is not just an add-on; it's the core of any effective Lidl savings strategy. Don't shop without it.
