How to Shop Smart at Waitrose This Week

Published on December 31, 2024 | Category: Quality Groceries

Waitrose is known for its high-quality food, but that doesn't mean you can't find great value there. By using the free myWaitrose loyalty card and looking for specific types of deals, you can enjoy premium groceries without overspending. The key is to shop strategically using the Waitrose offers this week.

Using Your myWaitrose Card

The myWaitrose card is the best tool for saving money at Waitrose. It's free to join and gives you personalised savings.

  • Personalised Vouchers: Every week, check your account for money-off vouchers based on what you normally buy.
  • Member-Only Prices: Just like other supermarkets, Waitrose offers special lower prices on certain items just for members.
  • Regular Perks: The program often includes ongoing deals, like 20% off the fish counter on Fridays, which provides consistent savings.

Other Ways to Find Value

Beyond the loyalty card, there are other smart ways to save at Waitrose.

  • 'Essential Waitrose' Range: This is their own-brand range of everyday items. The quality is excellent and the prices are very competitive.
  • 'Dine-In' Meal Deals: Waitrose often has a high-quality "Dine-In for Two" offer that includes a main, side, dessert, and a bottle of wine, which is great value.
  • Wine Case Discount: They regularly run a "25% off when you buy 6 bottles" deal on wine, which is the perfect time to stock up.
