Fresh Deals: Making the Most of Morrisons Market Street

Morrisons stands out from other supermarkets with its "Market Street" concept, featuring in-store butchers, bakers, and fishmongers. This focus on fresh food provides a unique opportunity for savings, especially when you combine it with the Morrisons offers this week.

Shopping Smart on Market Street

The fresh counters are where you can find high-quality products, often at a better price than their pre-packaged counterparts. You can buy exactly the amount you need, which helps to reduce food waste.

Tips for Market Street Savings:

  • Look for the "Ask Us" Signs: Staff at the counters can offer cooking advice and point you towards the best value cuts of the day.
  • End-of-Day Reductions: Visit later in the day to find freshly baked bread, cakes, and cooked meats with yellow reduction stickers.
  • Combine with Weekly Offers: Check the weekly flyer on Risij.uk to see which Market Street items are on promotion.

Beyond the Counters

Of course, Morrisons offers deals across the entire store. Their More Card loyalty scheme allows you to earn points on your shopping and on fuel. Always check the More Card exclusives for extra discounts. By planning your shop, you can easily see how Morrisons' deals stack up against the Sainsbury's offer this week for your family's needs.
