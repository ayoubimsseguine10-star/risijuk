A Simple Guide to ASDA Offers This Week

Published on January 4, 2025 | Category: Shopping Guides

ASDA is known for its good value, but smart shoppers know there's more to it than just the shelf price. To get the most out of the ASDA offers this week, you need to understand how their main promotions and rewards app work together.

What is a Rollback?

ASDA's "Rollback" is their most common type of deal. It simply means they have lowered the price on an item for an extended period. It’s not just a quick sale, so it's a great time to stock up on household essentials and pantry items you use regularly.

Simple Tips for Shopping at ASDA:

  • Check Multi-buys: Before buying a "2 for £5" deal, quickly check the price of a single item to make sure you're actually saving.
  • Look for Yellow Stickers: If you see a yellow "Smiley" sticker, it means the item is on clearance or has a significant discount.
  • Don't Forget George: The George section often has great deals on clothing and home goods that are separate from the main grocery offers.

How the ASDA Rewards App Works

If you shop at ASDA often, the ASDA Rewards app is a must-have. It lets you earn money back in a "cashpot" when you buy special "Star Products" or complete challenges in the app. It's an easy way to get a little money back on your regular shopping.
