Simple Ways to Boost Your Nectar Points

Published: Oct 17, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Guides

The Nectar card is a fantastic little helper for saving money. But are you getting the most out of it? This guide will show you some easy ways to collect points faster with the Sainsbury's offers this week.

The Most Important Thing: Nectar Prices

Sainsbury's now has special "Nectar Prices" on lots of items, which means you get an instant discount just for being a member. This is the number one reason to make sure you scan your Nectar card every single time you shop!

Easy Boost #1: Check Your Nectar App

Your Nectar app is like a personal treasure chest of bonus points. Each week, you'll find special offers just for you. All you have to do is tap to 'save' the offers before you shop, and you'll collect extra points without any effort.

Easy Boost #2: Watch for "Double Up" Events

A couple of times a year, Sainsbury's runs a "Double Up" event. This is your chance to make your points worth twice as much! You can swap them for vouchers to spend on things like clothes, toys, or festive treats. Saving your points for these events is a brilliant strategy.

The Smart Shopper's Trick: Stacking Deals

Here's a clever trick: look for an item that has a Nectar Price discount AND a bonus points offer in your app at the same time. This way, you save money instantly and get a bundle of extra points too. It’s a bit more involved than just grabbing one of the ASDA offers this week, but it's so rewarding when you get the hang of it!

Top Tip: Get into the habit of checking your Nectar app for a few minutes while you're making your shopping list. It's the easiest way to find bonus points.
