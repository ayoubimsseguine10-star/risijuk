Your Aldi Specialbuys Cheat Sheet

Published: Oct 26, 2024 | Category: Shopping Guides

The Aldi "Specialbuys" aisle is famous for a reason—it's full of amazing and unexpected finds! But it can also be a bit overwhelming. This simple cheat sheet will help you navigate the Aldi offers next week like a pro.

So, What Are Specialbuys?

Think of them as a weekly surprise. Every Thursday and Sunday, Aldi stores get a limited amount of fantastic non-food items, from garden furniture to kitchen gadgets. The most important thing to remember is that once they're sold out, they're gone! That's what makes it so exciting.

Tip #1: Do a Little Homework

The secret to a successful Aldi trip is knowing what's coming. You can check the leaflet in-store or look online a few days before. Pick out what you'd like to find, and have a budget in mind. It's a great habit to get into, just like when you're checking out the Lidl offers this week.

Tip #2: Thursday is the Golden Day

If there's something you really want, especially a big-ticket item, Thursday morning is the best time to go. The most popular Specialbuys can disappear in just a few hours. Waiting until the weekend might be too late for the best stuff!

Don't Forget the "Super 6"!

While you're on the hunt for a bargain, make sure to check out the "Super 6" deals on fruit and veg. It's an easy way to get healthy, fresh food for less, often at prices that are hard to beat, even by the ASDA offers this week.

Top Tip: If you see a Specialbuy you like, grab it! Don't assume it will be there when you come back.
