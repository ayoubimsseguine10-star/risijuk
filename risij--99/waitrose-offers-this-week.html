A Guide to Finding Value at Waitrose

Published on Jan 21, 2025 | In Savings Guides

Waitrose is known for its wonderful quality, and with a few simple tips, you can enjoy it for less than you might think! The myWaitrose card is the key to unlocking some lovely savings. Here is our friendly guide to the Waitrose offers this week.

Your Best Friend at Waitrose: The myWaitrose Card

The myWaitrose card is free to join and is an essential for any Waitrose shopper. It helps you save in a couple of great ways:

  • Choose Your Own Vouchers: This is a brilliant feature! Every week, the app lets you choose two personalised vouchers for money off things you actually buy, like "20% off all fruit." It's a good idea to choose these before you do your shopping.
  • Special Member Prices: As you're walking around the store, look for the green "myWaitrose price" signs. These are special deals just for members that give you an instant discount.

More Friendly Tips for Your Waitrose Shop

  1. Look for the "Dine In" Deal: Waitrose often has a "Dine In" offer which includes a main, a side, a dessert, and a bottle of wine for a set price. It can be a lovely and affordable treat.
  2. Explore the Essentials Range: The "Essential Waitrose" brand is their own range of everyday items. It's a fantastic way to get that Waitrose quality on your basics for a much more competitive price.
  3. Check the Reduced Section: Like any supermarket, Waitrose reduces items that are near their use-by date. You can often find some amazing bargains here, especially on high-quality ready meals and fresh meat.
