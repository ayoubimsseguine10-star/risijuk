Your Guide to Farmfoods Offers and Vouchers This Week

Published on July 17, 2024 in Supermarket Guides

Farmfoods is a haven for bargain hunters, specializing in frozen food but also offering a great range of groceries, chilled items, and household goods. To get the absolute best value, you need to combine their already low prices with the right Farmfoods offers this week and their famous money-off vouchers.

The All-Important Farmfoods Vouchers

The key to unlocking the biggest savings at Farmfoods is to use their discount vouchers. These are typically:

  • £2.50 off a £25 spend
  • £5 off a £50 spend

To get these, you need to sign up for their mailing list on the Farmfoods website. They will email you new vouchers periodically, which you can print at home or simply show on your phone at the checkout.

Strategy: Hitting the Spend Threshold

Plan your shop to ensure your total comes to just over £25 or £50 to get the maximum benefit from the voucher. This is where their multi-buy and bulk deals come in handy.

Top Deals to Look For

Farmfoods is built on a model of "case saving" deals and big-brand bargains.

  1. Multi-buy Offers: Look for deals like "3 for £10" on branded frozen items like pizzas, ready meals, and desserts. These are great for stocking up.
  2. Big Bags and Bulk Packs: You can often find huge bags of frozen chicken, chips, and vegetables that offer far better value per kilogram than standard supermarkets.
  3. Branded Groceries: Check the ambient aisles for amazing prices on big brands of cereal, coffee, sauces, and tinned goods. These are often clearance items, so the stock changes regularly.
