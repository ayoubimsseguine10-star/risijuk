Get More from Morrisons: Your Guide to This Week's Offers

Published on October 09, 2024 in Supermarket Guides

Morrisons distinguishes itself with its unique "Market Street," which features in-store butchers, bakers, and fishmongers. This dedication to freshness, combined with a powerful loyalty program, opens up multiple avenues for savings. Let's dive into the Morrisons offers this week.

Unleashing the Power of the Morrisons More Card

The Morrisons More card is an essential tool for any regular shopper. While it's a points-based system that rewards you on selected products and fuel, its true power lies in personalized, app-based offers.

Effective More Card Tactics

  • Activate Before Shopping: Always check the Morrisons app or website before you leave. Activate personalized offers based on your shopping history to earn extra points or get direct discounts.
  • Scan Every Time: Make it a non-negotiable habit to scan your card (physical or digital) at every checkout to collect points and redeem your activated offers.
  • Bank Your Fivers: Once you accumulate 5,000 points, you'll be rewarded with a "Morrisons Fiver" (£5 voucher) to slash the cost of a future shop.

The Market Street Advantage

Market Street is where you'll often find the freshest products and some of the best deals in the entire store.

  1. Butcher's Bundles: Keep an eye out for multi-buy deals like "3 for £10" on fresh chicken, mince, sausages, and other expertly prepared butcher's items.
  2. Fishmonger's Catch of the Day: Many stores feature special prices on fresh fish and seafood, particularly on Fridays.
  3. Bakery Delights: The in-store bakery frequently has promotions on fresh loaves and rolls, with prices often dropping further towards the end of the day.

Fight Waste, Maximize Value with "Magic Box"

For a truly exceptional bargain, check if your local Morrisons participates in the "Too Good To Go" app. You can reserve a "Magic Box" filled with surplus food from the store at a massive discount. It's a fantastic way to save money while helping to combat food waste.
