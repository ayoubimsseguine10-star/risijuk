A Simple Guide to Lidl Offers This Week

Published on Jan 16, 2025 | In Saving Guides

Shopping at Lidl is an adventure! Between the great food deals and the surprising finds in the "Middle of Lidl," there's always a bargain to be found. This guide will help you make the most of the Lidl offers this week.

Use the Lidl Plus App

The Lidl Plus app on your phone is a must-have for saving money. Here are a few simple tips:

  • Activate Your Coupons: Before you shop, open the app and tap to "activate" your weekly coupons. You need to do this for the discounts to work at the till.
  • Earn More Coupons: The more you spend during the month, the more free coupons the app gives you. It's a nice little bonus!
  • Don't Forget to Scan: Always scan your app at the checkout to get your discounts and track your spending for rewards.

Explore the "Middle of Lidl"

The middle aisle is where Lidl puts all its non-food special offers, with new things arriving every Thursday and Sunday.

  1. Check the Leaflet First: See what's coming up by checking the weekly leaflet on Risij.uk. This will tell you if they have a theme you're interested in, like gardening or DIY.
  2. Shop Early for Popular Items: If you see something you really want, it's best to go on the day it's released (Thursday or Sunday) as popular items can sell out fast.

By using the app for your food shop and planning your trips for the middle aisle, you'll become a Lidl savings pro in no time!
