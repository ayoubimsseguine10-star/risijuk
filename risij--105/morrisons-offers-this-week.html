Leveraging the My Morrisons Digital Platform

How to harness personalized, data-driven offers for maximum advantage.

While Morrisons maintains traditional strengths with its in-store market-style counters, its savings strategy has pivoted to a digital-first approach. Unlocking the full value of the Morrisons offers this week is contingent on adept use of the My Morrisons application.

Operational Mechanics of the My Morrisons App

The platform has abandoned a traditional points-based system in favour of direct, personalized discounts. The user workflow is designed for efficiency:

  • Pre-Shop Offer Review: The application presents a list of discounts tailored to the user's purchasing history. A review of these offers prior to shopping is essential.
  • Activation and Redemption: Users must manually activate desired offers within the app. A scan of the digital card at the point of sale automatically applies the selected discounts.
  • Stochastic Rewards: The system incorporates a "Basket Bonus" feature, which may randomly award a significant discount for a future transaction upon completion of a shop.

Ancillary In-Store Promotions

The digital strategy is complemented by conventional in-store multi-buys and periodic "Price Lock" campaigns, which fix the cost of hundreds of staple goods for a set duration, aiding in budgetary forecasting.

This personalized, app-centric model contrasts sharply with the universal, card-based discount systems employed by competitors, such as the one underpinning the Sainsbury's offer this week.
