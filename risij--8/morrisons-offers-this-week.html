How to Maximize the Morrisons Offers This Week

Published on: October 19, 2024 | Category: Supermarket Deals

Morrisons stands out with its "Market Street," offering fresh produce, meat, and fish from dedicated counters. But beyond that, there are plenty of ways to save. Let’s look at the best Morrisons offers this week.

The Morrisons More Card

The recently revamped Morrisons More card is now a points-based system. You earn points on selected products and fuel. Once you reach 5,000 points, you get a £5 voucher ('a Fiver') to spend on your next shop. The app also gives you access to exclusive lower prices, similar to other supermarket loyalty schemes.

Look for the Yellow Labels

Morrisons is excellent for "yellow sticker" reductions. These are items nearing their sell-by date, offered at a significant discount. The best time to find these is usually in the evening. This is a great way to find bargains on meat, ready meals, and bakery items.

Dine In For Two Deals

A popular promotion at Morrisons is their meal deal, often branded as "The Best Dine in for Two" for around £12-£15. This typically includes a main, a side, a dessert, and a drink (including wine), offering fantastic value for a high-quality meal at home.
