Maximizing Savings with Tesco Offers This Week

Published on July 12, 2024 in Supermarket Guides

Tesco is a giant of UK grocery retail, and for savvy shoppers, that means a huge variety of offers. The single most important tool in your savings arsenal is the Tesco Clubcard, which unlocks exclusive prices on hundreds of products every single week. Let's explore the Tesco offers this week.

The Power of Clubcard Prices

If you shop at Tesco, having a Clubcard is not optional—it's essential. "Clubcard Prices" are special, lower prices on items throughout the store, exclusively for Clubcard holders. The difference between the regular price and the Clubcard price can be substantial.

How to Make the Most of Your Clubcard

  • Scan Every Time: Whether you use the physical card, the app, or key fob, make sure you scan it with every shop to get the instant discounts.
  • Check Your App: The Tesco Grocery & Clubcard app often has personalised coupons for money off your total shop or bonus points on certain items.
  • Collect Points: For every £1 you spend, you earn 1 point. These points are converted into vouchers that can be spent at Tesco or with Reward Partners.

Beyond Clubcard Prices: Other Ways to Save

While Clubcard Prices are the main event, don't forget these other saving strategies.

  1. Meal Deals: Tesco's £3.90 meal deal (with a Clubcard) is a lunchtime institution, offering a main, a snack, and a drink for a set price.
  2. Reduced to Clear Section: Head to the chilled aisles in the evening to find the yellow-sticker reductions on items nearing their expiry date. This is a great way to find bargains.
  3. Aldi Price Match: Tesco matches Aldi's prices on hundreds of basic products, ensuring you get great value on your everyday essentials.

Clubcard Reward Partners

The best way to use your Clubcard vouchers is often with Reward Partners. You can exchange your vouchers for up to double their value with partners for things like restaurant meals, cinema tickets, and days out, stretching your savings even further.
