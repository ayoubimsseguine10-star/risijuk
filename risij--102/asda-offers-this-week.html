ASDA Savings: Rollbacks vs. Rewards

Category: Shopping Tips

ASDA has two main ways to help you save: the classic 'Rollback' and the modern ASDA Rewards app. Understanding how each one works is key to getting the best value from the ASDA offers this week.

What is a 'Rollback'?

A Rollback is a straightforward price cut. ASDA lowers the price of a product for an extended period, making it cheaper than its usual price. These are great opportunities to stock up on household staples or your favourite branded goods.

  • Look for: The prominent green 'Rollback' signs in-store.
  • Best for: Stocking up on non-perishables and frozen foods.

How Does the ASDA Rewards App Work?

The ASDA Rewards app is a cashback system. Instead of getting a discount at the till, you build up a 'Cashpot' of money that you can spend on a future shop. You earn money in two main ways:

  • Star Products: Buy specific items marked as 'Star Products' to get a percentage of their value (usually 10%) back in your Cashpot.
  • Missions: Complete simple tasks, like "buy 5 pieces of fruit," to earn a cash bonus.

The best strategy is to look for a product that is both on Rollback and is also a Star Product. This way, you save instantly and earn cashback at the same time. Check the full list of deals on Risij.uk to spot these double-win opportunities.
