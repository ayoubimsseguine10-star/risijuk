Unlocking Extra Savings: A Guide to Iceland's Bonus Card

Published on July 29, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Programs

Iceland is a haven for frozen food fans, offering incredible value and a huge range of products. While their headline deals are always tempting, the real key to maximizing savings is the Iceland Bonus Card. Let's look at how it works alongside the Iceland offers this week.

How the Iceland Bonus Card Works

The Bonus Card is a savings card with a twist. For every £20 you load onto the card, Iceland gives you a £1 bonus. That's an instant 5% return, which is better than any cashback scheme out there. You can then spend the money in-store or online whenever you like.

Strategies for Maximum Benefit

  1. Budget and Load: If you know you'll be doing a big shop, load the money onto your Bonus Card first to get the reward.
  2. Combine with Weekly Deals: The bonus is on top of any existing offers. Use your loaded card to pay for the famous "3 for £10" deals to stack your savings.
  3. Free Delivery: The Bonus Card also gets you free home delivery on in-store shops over £25.
  4. Exclusive Perks: Cardholders often get entered into special prize draws and receive exclusive treats on their birthday.

What to Buy at Iceland

Beyond the obvious frozen pizzas and vegetables, look out for their exclusive ranges from brands like TGI Fridays and Greggs. Comparing the price of these items to their restaurant counterparts shows the massive value. Also, check out their fresh and dry goods aisle—it's bigger than you might think and often has prices competitive with Farmfoods.
