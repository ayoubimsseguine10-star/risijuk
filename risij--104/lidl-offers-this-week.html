A Simple Guide to Lidl's Weekly Deals

Published: July 29, 2024 | Category: Shopping Tips

Shopping at Lidl can save you a lot of money, but their weekly offers can be a bit confusing. This simple guide breaks down the Lidl offers this week so you know exactly where to find the best value.

The Two Main Types of Offers

Every week, Lidl's deals fall into two main categories:

  • The "Middle of Lidl": This is the famous middle aisle where you'll find a random mix of non-food items like DIY tools, kitchenware, or clothing. These are limited-time offers and are great for finding bargains.
  • Weekly Food Themes: Lidl also runs food-related themes each week, like "Italian Week" or "BBQ Week." This is a good chance to try new products that aren't usually in stock.

Three Tips to Save More at Lidl

To get the most out of your shop, follow these simple tips:

  1. Use the Lidl Plus App: This is the most important tip. The app gives you exclusive digital coupons that you won't find anywhere else. Always scan your app at the checkout.
  2. Check the Weekly Leaflet: Pick up a leaflet in-store or view it online. It tells you everything that's coming up so you can plan your shopping list in advance.
  3. Don't Forget the Bakery: The in-store bakery often has great prices on fresh bread, pastries, and other baked goods. It’s an easy way to add value to your shop.
