How to Save at Co-op This Week

Published on January 3, 2025 | Category: Store Guides

Your local Co-op is perfect for convenience, but it can also be a great place to save money, especially if you're a member. Understanding the Co-op offers this week can help you get great value on your top-up shops.

Why Co-op Membership is Worth It

For just £1, becoming a Co-op member gives you access to special member-only prices and personalised deals in the app. It's the easiest way to save money every time you shop there.

  • Member Prices: Look for the blue shelf tickets to get instant discounts on everyday items.
  • App-Only Deals: The Co-op app gives you weekly offers that are chosen just for you.
  • Support Your Community: A portion of what you spend on Co-op own-brand products goes to local causes.

Key Deals to Look For

Co-op runs some great regular promotions that are perfect for a quick and easy meal, or for stocking up the freezer.

Common Co-op Offers:

  • Freezer Deals: Often you'll find a "5 for £5" deal on popular frozen foods like pizzas, chips, and ice cream.
  • Dinner Deals: Look for their meal bundles, which might include a pizza and a drink for a set price, making for an affordable dinner.
  • Wine and Beer Offers: Co-op frequently has multi-buy deals on alcohol, like "2 for £12" on wines.
