Plan Your Attack: A Guide to Aldi Offers Next Week

Published on July 28, 2024 | Category: Shopping Strategy

Success at Aldi is all about preparation. The most dedicated shoppers know that the key to unlocking the best deals lies in checking the Aldi offers next week before anyone else. This guide gives you the intel to stay one step ahead.

Decoding the Specialbuys Calendar

Aldi's legendary Specialbuys are limited-edition items released every Thursday and Sunday. They cover a vast range of products, from garden furniture to high-tech kitchen gadgets. Because stock is finite, the most popular items can sell out in hours.

Your Intelligence Sources:

  1. The Aldi Leaflet: The classic paper leaflet in-store (or online) reveals the upcoming week's treasures.
  2. Risij.uk: We curate and display the upcoming offers clearly on our Aldi page, saving you time.
  3. Aldi's Website & App: Their "Coming Soon" section provides a digital preview, and some items can even be pre-ordered online.

The "Super 6": Your Fresh Food Win

Every two weeks, Aldi discounts a selection of six fruits and vegetables to rock-bottom prices. This "Super 6" is your ticket to affordable, healthy eating. A smart shopper plans their meals around these deals to maximize savings.

Pro-Level Aldi Tactics

  • Calendar Alerts: If a high-demand Specialbuy is on your list, set a calendar alert for Thursday or Sunday morning.
  • Be Prepared to Pivot: If an item is sold out, don't despair. Sometimes stock is replenished, or you might find it at another nearby store.
  • Online First: For big-ticket items like furniture, check Aldi's website. They often release them online before they appear in the physical middle aisle.
