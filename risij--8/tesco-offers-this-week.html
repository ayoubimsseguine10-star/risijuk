How to Navigate the Best Tesco Offers This Week

Published on: October 16, 2024 | Category: Supermarket Deals

Tesco is the UK's largest supermarket, and its loyalty scheme, the Clubcard, is a powerhouse for savings. To get the best value, you absolutely need to be a member. Let's look at how to find the top Tesco offers this week.

The Importance of Clubcard Prices

The single biggest tip for saving at Tesco is to use a Clubcard. "Clubcard Prices" offer significant, instant discounts on hundreds of items across the store. The difference between the regular price and the Clubcard price can be substantial. If you shop at Tesco, having a Clubcard (which is free) is a no-brainer.

Using Clubcard Vouchers

For every pound you spend, you earn one Clubcard point. Every 150 points are worth £1.50 in vouchers, which are sent out quarterly. You can spend these on your shopping or, for better value, exchange them with Tesco's Reward Partners for up to double their value on things like restaurant meals, cinema tickets, and days out.

Weekly Deals and Multi-buys

Even beyond Clubcard Prices, Tesco runs weekly promotions and multi-buy offers. Look out for their popular "Dine In" meal deals, which offer great value for a complete meal for two. The "Low Everyday Prices" range also provides consistent value on hundreds of staple grocery items.
