A Simple Guide to This Week's Deals at Lidl

Published on July 29, 2024 | Category: Shopping Guides

Shopping at Lidl is a great way to save, but there's more to it than just low prices. The Lidl offers this week include everything from surprising finds in the middle aisle to special food themes. This simple guide will help you get the most out of your next Lidl shop.

Don't Miss the "Middle of Lidl"

The middle aisle is where Lidl places its limited-time non-food items. You can find anything from kitchen gadgets to clothing and DIY tools. These change twice a week (on Thursdays and Sundays), and the best stuff goes quickly, so it's always worth a look.

Check Out This Week's Food Theme

Lidl often has weekly food themes, like "Italian Week" or "Greek Week." It's a fantastic chance to try new ingredients and dishes without spending a lot. Check the weekly leaflet online to see what the theme is before you go, so you can plan some meals around it.

Top Tips for Shopping at Lidl

  1. Use the Lidl Plus App: This is a must-have. The app gives you exclusive digital coupons that you can use on top of the regular offers. You just scan your phone at the checkout.
  2. Visit the Bakery: The in-store bakery has fresh bread, pastries, and treats. For the best selection, go in the morning. For the best prices, check for discounts on leftover items late in the day.
  3. Shop on Thursdays & Sundays: These are the days the new "Middle of Lidl" items are put out. If there's something you really want, go early on these days to avoid disappointment.
