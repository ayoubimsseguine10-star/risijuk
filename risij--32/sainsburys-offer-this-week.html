Your Guide to Nectar Prices

Updated: November 05, 2024 | Category: Shopping Guides

Sainsbury's is well-known for its quality, and with the introduction of Nectar Prices, it's now easier than ever to save money there. The Sainsbury's offer this week is all about getting more value with your Nectar card.

How Nectar Prices Work

Your Nectar card is no longer just for collecting points – it's your key to getting instant discounts at the till. Nectar Prices are special lower prices on hundreds of items, exclusively for members.

How to Get the Deals:

  • Look for the Nectar Price Tags: Keep an eye out for the special purple tags on the shelves.
  • Scan Your Card or App: Simply scan your Nectar card or the barcode in your app when you pay, and the discount will be applied automatically.
  • You Still Collect Points: The best part is, you still collect your usual Nectar points on what you spend, even at the lower price!

We find the very best Nectar Prices each week and list them on our Sainsbury's offers page at Risij.uk to help you plan.

Other Ways to Save at Sainsbury's

1. Look for the Aldi Price Match

Sainsbury's matches the price of hundreds of everyday items to Aldi. When you see the "Aldi Price Match" sign, you know you're getting a competitive low price on that product.

2. "Taste the Difference" Offers

Sainsbury's often has great deals on its premium "Taste the Difference" range. This is a perfect opportunity to treat yourself to something special for less.
