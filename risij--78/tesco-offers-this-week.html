The Golden Rule of Shopping at Tesco

Published: October 16, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

Shopping at Tesco has one simple, golden rule: always use a Clubcard. The loyalty scheme has transformed into an instant discount tool that can save you a significant amount on every shop. This guide explains how to make the most of the Tesco offers this week.

Clubcard Prices: Your Key to Savings

The single most important feature of the Clubcard is "Clubcard Prices." Forget about just collecting points for later; this is about saving money right now, at the till.

  • How it Works: Hundreds of products throughout the store have two prices displayed on the shelf label. The higher price is for everyone else. The lower, yellow-highlighted price is for Clubcard holders.
  • The Savings Add Up: The discounts can be substantial—it's not uncommon to save 30-50% on branded items, fresh produce, and household essentials just by scanning your card.
  • Always Scan: Make it a habit to scan your Clubcard (or the app) for every purchase, no matter how small. Even a single item in a meal deal can have a Clubcard Price.

Use the App for Convenience

The easiest way to carry your Clubcard is on your phone. The Tesco Grocery & Clubcard app displays your card for easy scanning, lets you check your points, and even allows you to turn your points balance into vouchers to spend in-store.

What About the Points?

While instant discounts are the main attraction, you do still collect points on your spending. You can convert these into vouchers for money off your shopping or exchange them with Tesco's Reward Partners, where they can be worth up to double their value for things like restaurant meals or family days out.
