Sainsbury's Nectar: A Quick Start Guide

Category: Loyalty Programmes

The Nectar card is one of the UK's most popular loyalty schemes, but many people don't use it to its full potential. By taking a few simple steps, you can earn points much faster and get more value from the Sainsbury's offer this week.

1. Get Instant Savings with Nectar Prices

This is the easiest way to save. Just like other supermarkets, Sainsbury's offers lower prices on many items just for Nectar members. Look for the 'Nectar Price' shelf tags and make sure you scan your card at the checkout to get the discount.

2. Boost Your Points with the Nectar App

The Nectar app is your key to earning points faster. Before you shop, open the app to find a list of personalised bonus offers. These might give you extra points for buying certain products. Just tap to 'save' the offers you want before you pay.

3. Collect Points Beyond Sainsbury's

A big advantage of Nectar is that you can collect points at hundreds of other places, not just Sainsbury's. You can link your Nectar card to your accounts at places like:

  • eBay
  • Argos
  • Esso petrol stations
  • And many more online retailers.

By collecting points across different retailers, your balance will grow much more quickly. Always check the latest Nectar Prices on the main Sainsbury's page on Risij.uk to plan your shop.
