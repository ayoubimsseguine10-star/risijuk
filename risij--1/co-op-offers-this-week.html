Saving Locally: A Guide to Co-op Offers This Week

Published on: 24th July 2024 | Category: Supermarket Deals

The Co-op is more than just a convenience store; it's a community hub with a surprising number of ways to save. Whether you're popping in for a pint of milk or doing a full shop, knowing about the latest Co-op offers this week can make a real difference.

Unlock Savings with a Co-op Membership

The single best way to save at the Co-op is by becoming a member. For just £1, you gain access to a range of benefits that easily outweigh the cost.

Membership Perks:

  • Member Prices: Look for the blue signs in-store for exclusive lower prices on dozens of everyday items.
  • Personalised Offers: Choose two new offers each week through the Co-op App for products you actually buy.
  • Community Support: 2p for every £1 spent on own-brand products goes to your chosen local cause.

See the Deals! Find a list of the current member prices and other promotions on the Co-op Offers This Week page on Risij.uk.

Spotlight on Meal Deals and Freezer Fillers

The Co-op is famous for its excellent value meal deals. The "£6 Pizza Night" deal (two pizzas and a drink) and their "Freezer Fillers" bundles are consistent favourites, offering a complete meal or a stocked freezer for a set, low price. These are perfect for easy weeknight dinners.

Your Co-op Shopping Strategy

To maximize savings, always scan your membership card. Before you go, check the Co-op App to activate your two weekly personalised offers. Plan a few meals around the member prices and look out for their ever-popular meal deals. By shopping smart, your local Co-op can be a source of great value and convenience.
