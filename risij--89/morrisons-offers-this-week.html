A Shopper's Guide to Morrisons Offers This Week

Morrisons offers a unique shopping experience with its in-store "Market Street," featuring fresh food from butchers, bakers, and fishmongers. To get the best value, you need to combine these fresh deals with the Morrisons offers this week and the My Morrisons app.

How to Save Money at Morrisons

Morrisons provides several ways to cut your spending. Here are the most important ones:

  • The My Morrisons App: This is your key to personalized savings. The app sends you exclusive money-off coupons based on what you usually buy. The more you use it, the better the deals get.
  • Market Street Deals: The fresh food counters often have their own special offers. Keep an eye out for daily deals on meat, fish, and baked goods.
  • Weekly Promotions: Morrisons runs regular offers across the store, from half-price sales to big multi-buy deals on popular brands.

Your Morrisons Shopping Checklist

Follow these simple tips for a cheaper shop:

  1. Check the Offers Before You Go: Visit our Morrisons offers page on Risij.uk to see a clear list of the week's best promotions.
  2. Activate Your App Coupons: Don't forget to check the My Morrisons app and activate your personalized coupons before you start shopping.
  3. Look for "Too Good To Go" Boxes: Morrisons partners with the Too Good To Go app to sell "Magic Boxes" of surplus food at a huge discount. It's a great way to save money and reduce food waste.
