Your Guide to Aldi's Famous Specialbuys

Updated: November 05, 2024 | Category: Shopping Guides

Shopping at Aldi is an adventure, especially when it comes to their legendary 'Specialbuys'. To get the best stuff, it pays to be prepared. Looking at the Aldi offers next week in advance is the secret to success. Here’s our simple guide for 2025.

Step 1: Do a Little Homework

Aldi's Specialbuys go on sale every Thursday and Sunday, and the best items can sell out fast. The good news is that you can see what's coming ahead of time in their online leaflet.

Our Top Tip:

We check the leaflet every week and pull out the highlights on our Aldi offers page at Risij.uk. Have a quick look before you go to make a wishlist of the items you really want.

Step 2: Shopping Day Strategy

  • Be an Early Bird: If there’s a popular item you have your heart set on, getting to the store early on Thursday or Sunday morning is your best bet.
  • Head to the Middle Aisle: This is where you'll find all the Specialbuys. It's a good idea to grab what you want from there first before you start your regular grocery shop.
  • Stick to Your List: It's easy to get carried away in the middle aisle! Having a list helps you stay focused on what you came for and keeps your budget on track.
