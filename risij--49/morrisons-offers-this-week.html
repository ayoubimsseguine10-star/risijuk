Morrisons' Fresh Deals: A Guide to Market Street & More

Published on: October 20, 2024 | Category: Saving Strategies

Morrisons stands out for its "Market Street," a collection of in-store counters including a butcher, a fishmonger, and a bakery. This focus on fresh food provides unique opportunities for savings. Let's look at the Morrisons offers this week.

The Value of Market Street

Unlike many other supermarkets, Morrisons prepares a lot of food fresh in-store. This means you can often find great end-of-day reductions on bread, meat, and fish. It also allows you to buy exactly the amount you need, reducing food waste.

Tip 1: Look for End-of-Day Reductions

Visit in the late afternoon or evening to find yellow-sticker bargains on items that are nearing their sell-by date. This is especially true for fresh items from the Market Street counters. This tactic can yield better quality fresh produce than waiting for weekly deals at other stores.

Tip 2: Use the My Morrisons App

The "My Morrisons" loyalty scheme provides personalised offers and discounts directly to your app. It's a simple way to get money off your shopping without needing to collect points. Always check the app before you head to the store. This is similar to how the Co-op offers this week are tailored to members.

Seasonal Events and Multi-buys

Morrisons runs excellent seasonal promotions and multi-buy offers, particularly on drinks and party food around holidays. Keep an eye on their "2 for £X" deals on fresh produce and meats, which can be great value if you plan your meals accordingly. Always compare them to deals like the Sainsbury's offers this week to ensure you're getting the best price.
