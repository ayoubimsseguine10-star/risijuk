Costco: Your Guide to Bulk Buying

Updated: November 05, 2025 | Category: Savings Guides

Costco isn't your average supermarket—it's a warehouse where buying in bulk can lead to big savings. But to get the most out of it, you need a plan. The Costco UK offers are amazing, but only if you shop smart.

Is a Costco Membership Worth It?

The yearly membership fee is the first thing to consider. It's likely to be a great investment for you if:

  • You have a large family: The more mouths you have to feed, the more you'll save by buying in bulk.
  • You have storage space: You'll need a pantry or a chest freezer to store all your brilliant bulk buys.
  • You can stick to a list: It's easy to get carried away! Knowing what you need before you go is key to saving money.
  • You buy a lot of petrol: Costco's fuel prices are often much cheaper than regular petrol stations, which can save you a lot.

Tips for Finding the Best Deals

1. Read the Coupon Book

Costco's "Coupon Book" is sent to members every month and shows you all the upcoming discounts. It's worth looking through it before you visit. We pick out the best deals on our Costco offers page at Risij.uk to make it even easier for you.

2. Look for the Star (*)!

If you see a little star (*) on the price tag, it means the item won't be restocked. If it's something you love, now's the time to grab it before it's gone!

3. Don't Forget Kirkland Signature

Kirkland Signature is Costco's own brand, and it's fantastic. The quality is often as good as (or even better than) big-name brands, but the price is much lower. Always check for a Kirkland version of what you need.
