A Smart Shopper's Guide to Lidl Deals This Week

Lidl has become a favourite for millions of shoppers, thanks to its amazing value and the exciting "Middle of Lidl" aisle. To get the most from every visit, you need a smart approach to the Lidl offers this week. This guide will show you how.

Lidl's Weekly Savings Events

Lidl's deals are predictable and fun. Here's what to look for:

  • The Middle of Lidl: Every Thursday and Sunday, the famous middle aisles are restocked with a new range of limited-edition products, from kitchen gadgets to DIY tools. Get there early for the best finds!
  • Themed Food Weeks: Each week, Lidl celebrates a different country's cuisine, like Italian or Greek week. It's a great chance to try new foods at a low price.
  • The Lidl Plus App: This app is your ticket to extra savings. It gives you exclusive coupons, a digital receipt, and a fun "scratch and win" game after every shop.

Simple Tips for a Great Lidl Shop

Follow these steps to become a Lidl expert:

  1. Plan Ahead: Check our Lidl offers page on Risij.uk to see what's coming up. Knowing the weekly theme and the Middle of Lidl items helps you create a smart shopping list.
  2. Activate Your Coupons: Before you get to the checkout, remember to open the Lidl Plus app and activate any coupons you want to use.
  3. Be Quick for the Best Bargains: The most popular items in the Middle of Lidl can sell out very fast. If you see something you really want, it's best to grab it right away.
