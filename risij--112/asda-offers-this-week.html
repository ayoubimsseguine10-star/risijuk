Decoding Asda's Offers This Week: A Shopper's Guide

Published on July 20, 2024 in Supermarket Guides

Asda is famous for its "Rollback" prices and extensive range of products. Navigating the Asda offers this week can lead to significant savings on everything from groceries to George clothing. Here's how to make sure you're getting the best value at Asda.

What is a Rollback?

A "Rollback" is Asda's term for a temporary price reduction on a specific product. These aren't clearance items; they are popular products offered at a lower price for a limited time. They are the cornerstone of Asda's value proposition.

Top Strategies for Asda Shopping

  • Look for the Green Signs: Rollbacks are clearly marked with green signage in-store and online. Keep an eye out for them as you shop.
  • Check Multi-buy Deals: Asda often runs "2 for £X" or "3 for 2" deals on essentials. These can be great value, but always check the single item price to confirm the saving.
  • Explore Own Brands: Asda's own brands, like "Just Essentials" and "Extra Special," offer fantastic quality at competitive prices. They are often included in weekly promotions.

Using the Asda Rewards App

The Asda Rewards app is a must-have for regular shoppers. You can earn pounds, not points, in your "Cashpot" by buying "Star Products" or completing "Missions."

  1. Scan Your App: Always scan your app at checkout to earn rewards.
  2. Check for Star Products: Before you shop, browse the app for Star Products on your list. These offer an instant Cashpot boost.
  3. Convert Cashpot to Vouchers: Once you've built up your Cashpot, you can easily convert it into vouchers to spend in-store or online.
