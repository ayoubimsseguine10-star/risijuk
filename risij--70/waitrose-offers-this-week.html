Waitrose Offers This Week: The 2025 Guide to Affordable Quality

Published on January 21, 2025 | Category: Saving Strategies

Waitrose is synonymous with quality, but that doesn't mean it's out of reach for savvy shoppers. By strategically using the myWaitrose loyalty program and keeping an eye on their curated offers, you can enjoy premium products without the premium price tag. This is your guide to the best Waitrose offers this week.

The myWaitrose Card: Your Personal Discount Tool

The free myWaitrose card is essential for any Waitrose shopper. Its primary benefit comes from personalization and member-exclusive deals:

  • Personalised Vouchers: The most powerful feature. Each week, you can select two vouchers in the app for discounts on entire categories, such as "20% off fish" or "15% off bakery items." Choose the ones that align with your shopping list for guaranteed savings.
  • Member-Only Prices: Look for the special green "myWaitrose price" tags in-store for instant discounts on a rotating selection of products.

Finding Value Throughout the Store

  1. Target the Dine-In Deal: Waitrose frequently offers a "Dine In for £X" deal, which typically includes a main, side, dessert, and wine or non-alcoholic alternative. This is one of the best-value meal deals on the market.
  2. Embrace the Essentials Range: Don't dismiss the "Essential Waitrose" range. It comprises thousands of products that offer the quality you expect from the brand at a more competitive price point.
  3. Time Your Visit for Reductions: The reduced-to-clear section at Waitrose can be a goldmine for high-quality items at a fraction of their original price. An evening visit often yields the best results.
