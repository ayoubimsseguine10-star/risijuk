Finding Value at Morrisons This Week

Published October 28, 2024 | Supermarket Strategy

Morrisons distinguishes itself in the UK supermarket sector through its "Market Street" concept, which integrates traditional butcher, baker, and fishmonger counters into the modern store. An effective savings strategy requires combining these fresh offers with the digital promotions available in the Morrisons offers this week.

The Key Components of Saving at Morrisons

Value at Morrisons is primarily accessed through three channels:

  • The My Morrisons App: The core of the loyalty program, this app delivers personalised, money-off coupons based on an individual's purchasing history. Consistent use leads to increasingly relevant and valuable offers.
  • Market Street Promotions: The fresh food counters are a source of dynamic daily and weekly deals that are often distinct from the main store's promotions. Vigilance in these areas is rewarded.
  • Store-Wide Weekly Offers: Alongside its unique features, Morrisons runs conventional promotions, including half-price events and multi-buy deals on a wide array of branded and own-label goods.

A Shopper's Checklist for Morrisons

To shop with financial prudence, the following practices are recommended:

  1. Review Offers Pre-Visit: A preparatory check of the week's main promotions is a crucial first step. A curated list on a site like Risij.uk's Morrisons page provides a time-efficient overview.
  2. Engage with the Loyalty App: Before shopping, it is essential to open the My Morrisons app and digitally activate the personalised coupons you intend to use.
  3. Explore Food Waste Reduction Schemes: Morrisons actively participates in the Too Good To Go program, offering "Magic Boxes" of surplus food at a significant discount. This presents an excellent opportunity for both savings and waste reduction.

A Synthesis of Quality and Value

Morrisons successfully balances its commitment to fresh, high-quality produce with the competitive pricing expected of a major UK supermarket. Its model offers a different path to value than a discounter like Aldi, but for the shopper who utilises the app and pays attention to the fresh counters, the savings can be equally substantial.
