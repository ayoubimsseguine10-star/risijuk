Get More from Morrisons: A Guide to This Week's Offers

Published on January 09, 2025 in Supermarket Guides

Morrisons sets itself apart with its unique "Market Street," featuring skilled in-store butchers, bakers, and fishmongers. This commitment to freshness, paired with a robust loyalty program, creates numerous avenues for savings. Let's explore the Morrisons offers this week.

Harnessing the Power of the Morrisons More Card

The Morrisons More card is an indispensable tool for any regular shopper. It’s a points-based system that rewards you on select products and fuel, but its real strength lies in personalized offers.

Effective More Card Strategies

  • Activate Before You Shop: Always check the Morrisons app or website before you go. Activate tailored offers based on your shopping habits for extra points or direct discounts.
  • Scan Religiously: Make it a habit to scan your card (physical or digital) at every checkout to accumulate points and redeem your activated offers.
  • Claim Your Fivers: Once you hit 5,000 points, you'll be rewarded with a "Morrisons Fiver" (£5 voucher) to use on a future shop.

The Market Street Advantage

Market Street is where you can find some of the freshest products and best deals in the store.

  1. Butcher's Bundles: Watch for multi-buy promotions like "3 for £10" on fresh chicken, mince, sausages, and other butcher-prepared items.
  2. Fishmonger's Specials: Many stores feature special prices on fresh fish, particularly on Fridays.
  3. Bakery Bargains: The in-store bakery often has deals on fresh loaves and rolls, with prices dropping further towards the end of the day.

Fight Waste, Find Value with "Magic Box"

For an incredible bargain, see if your local Morrisons is on the "Too Good To Go" app. You can reserve a "Magic Box" filled with surplus food from the store at a huge discount. It's a fantastic way to save money and combat food waste.
