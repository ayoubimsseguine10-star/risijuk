A Simple Guide to Aldi's Weekly Treasures

Simple Guide | Updated: October 2024

Shopping at Aldi can feel like a treasure hunt. Between the famous 'middle aisle' and amazing fruit and veg deals, there are big savings to be had. To get the most from the Aldi offers for next week, all you need is a simple plan.

The Two Big Deals to Look For

Every week, Aldi has two main events that are great for saving money:

  • Specialbuys: These are the interesting items you'll find in the middle aisles, from garden furniture to kitchen gadgets. They appear every Thursday and Sunday and sell out fast!
  • Super 6: This is a selection of six fruits and vegetables at super low prices. The selection changes every couple of weeks, making it a great way to save on fresh food.

Our 3 Simple Tips for a Great Aldi Shop

Just follow these easy steps to make your trip a success:

  1. Check the Deals Before You Go: Have a quick look online to see what Specialbuys are coming up. A helpful site like Risij.uk can show you the deals in advance so you can make a list.
  2. Pick the Best Time to Visit: If you want a popular Specialbuy, it's best to go on a Thursday morning. For your regular grocery shop, popping in during a quieter time makes for a more relaxed trip.
  3. Remember the Basics: On top of the special deals, Aldi's own-brand products are great quality and offer fantastic value compared to bigger brands.
