A Guide to Morrisons' Market Street & Weekly Savers

Category: Shopping Guides

Morrisons holds a unique position with its in-store 'Market Street'—a collection of fresh counters including a butcher, baker, and fishmonger. A smart shopping strategy involves combining these fresh, high-quality deals with the standard Morrisons offers this week to elevate your meals for less.

How to Unlock the Best Value at Morrisons

An effective Morrisons shop blends loyalty rewards with a keen eye for their fresh food promotions and weekly savers.

  • My Morrisons Rewards: This app-based scheme is essential. It provides personalized money-off coupons on products you actually buy. Always activate your offers before you shop.
  • Market Street Deals: Look for special promotions at the fresh counters. These often provide superior quality and value compared to pre-packaged alternatives.
  • Weekly Savers: Scan the aisles for deals on major brands and pantry essentials, which are updated every week.

Top Tips for Shopping at Morrisons

1. Activate Your App Offers First

Before you even write your shopping list, open the My Morrisons app to see your personalized offers. This helps shape your meal plan and guarantees you don't miss out on savings tailored specifically to you.

2. Engage with Market Street

Don't hesitate to talk to the staff at the fresh counters. They can offer advice on the best cuts of meat or the day's freshest catch, and you can buy the exact quantity you need, which helps to reduce food waste.

3. Hunt for Evening Reductions

Morrisons is known for its end-of-day reductions on fresh items. Their "Magic Boxes" on the Too Good To Go app are also a fantastic way to get a surprise selection of quality food at a steep discount.
