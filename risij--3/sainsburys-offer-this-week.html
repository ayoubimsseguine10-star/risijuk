How to Make the Most of Every Sainsbury's Offer This Week

Published on July 28, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Programs

Sainsbury's has fully embraced the loyalty card model, making a Nectar card essential for any regular shopper. The key to saving is to look beyond the shelf price and understand how to leverage all the tools they offer with every Sainsbury's offer this week.

Nectar Prices: The New Norm

Similar to Tesco's Clubcard Prices, Sainsbury's now offers "Nectar Prices" on hundreds of items across the store. These are instant discounts available only to Nectar card holders. You simply scan your card or app at the till. Without a Nectar card, you'll pay the higher price, so it's a must-have.

Personalized Savings with SmartShop

The SmartShop app (or the in-store handsets) is Sainsbury's secret weapon for savers. As you scan your items, the app will often present you with personalized digital coupons based on your shopping history. These can be significant discounts on your favourite products. It pays to check the "My Offers" section of the Nectar app before you even start your shop.

Nectar Bonus Points

Keep an eye on the Nectar app for bonus point events. They often run promotions like "10x points on fresh fruit and veg" or bonus points for spending a certain amount. These can help your points balance grow much faster, which you can then spend at Sainsbury's, Argos, or other partners.

While the weekly deals at Morrisons might be more focused on fresh counters, Sainsbury's excels in personalized digital offers.
