Fresh & Frugal: A Guide to Morrisons Offers This Week

Published on: 24th July 2024 | Category: Shopping Strategy

Morrisons sets itself apart with its unique "Market Street," featuring in-store butchers, bakers, and fishmongers who bring a traditional touch to the modern supermarket. This emphasis on freshness, paired with a robust loyalty scheme, creates multiple pathways to savings. Here’s how to leverage the Morrisons offers this week.

The Power of the Morrisons More Card

The "Morrisons More" loyalty program is a simple and effective tool for extracting extra value from every shop. It focuses on providing instant discounts and earning points for future savings.

Key More Card Benefits:

  • More Card Exclusives: Get immediate price reductions on hundreds of items throughout the store simply by scanning your card at the checkout.
  • Earn Points on Fuel & More: Collect points on fuel and selected in-store products. These points are then converted into "Morrisons Fivers" – £5 vouchers to spend on your groceries.
  • Boost Your Points with the App: The app is home to personalized coupons and bonus point offers, helping you reach your next reward even faster.

See This Week's Exclusive Prices! Find all current promotions and More Card deals on the Morrisons Offers This Week page on Risij.uk.

Finding Value on Market Street

The Market Street counters are a goldmine for savvy shoppers. The skilled staff can provide custom cuts of meat or specific fish fillets, which not only ensures freshness but also helps reduce food waste and can be more economical than buying pre-packaged options. Also, be sure to look out for "when it's gone, it's gone" (WIGIG) deals across the store for limited-time bargains.

Your Morrisons Action Plan

Before you shop, browse the weekly offers online and check the Morrisons More app to activate any personalized coupons. Always scan your More card to secure exclusive prices and earn points. A quick visit to the Market Street counters can also yield high-quality, fresh products at excellent prices, making your shop both delicious and affordable.
