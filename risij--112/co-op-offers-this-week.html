Unlocking Value with Co-op Offers This Week

Published on July 19, 2024 in Supermarket Guides

As a convenience retailer, Co-op might not be the first place you think of for a big weekly shop, but their member deals and weekly offers can provide surprising value. The Co-op offers this week are especially beneficial for members.

The Power of Co-op Membership

Becoming a Co-op member for just £1 unlocks a world of savings. The biggest perk is access to "Member Prices," which are significant discounts on everyday items. These are clearly marked in-store with blue shelf labels.

How Membership Benefits You

  • Exclusive Prices: Save instantly on dozens of products across the store, from fresh food to household essentials.
  • Personalised Offers: In the Co-op app, you can choose two new personalised offers each week.
  • Support Your Community: For every £1 you spend on Co-op own-brand products, 2p goes to support local community causes.

Finding the Best Deals

Beyond member pricing, Co-op runs regular promotions that are available to all shoppers.

  1. Check the Freezer Deals: Co-op often has excellent bundle deals on frozen food, like pizzas, ice cream, and sides for a set price (e.g., £5).
  2. Look for Meal Deals: Their popular meal deals for lunch are great value, but also look for evening meal deals which often include a main, a side, and a dessert or drink.
  3. Use Risij.uk: We track the best Co-op offers and member prices so you can plan your top-up shop for maximum savings.

Ethical Shopping

Shopping at Co-op also means supporting an ethical retailer. They are committed to Fairtrade products, British sourcing, and supporting local communities, so you can feel good about where your money is going.
