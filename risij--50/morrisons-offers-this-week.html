Navigating Morrisons Offers This Week

Published on October 24, 2024 | Category: Shopping Guides

Morrisons stands out with its "Market Street," featuring in-store butchers, bakers, and fishmongers that give it a traditional market feel. Shopping the Morrisons offers this week is a great way to get fresh, high-quality food at competitive prices.

The 'My Morrisons' Loyalty Program

The key to saving at Morrisons is the 'My Morrisons' app. Unlike points-based systems, it provides instant money-off rewards and personalised offers based on your shopping history. It's a simple, direct way to save money on the things you actually buy.

How to Maximise 'My Morrisons':

  • Activate your offers: Check the app before you shop and activate the personalised deals waiting for you.
  • Join the clubs: Sign up for clubs like the "Christmas Savers" or "Baby Club" for extra exclusive deals.
  • Scan your card: Always scan your digital card at the checkout to ensure you get your discounts.

Best Things to Buy at Morrisons

While their multi-buy deals on groceries are always worth a look, Morrisons really shines in its fresh food departments. Look for deals on:

  • Market Street Meat & Fish: The counters often have better deals and quality than the pre-packaged options.
  • In-store Bakery: Freshly baked bread, cakes, and pastries are often on promotion.
  • Salad Bar: A great value option for a healthy and customisable lunch.
