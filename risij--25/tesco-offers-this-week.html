Your Essential Guide to Tesco Offers This Week & Clubcard Prices

Published on July 28, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Programs

Shopping at Tesco has changed significantly in recent years. To get the best value, having a Clubcard is no longer optional – it's essential. The majority of the best Tesco offers this week are exclusively available to Clubcard holders.

What Are Clubcard Prices?

Clubcard Prices are instant discounts you get at the checkout simply by scanning your Clubcard app or physical card. You'll see two prices on the shelf for many items: the regular price and the lower Clubcard Price. These deals cover a massive range of products, from fresh meat and vegetables to household essentials.

It's Tesco's way of rewarding loyal customers, and the savings can be huge over the course of a weekly shop. Without a Clubcard, you could be paying significantly more for the same basket of goods.

How to Get the Most Out of Your Clubcard

  1. Always Scan It: Make it a habit to scan your Clubcard for every purchase, no matter how small.
  2. Use the App: The Tesco Grocery & Clubcard app not only stores your card but also gives you personalized coupons.
  3. Convert Vouchers Wisely: Every three months, your points are converted into vouchers. You can spend these in-store or, for better value, exchange them with Reward Partners for up to 2x their value on things like restaurant meals or days out.

While discounters like Aldi focus on everyday low prices, Tesco's strategy is to provide deep discounts to its loyal members.
