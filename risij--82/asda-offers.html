Asda's "Rollback" is a cornerstone of its commercial strategy, signifying a temporary but genuine reduction in price on a diverse range of merchandise. A Prudent Assessment of Multi-Buy Promotions

Offers styled as "2 for £5" or similar are ubiquitous at Asda. While they possess an immediate appeal, their true economic merit warrants a brief calculation. A discerning shopper will always verify the per-unit cost to confirm a real saving. Such promotions are most advantageous when applied to household staples that are consumed with regularity, thereby preventing waste.
