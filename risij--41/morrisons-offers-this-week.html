How to Get More from Morrisons: A Guide to This Week's Offers

Published on October 09, 2024 in Supermarket Guides

Morrisons stands out with its unique "Market Street," featuring in-store butchers, bakers, and fishmongers who bring a touch of tradition to the modern supermarket. This focus on freshness, combined with a powerful loyalty program, creates multiple ways to save. Let's explore the Morrisons offers this week.

Mastering the Morrisons More Card

The Morrisons More card is your key to unlocking deeper savings. While it functions as a points-based system on select products and fuel, its real power is in the personalized offers delivered via the app.

Your More Card Action Plan

  • Activate Offers Before You Go: Always check the Morrisons app or website before you shop. Activate personalized deals to earn extra points or get instant discounts.
  • Scan Without Fail: Make it a habit to scan your card (physical or on the app) at every single checkout to collect points and redeem your activated offers.
  • Claim Your "Fivers": Once you hit 5,000 points, you'll get a "Morrisons Fiver" (£5 voucher) to take off a future shop.

The Market Street Advantage

Market Street is where you'll find the freshest products and some of the best deals in the store.

  1. Butcher's Bundles: Watch for multi-buy deals like "3 for £10" on fresh chicken, mince, and sausages prepared by their in-store butchers.
  2. Fishmonger's Specials: Many stores have a "Catch of the Day" with special prices on fresh fish and seafood, especially on Fridays.
  3. Bakery Bargains: The in-store bakery often has promotions on fresh loaves, and prices tend to drop later in the day.

Save Food, Save Money with "Magic Boxes"

For a fantastic bargain, check if your local Morrisons is on the "Too Good To Go" app. You can reserve a "Magic Box" filled with surplus food from the store at a huge discount—a great way to save money and fight food waste.
