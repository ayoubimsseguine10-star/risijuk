A Sneak Peek at Next Week's Aldi Specialbuys

Published on July 29, 2024 | Category: Shopping Tips

One of the best things about Aldi is the Specialbuys aisle! It’s always fun to see what treasures you can find. This little guide will give you a friendly heads-up on the Aldi offers for next week so you can plan ahead and snag the best bargains.

What are Specialbuys?

Specialbuys are the limited-edition items Aldi sells in the middle aisle every Thursday and Sunday. They can be anything from garden furniture and kitchen gadgets to clothes and DIY tools. Once they're gone, they're gone, which is why a little planning can go a long way!

What to Look Out For

Based on the time of year and what's been popular lately, we think you'll see some great items in these categories:

  • Gardening Gear: As the seasons change, keep an eye out for planters, tools, and lovely outdoor decor.
  • Kitchen Helpers: We're expecting to see some handy gadgets like air fryers, coffee machines, or special cookware.
  • Cozy Home Comforts: Look for things like soft blankets, nice candles, and clever storage solutions to make your home feel extra cozy.

Our 3 Simple Shopping Tips

Here’s how to make your Specialbuys trip a success:

  1. Check Online First: Aldi shows you what's coming up on its website. Have a look beforehand and make a little wishlist. You can see the full list on our main site, Risij.uk.
  2. Go on Thursday or Sunday Morning: These are the days the new items arrive. Going in the morning gives you the best chance to find what you're looking for before it sells out.
  3. If You Love It, Grab It: It’s easy to think "I'll get it on my way out," but popular items can disappear fast! It's usually best to pop what you want into your trolley right away.
