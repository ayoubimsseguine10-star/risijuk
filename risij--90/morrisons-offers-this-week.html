Extracting Value from Morrisons' Weekly Offers

Published on October 20, 2024 | Category: Retail Analysis

Morrisons differentiates itself through its "Market Street" concept, which integrates fresh food counters into the supermarket layout. This, combined with the Morrisons More loyalty program, provides two distinct avenues for savings. A successful shop requires monitoring the Morrisons offers this week across both fronts.

A Guide to Market Street Value

The fresh food counters offer opportunities for savings that are not available in pre-packaged formats.

  • Butcher & Fish Counters: Multi-buy promotions and "Catch of the Day" style deals are common. These are ideal for planned bulk purchases and can offer superior value to packaged equivalents.
  • Bakery & Deli Counters: Items are often reduced in price towards the end of the day to minimize waste. A late-afternoon visit can yield significant discounts on fresh goods.

The Morrisons More Program: An Essential Tool

The current iteration of the Morrisons More card focuses on personalized, app-based offers. It is no longer a passive points-collection system. The crucial step for any shopper is to open the app and activate relevant offers *before* checkout. This simple action unlocks discounts that would otherwise be missed and is a fundamental component of saving money at Morrisons.
