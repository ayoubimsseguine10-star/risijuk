How to Get More Value with Morrisons Offers This Week

Published: October 20, 2023 | Category: Loyalty Programs

Morrisons stands out with its "Market Street" of fresh counters and a strong loyalty program. To truly benefit from shopping here, you need to engage with their More Card scheme and keep an eye out for specific in-store deals. Here’s how to make the most of the Morrisons offers this week.

The Morrisons More Card is Essential

The recently revamped More Card is your key to savings. It's a points-based system with several ways to earn and save:

  • Points on Everything: You earn points on almost every pound you spend in-store, online, and on fuel.
  • More Card Exclusives: Look for special prices throughout the store that are only available to More Card holders.
  • Personalised Offers: Check the app for offers tailored to your shopping habits. Activating these can earn you bonus points on products you buy regularly.
  • Fuel Savings: Points earned on shopping can be converted into money-off vouchers for fuel at Morrisons petrol stations.

Tip: Check the App for Bonus Points

Before you shop, always check the Morrisons More app. Activate any personalised offers and look for "Points Boosters" on certain products. Buying these items can significantly speed up how quickly you earn a £5 voucher (5,000 points).

Don't Miss Market Street

Morrisons' fresh food counters—the butcher, the baker, the fishmonger—are a key feature. They often have their own deals and promotions that aren't part of the main store offers. It's a great place to find high-quality meat, fish, and bread at competitive prices.
