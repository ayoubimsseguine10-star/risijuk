A Simple Guide to Lidl Offers This Week

Published on January 4, 2025 | Category: Discount Grocers

Shopping at Lidl can feel like a treasure hunt, especially in the famous middle aisle. To make the most of the Lidl offers this week, it helps to have a simple plan, especially for their limited-time deals.

How to Shop the "Middle of Lidl"

The middle aisle is where Lidl sells a changing range of non-food items, with new products arriving every Thursday and Sunday. These items are only available while stocks last, so it pays to be prepared.

Tips for the Middle Aisle:

  • Check the Leaflet First: Look at the Lidl leaflet online before you go to see what's on offer and decide if there's anything you want.
  • Go Early for the Best Choice: The most popular items can sell out quickly, so shopping on Thursday or Sunday morning is your best bet.
  • If You Want It, Get It: Unlike regular stock, these items won't be replaced once they're gone. If you see something you want, it's best to buy it then and there.

Why You Should Use the Lidl Plus App

The Lidl Plus app is a free and essential tool for saving even more. It gives you weekly digital coupons for money off specific products, a digital scratch card for a chance to win prizes, and it stores all your receipts. Always check the app for special deals, like the "Pick of the Week" on fresh fruit and veg.
