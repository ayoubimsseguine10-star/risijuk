About Risij.uk

Our mission is simple: to help you save money on your weekly grocery shop.

In a world of rising costs, keeping track of which supermarket has the best deals can be a full-time job. That's where Risij.uk comes in. We created a platform to consolidate all the weekly offers from major UK supermarkets into one easy-to-use place.

What We Do

We monitor the weekly flyers, digital coupons, and loyalty card exclusives from stores like Lidl, Aldi, Tesco, Sainsbury's, and many more. Our team works around the clock to ensure the information on our site is accurate and up-to-date, so you can plan your shopping with confidence.

From the famous Lidl offers this week to the exclusive Clubcard prices in the Tesco offers this week, we cover it all.

Why This Blog?

This blog is an extension of our main service. Here, we go beyond just listing deals. We provide:

  • Shopping Strategies: Tips on how to make the most of each store's offers.
  • Deal Breakdowns: In-depth looks at standout bargains and whether they're worth it.
  • Savings Advice: General money-saving advice to help your budget stretch further.

Thank you for trusting Risij.uk. We're excited to be part of your savings journey!
