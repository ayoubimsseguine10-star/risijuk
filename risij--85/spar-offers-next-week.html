A Simple Guide to Spar Offers Next Week

Published on Jan 19, 2025 | In Saving Guides

Your local Spar is perfect for when you need to grab a few things without going to a big supermarket. And with a little planning, you can find some great deals there too. Here's our simple guide to the Spar offers for next week.

What Kind of Deals Does Spar Have?

Spar's offers are usually focused on making life a little easier. You'll often find deals on:

  • Easy Meals: Look for deals like "Tonight's Tea" that bundle items together for a simple dinner.
  • Everyday Items: Each week, you'll find discounts on basics like bread, milk, and coffee.
  • Snacks and Drinks: Spar often has multi-buy deals on crisps, chocolate, and drinks, which are great for a treat.

How to Find the Best Value

  1. Check for a Leaflet: The easiest way to see what's on offer is to pick up a leaflet in-store. This will show you the main deals for the week.
  2. Look at the Window Posters: Spar usually advertises its best offers on big posters in the window, so you can see them before you even go inside.
  3. Try the Spar Brand: The Spar own-brand products are a good way to save money and are often part of the weekly deals.

While the deals can be slightly different from one store to another, you can see the main national offers on the Risij.uk website to get an idea of what to look for.
