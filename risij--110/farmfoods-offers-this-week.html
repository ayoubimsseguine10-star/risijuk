A Simple Guide to Saving Money at Farmfoods

Simple Guide | Updated: October 2024

Farmfoods is brilliant for stocking up your freezer and cupboards, and their savings method is super simple. To get the best value from the Farmfoods offers this week, the number one rule is to use their money-off vouchers.

How Farmfoods Savings Work

It's a really easy two-step process:

  • Step 1: The In-Store Deals. Farmfoods prices are already low, especially with their multi-buy deals like "3 for £5". This is your first layer of savings.
  • Step 2: The Voucher Boost. On top of those deals, Farmfoods offers vouchers for money off when you spend a certain amount (like £5 off when you spend £50). This is how you get the biggest discounts.

Your Easy Farmfoods Shopping Plan

For the best possible shopping trip, just follow these three steps:

  1. Get Your Voucher First: This is the most important step! Never go to Farmfoods without a voucher. You can easily find them on their website or on a deals site like Risij.uk.
  2. Check Your Freezer Space: Since Farmfoods is great for frozen food, make sure you have enough room at home before you go on a big shopping trip.
  3. Look Beyond the Freezers: While you're there, have a look at their other aisles. They often have great deals on brand-name items like cereals, drinks, and cleaning supplies.
