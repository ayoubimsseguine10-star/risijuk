A Simple Guide to Tesco Offers This Week

Published on Jan 20, 2025 | In Saving Guides

If you shop at Tesco, the single best way to save money is by using a Clubcard. It's no longer just for points; it's your key to big discounts. This simple guide will explain how to get the most from the Tesco offers this week.

Clubcard Prices are Key

Shopping at Tesco without a Clubcard means you'll pay more than you need to. Clubcard Prices are special discounts that are applied instantly when you scan your Clubcard (or the app) at the checkout. These deals are the main way Tesco gives value back to its customers.

A Simple Plan for Saving at Tesco

  1. Look for Clubcard Prices: Before you shop, check the latest Clubcard Prices on Risij.uk. Plan to buy items on this list to get the biggest discounts, especially on things like meat, coffee, or cleaning supplies.
  2. Find the Aldi Price Match: For everyday items like bread, milk, and eggs, look for the "Aldi Price Match" sign. This means Tesco has matched its price to Aldi's, so you know you're getting a competitive price.
  3. Keep an Eye Out for Yellow Stickers: Tesco reduces the price of items that are close to their use-by date with yellow stickers. The best time to find these is usually in the evening. It's a great way to find a bargain!

By always using your Clubcard and looking out for these different types of deals, you can easily create a smart shopping plan that saves you money every week.
