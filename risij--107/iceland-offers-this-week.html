The Iceland Bonus Card: A Simple Savings Multiplier

The Iceland Bonus Card is a refreshingly simple loyalty mechanism. For every £20 you load onto the card, Iceland provides a £1 bonus. This translates to a guaranteed 5% return on your investment before any other offers are applied.

How to Maximize Value at Iceland:
