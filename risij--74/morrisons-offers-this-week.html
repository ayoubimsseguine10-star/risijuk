Exploring the Charm of Morrisons' Market Street

Published: Oct 20, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

What makes Morrisons special is its "Market Street" – a lovely collection of counters with butchers, bakers, and fishmongers, right in the store! It feels like a traditional market and is a wonderful place to find fresh food and great deals. Let's explore the Morrisons offers this week together.

The Best of Market Street

Because so much is prepared fresh in-store, you can often find better quality and value here than elsewhere. You can ask the butcher for a specific cut of meat or get advice from the fishmonger on what's best that day.

A Hot Tip: Look for "Yellow Sticker" Bargains

One of the best ways to save is to keep an eye out for items with yellow reduction stickers. Towards the end of the day, fresh bread from the bakery or cuts of meat can be marked down. It's a fantastic way to get high-quality food for less.

Don't Forget the My Morrisons App!

The "My Morrisons" app is your key to extra savings. It doesn't bother with complicated points; it just gives you money-off coupons tailored to you. It's a bit like the personalised deals you find with the Co-op offers this week. Just remember to activate them before you shop!

Keep an Eye on Seasonal Deals

Morrisons always has great multi-buy offers, especially around holidays and different seasons. Their "2 for £X" deals on fresh fruit and veg are usually very good. It's always worth comparing them to the Sainsbury's offers this week to see who has the best price on your favourites.

Top Tip: Don't be shy to chat with the staff on the Market Street counters! They are full of expert knowledge and can help you find the best value of the day.
