A Strategic Guide to Maximising the Lidl Plus App

Category: Shopping Strategy | Published: October 2024

While Lidl is renowned for its excellent baseline prices, the Lidl Plus application is the key differentiator between a casual shopper and a strategic saver. This complimentary digital tool provides access to a tier of discounts not available through any other means.

Accessing an Additional Layer of Value

The primary function of the Lidl Plus app is the delivery of weekly digital coupons. Each Thursday, a new selection of personalized discounts becomes available, offering savings on specific products or a percentage off a total spend. Consistent use of the app is an essential practice for any serious Lidl customer.

The Lidl Shopper's Digital Protocol

  • Pre-Activation is Mandatory: Before beginning your shop, open the app and manually tap 'Activate' on all available coupons. They will not be applied automatically at the checkout unless this step is taken.
  • Scan Your Digital Card: At the till, present the QR code within the app for scanning. This single action applies all of your activated coupons to the transaction.
  • Plan Around the "Middle of Lidl": This central aisle contains weekly themed, non-food promotions. Reviewing the Lidl offers this week online allows you to anticipate these themes and plan accordingly.
  • Incorporate the Bakery: Lidl's in-store bakery provides high-quality products at competitive prices, offering an easy way to add value to your shop.

Ensure your savings are applied. Review this week's app-exclusive coupons and the full Lidl offers for this week on our main platform.

By integrating the Lidl Plus app into your regular shopping routine, you can consistently achieve savings that extend well beyond Lidl's already competitive shelf prices.
