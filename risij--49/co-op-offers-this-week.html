Your Guide to Co-op's Member Deals This Week

Published on: October 24, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Programs

Co-op is more than just a convenience store; it's a community-focused retailer with a powerful membership program. Unlocking the best value here means taking full advantage of your membership card. Let's explore the Co-op offers this week.

The Power of Co-op Membership

For a one-off cost of £1, you become a Co-op member. This gives you access to exclusive member-only prices on a range of products each week. These are often significant discounts that make Co-op highly competitive with larger supermarkets.

Tip 1: Look for the Blue Member Price Labels

In store, member deals are highlighted with bright blue shelf tickets. These are your cue to scan your card at the checkout to get the lower price. It's a similar principle to Tesco's Clubcard, so if you're used to spotting those deals, you'll feel right at home.

Tip 2: Choose Your Personalised Offers

Through the Co-op app, you can choose two personalised offers each week. These are tailored to your shopping habits, so you can save money on products you actually buy. This is a key advantage over blanket promotions like the SPAR offers next week, which are the same for everyone.

Community and Convenience

Remember that a portion of what you spend on Co-op branded products goes back into your community. So, when you save money on the Co-op offers this week, you're also contributing to local causes.
