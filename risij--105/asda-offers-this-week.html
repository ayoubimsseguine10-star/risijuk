An Analysis of ASDA's 'Rollback' Pricing

Deconstructing ASDA's signature pricing strategy and its value proposition.

ASDA's market strategy is predicated on transparent, straightforward value. The ASDA offers this week eschew complex loyalty mechanics in favour of two primary tactical pillars: the "Rollback" and volume-based discounts.

Defining the "Rollback"

The "Rollback" is a term for a temporary, direct price reduction on a given product. It is ASDA's principal mechanism for promotional activity. These are not end-of-line clearances but are tactical, time-limited discounts on staple and high-demand consumer goods, designed to drive footfall and communicate value.
