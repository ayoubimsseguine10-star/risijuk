How to Get More from Morrisons: A More Card Guide

Published: October 20, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

Morrisons offers a unique shopping experience with its "Market Street" fresh counters and a robust loyalty scheme. The key to unlocking genuine savings here is the Morrisons More Card. This guide breaks down how to use it effectively with the Morrisons offers this week.

Understanding the More Card

The More Card is a comprehensive loyalty program with several layers of benefits. Let's break them down.

1. Earning Points

The system is straightforward: you earn points on your spending. This includes groceries in-store, online orders, and even fuel from Morrisons petrol stations. Once you reach 5,000 points, you receive a £5 voucher to spend.

2. More Card Exclusives

This is a major perk. Similar to a Tesco Clubcard, your More Card gives you access to instant lower prices on hundreds of items throughout the store. These are clearly marked and offer immediate value.

3. The Morrisons More App

The app is your tool for accelerating your savings. Before you shop, open the app and "activate" personalised offers for bonus points on products you frequently buy. This is the fastest way to earn those £5 vouchers.

Don't Forget Market Street

A key feature of Morrisons is its in-store specialists—the butcher, baker, and fishmonger. These counters often have their own unique deals and multi-buy offers that provide excellent quality and value, separate from the main store promotions.
