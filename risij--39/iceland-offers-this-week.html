How to Save Big at Iceland with the Bonus Card

Published: October 28, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

Iceland is the expert when it comes to frozen food, offering both convenience and great value. By taking advantage of their loyalty card and special offers, you can make your budget stretch further. Let's look at how to get the most from the Iceland offers this week.

How the Bonus Card Works

Iceland's Bonus Card is one of the best loyalty schemes around. It's really simple: for every £20 you save onto your card, Iceland gives you an extra £1 for free. That's like getting an instant 5% bonus, which is a great deal.

Simple Tips for Saving at Iceland

  • Top Up Your Bonus Card: Before you shop, add money to your Bonus Card. You'll get the £1 bonus right away for every £20 you add. Then, just use your card balance to pay for your shopping.
  • Look for Multi-Buy Deals: Iceland is famous for its multi-buy offers, like "3 for £10" on pizzas, ready meals, or frozen chicken. Stocking up your freezer when these deals are on is a very smart move.
  • Get Free Home Delivery: If you shop online and spend over a certain amount, Iceland will deliver your shopping for free. This saves you both time and money.
  • Find Exclusive Brands: Iceland often sells exclusive products from brands like Greggs and TGI Fridays that you won't find in other supermarkets.

The Benefits of Frozen Food

Frozen food is a great way to save money and reduce waste. Freezing locks in nutrients, so frozen fruits and vegetables are just as healthy as fresh ones. They also last much longer, meaning you throw less away. Using Iceland's offers is a clever way to manage your budget, which is a different approach to the fresh-food focus at a store like Morrisons.
