The Ultimate Lidl Offers Guide

Published on July 28, 2024 | Category: Shopping Tips

Lidl has become a firm favourite for UK shoppers, known for its high-quality produce and, of course, the legendary "Middle of Lidl." To get the best value, you need a strategy for tackling the Lidl offers this week.

Understanding Lidl's Deals

Lidl's promotions are structured and predictable, making it easy to plan your shopping trips for maximum savings.

The Middle of Lidl

Available from every Thursday and Sunday, this is where you'll find an eclectic mix of non-food items, from power tools to ski gear. These are limited-stock items, so it pays to be early. Lidl releases a leaflet and updates its app with the upcoming items, so you can plan ahead.

Weekly Food Themes

Each week, Lidl focuses on a different international cuisine, such as Italian, Greek, or American week. This is a fantastic opportunity to try new ingredients and products at a great price.

The Lidl Plus App

The Lidl Plus app is essential for any regular Lidl shopper. It provides weekly coupons for extra discounts, a digital scratch card for a chance to win prizes, and a "Coupon Plus" feature that rewards you for hitting spending targets.

Lidl's main competitor, Aldi, has a very similar model. It's always a good idea to also check the Aldi offers next week to see who has the better deals.
