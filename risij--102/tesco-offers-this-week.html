Get More from Your Tesco Clubcard

Category: Loyalty Programmes

Your Tesco Clubcard is more than just a way to get discounts; it's a powerful savings tool. By understanding its three main features, you can significantly lower your grocery bills. Here’s what you need to know about the Tesco offers this week.

1. Clubcard Prices: Your Instant Discount

This is the simplest and most common way to save. 'Clubcard Prices' are lower prices on hundreds of items throughout the store, exclusively for Clubcard holders. All you have to do is scan your Clubcard (or the app) at checkout to get the discount instantly.

2. Earning Points on Every Shop

Every time you scan your card, you earn points on almost everything you buy. You get 1 point for every £1 you spend. These points add up over time and are converted into vouchers that you can spend in-store or online.

3. The Secret Weapon: Reward Partners

This is where you can get the most value. Instead of spending your Clubcard vouchers at Tesco, you can exchange them with 'Reward Partners'. Often, this will double or even triple their value. For example, a £5 voucher could become £10 or £15 to spend at a restaurant, on a day out, or with another retailer.

Before you shop, it's always a good idea to check the full list of Tesco deals on Risij.uk to see which Clubcard Prices are most valuable this week.
