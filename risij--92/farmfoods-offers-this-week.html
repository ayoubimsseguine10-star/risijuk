Farmfoods Offers: A 3-Step Recipe for Savings

Published October 22, 2024 | In: Savings Guides

Farmfoods is a goldmine for budget shoppers, but you need a system to get the best deals. Forget wandering the aisles—use our simple, 3-step recipe to master the Farmfoods offers this week.

Step 1: Get Your Vouchers First

This is non-negotiable. Before you even think about your shopping list, go to the Farmfoods website and sign up for their email vouchers. They send out money-off coupons (£2 off £25, £5 off £60, etc.) that you can print or show on your phone. This is an instant discount on your total shop.

Step 2: Hunt the Multi-buys

Farmfoods' entire model is built on multi-buy deals, like "3 for £10" on pizzas or "2 for £5" on branded ice cream. These are your primary targets. Scan the weekly leaflet (you can find it on Risij.uk) and build your list around these core offers to get the lowest possible price per item.

Step 3: Stack and Save

This is where it all comes together. Fill your trolley with the multi-buy deals you identified. When you get to the checkout, present your money-off voucher from Step 1. You're now "stacking" your savings—getting a discount on top of already discounted items. That's the Farmfoods formula for success.
