Smart Shopping at Your Local SPAR

Category: Shopping Tips

SPAR is the king of convenience, perfect for when you need to grab something quickly. While prices can be higher than at a large supermarket, you can still find good value if you know what to look for. Here's a guide to smart shopping with the SPAR offers next week.

Focus on the Deals, Not the Shelf Price

The key to saving at SPAR is to focus on their promotions. Don't just grab the first thing you see; look for the special offers that are clearly marked.

Where to Find the Best Value:

  • Meal Deals: If you're buying lunch or a quick dinner, SPAR's meal deals (e.g., a sandwich, snack, and drink for a set price) are almost always the best value.
  • Weekly Promotions: Look for the signs advertising special prices on items like crisps, chocolate, wine, and beer. These are often very competitive.
  • SPAR Own-Brand: For basic items like milk, bread, or pasta, the SPAR own-brand range offers solid quality at a price that's much closer to what you'd pay in a larger supermarket.

Know When to Use SPAR

SPAR is ideal for immediate needs and top-up shops. It's not designed for your main weekly grocery haul. Use it for what it's good at—convenience—and focus on the promotions to avoid overpaying. You can quickly check the highlight deals on the SPAR page on Risij.uk.
