Waitrose: Quality Food for Less

Updated: November 05, 2025 | Category: Savings Guides

Waitrose is famous for its delicious, high-quality food and wonderful shopping experience. While it can seem like a more expensive option, savvy shoppers know that the Waitrose offers this week are a brilliant way to enjoy that premium quality without the premium price tag.

Unlock Savings with myWaitrose

The myWaitrose loyalty card is completely free and is the key to saving money at Waitrose. It doesn't work on points, but instead gives you instant benefits:

  • Vouchers Just for You: Every week, you'll get digital vouchers based on what you love to buy, giving you great discounts on your favourites.
  • Exclusive Member Prices: Look for the special myWaitrose prices on hundreds of items across the store for instant savings at the checkout.
  • Freebies and Perks: Enjoy a free newspaper when you spend £10, or get discounts at the in-store cafe.

Finding the Best Waitrose Deals

1. The No.1 Range Meal Deals

Waitrose often has a "Dine In" meal deal featuring their top-tier No.1 range. This usually includes a main, a side, a dessert, and a bottle of wine for a fantastic set price. It’s a very popular offer and a great way to have a special meal at home.

2. Look for the Yellow "Great Savings" Signs

Waitrose makes it easy to spot the weekly offers with their bright yellow signs. These promotions cover everything from fresh fruit and veg to cupboard staples. You can see all the top deals on our Waitrose offers page at Risij.uk before you shop.

3. The "Essential Waitrose" Range

Don't forget to check out the 'Essential' range. It features over 1,000 everyday items that have the quality you'd expect from Waitrose but at very competitive prices.
