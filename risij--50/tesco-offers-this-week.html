Tesco Offers This Week: Clubcard Savings Galore

Published on October 26, 2024 | Category: Loyalty Programs

Shopping at Tesco without a Clubcard is like leaving money on the table. The supermarket giant has fully integrated its loyalty scheme into its pricing structure, making it essential for accessing the best deals. The Tesco offers this week are almost entirely powered by Clubcard Prices.

Clubcard Prices: An Essential Tool

You'll see two prices on many items at Tesco: the standard price and a lower Clubcard Price. The difference can be huge. To get the discount, you simply need to scan your Clubcard or the Tesco app at the checkout. It's a simple step that can save you a significant amount on your total bill.

Maximising Your Clubcard:

  • Collect points everywhere: Earn points on groceries, fuel at Tesco petrol stations, and with partners like Tesco Mobile.
  • Turn vouchers into more: Every 3 months, your points are converted into vouchers. You can spend these in-store or, for better value, exchange them with Reward Partners for up to 2x their value on things like restaurant meals or days out.
  • Use the app: The Tesco Grocery & Clubcard app stores your card and provides you with personalised coupons for extra savings.

Aldi Price Match & Other Deals

To compete with discounters, Tesco runs an "Aldi Price Match" campaign on hundreds of essential products. This ensures you can get your basics at a low price without having to go to another store. Combine this with weekly multi-buy deals and promotions on their "Finest" range for a well-rounded, value-packed shop.
