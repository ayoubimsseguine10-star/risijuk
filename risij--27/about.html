Mission Statement

The Problem: Information Asymmetry

The UK grocery market is a fragmented data landscape. Consumers waste significant time and cognitive resources navigating multiple retailer websites, apps, and physical flyers to find the best prices. This inefficiency represents a market failure we aim to correct.

The Solution: Centralized Data Aggregation

Risij.uk is a platform engineered to solve this problem. We deploy automated scripts to scrape, parse, and centralize weekly offer data from all major UK supermarkets into a single, queryable database. Our function is to convert market noise into a clear signal for consumers.

Our tracked retailers include, but are not limited to:

  • Lidl
  • Aldi
  • Tesco
  • ASDA
  • Sainsbury's

This blog provides qualitative analysis and strategic interpretation of the quantitative data found on our main platform.

Core Principles

Data Integrity: Our primary objective is to provide accurate, up-to-date pricing information.

User Efficiency: Our UI/UX is designed for rapid information retrieval. We minimize clicks and cognitive load.

Market Agnostic: We are an independent entity. Our data is presented without bias toward any single retailer.
