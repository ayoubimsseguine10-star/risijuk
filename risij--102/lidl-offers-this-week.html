Winning at the 'Middle of Lidl'

Category: Shopping Tips

The 'Middle of Lidl' is famous for its eclectic mix of weekly treasures. To make sure you find the best items without getting overwhelmed, you need a smart approach. Here’s a simple guide to mastering the Lidl offers this week.

Your Most Important Tool: The Lidl Plus App

The Lidl Plus app is essential for any regular Lidl shopper. It’s more than just a digital loyalty card; it’s your key to planning and saving.

Key App Features to Use:

  • Weekly Leaflet Preview: The app shows you the upcoming middle aisle themes and products, so you can decide if a trip is worthwhile.
  • Exclusive Coupons: Before you shop, check the 'Coupons' section for extra discounts on everyday items. Activate the ones you need.
  • Digital Receipts: The app stores your receipts, which is great for tracking your spending or making returns.

Know the Brands, Know the Value

Lidl has its own range of popular in-house brands that offer fantastic quality for the price. Keep an eye out for these names, as they often represent the best value in the middle aisle:

  • Parkside: For reliable DIY tools and garden equipment.
  • Crivit: For affordable and functional sportswear and outdoor gear.
  • Silvercrest: For kitchen gadgets and small electronics.

Combining your knowledge of these brands with a quick scan of the weekly promotions on Risij.uk will ensure you're always getting the best deal.
