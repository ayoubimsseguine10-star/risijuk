Making the Most of Your Tesco Clubcard

Published: July 29, 2024 | Category: Shopping Tips

The Tesco Clubcard is a powerful tool for saving money, but many people don't use it to its full potential. This guide explains how to get the most value from the weekly Tesco offers this week using your Clubcard.

How "Clubcard Prices" Work

The biggest benefit of the Clubcard is access to "Clubcard Prices." These are instant discounts you get on hundreds of items just by scanning your card or app at the till. The price you see on the special yellow label is the price you pay—it's that simple.

Three Tips for Maximum Clubcard Value

To make sure you're getting every possible saving, follow these three simple rules:

  1. Scan It Every Single Time: Whether you're buying a week's worth of groceries or just a snack, scan your Clubcard. Every scan collects points and tells Tesco what kind of coupons you might like.
  2. Check the App for Coupons: Before you shop, open the Tesco Grocery & Clubcard App. Tesco often puts personalized coupons in there based on what you usually buy. You have to activate them in the app to use them.
  3. Use Points with Reward Partners: This is the secret to huge savings. Instead of turning your points into a £5 grocery voucher, you can exchange them with "Reward Partners" for things like restaurant meals or days out, often for double or triple the value.

Is It Worth It?

Yes. Since the Clubcard is free, scanning it for instant discounts on items you were already going to buy is an easy way to lower your shopping bill.
