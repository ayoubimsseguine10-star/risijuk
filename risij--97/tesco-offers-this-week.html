How to Effectively Use Your Clubcard on Tesco Offers This Week

Published on: October 16, 2024 | Category: Supermarket Guides

Tesco is one of the UK's largest supermarkets, and its Clubcard loyalty scheme is one of the most powerful tools for saving money. To get the best value, you need to go beyond simply collecting points and start strategically using the Tesco offers this week, especially the Clubcard Prices.

Clubcard Prices: The Key to Instant Savings

The most significant change to the Clubcard scheme in recent years is "Clubcard Prices." These are exclusive, lower prices on hundreds of products for Clubcard holders. You can save up to 50% on items just by scanning your Clubcard app or card at the till. It is essential to have your Clubcard with you every time you shop.

Maximizing Your Clubcard Points

While Clubcard Prices offer instant savings, collecting points is still a valuable long-term strategy. Here's how to boost your earnings:

  • Check the App for Coupons: Tesco regularly adds coupons to the app for bonus points on specific products or total shop value.
  • Use Reward Partners: Your Clubcard vouchers are worth up to 2x their value when exchanged with Reward Partners like Pizza Express, Cineworld, or for a Railcard.
  • Clubcard Plus: For a monthly fee, Clubcard Plus subscribers get 10% off two big shops each month, which can be a huge saving for families.

Finding This Week's Top Tesco Deals

  1. The Tesco Website and App: The 'Groceries' section clearly lists all current offers, which you can filter by category.
  2. In-Store Labels: Look for the bright yellow shelf-edge labels that highlight Clubcard Prices and other multi-buy offers.
  3. Risij.uk: We simplify your search by highlighting the very best deals. Visit our Tesco offers page for a convenient overview.
