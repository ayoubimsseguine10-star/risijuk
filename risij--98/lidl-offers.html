How to Use the Lidl Plus App Effectively

Category: Savings Guides | Published: October 2024

Lidl is known for its low prices, but the free Lidl Plus app provides an additional layer of savings that is essential for regular shoppers. Using it correctly can lead to significant extra discounts.

Unlocking Savings with Digital Coupons

The primary feature of the Lidl Plus app is its weekly selection of digital coupons. A new set of offers becomes available each Thursday, providing discounts on specific products or your total spend.

A Simple Checklist for Using Lidl Plus

  • Activate Coupons Before Shopping: This is the most important step. You must open the app and tap 'Activate' on the coupons you plan to use *before* you reach the checkout.
  • Scan Your Digital Card: At the till, simply present the QR code from the app for the cashier to scan. This will automatically apply all your activated coupons.
  • Browse the "Middle of Lidl": This central aisle contains weekly non-food bargains. You can preview the upcoming theme and items in the app or on the Lidl offers this week page online.
  • Visit the In-Store Bakery: Lidl's bakery offers fresh bread and pastries at very competitive prices, providing excellent value.

Actionable Tip: To ensure you receive all possible discounts, check the app's coupons and review the full list of Lidl offers for this week on our main site before you shop.

Incorporating the Lidl Plus app into your regular shopping routine is a simple and effective way to reduce your grocery bill even further at Lidl.
