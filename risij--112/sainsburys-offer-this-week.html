A Nectar Card Holder's Guide to Sainsbury's Offers

Published on July 13, 2024 in Supermarket Guides

Sainsbury's has transformed its loyalty program, making a Nectar card an essential tool for unlocking the best prices. Understanding Nectar Prices is the key to making the most of the Sainsbury's offers this week.

What are Nectar Prices?

Nectar Prices are exclusive lower prices on a wide range of products, available only to Nectar card holders. You'll see two prices on the shelf label: the regular price and the lower Nectar Price. To get the discount, you simply need to scan your Nectar card or app at the checkout.

How to Maximise Nectar Savings

  • Always Scan Your Card: This is the golden rule. No scan, no discount.
  • Check the App for Bonus Offers: The Nectar app provides personalised bonus point offers. Check and save these offers before you shop to boost your points balance.
  • Look for the Blue Labels: Nectar Prices are clearly marked with distinctive blue labels, making them easy to spot as you browse the aisles.

Beyond Nectar Prices

While Nectar Prices are the main attraction, there are other ways to save at Sainsbury's.

  1. Taste the Difference Deals: Look for introductory offers or multi-buy deals on Sainsbury's premium "Taste the Difference" range for an affordable treat.
  2. Aldi Price Match: Sainsbury's matches Aldi's prices on hundreds of staple products. Look for the "Aldi Price Match" logo to ensure you're getting great value on basics.
  3. Tu Clothing Sales: Keep an eye out for the regular 25% off sales on the Tu Clothing range, which offer fantastic value for money.

Spending Your Nectar Points

Once you've collected enough points (500 points = £2.50), you can spend them in-store at Sainsbury's or convert them for even better value with Nectar's partners, such as Argos or British Airways.
