Finding Value in Morrisons Offers This Week

Updated: November 01, 2024 Category: Fresh Food Savings

Morrisons stands out with its "Market Street," a collection of in-store counters staffed by specialist butchers, bakers, and fishmongers. This focus on freshness provides unique savings opportunities. Our guide will help you navigate the Morrisons offers this week and get the best of both fresh and packaged goods.

The Market Street Advantage

Market Street is the heart of Morrisons. Here, you can often find better quality and prices on fresh meat, fish, and baked goods than in the pre-packaged aisles. Don't be afraid to talk to the staff—they can offer cooking advice and point you to the day's best value.

My Morrisons Rewards: A Modern Approach

The "My Morrisons" app has moved away from points and towards instant, personalized offers. It's a crucial tool for any Morrisons shopper.

  • Activate Your Offers: Before you shop, browse the app and activate the digital coupons that are relevant to you. These are tailored to your shopping habits.
  • Look for Basket Bonuses: The app occasionally offers surprise rewards, like money off your total shop, once you've activated your offers.
  • Scan at Checkout: Simply scan your digital card at the till, and all your activated offers will be applied automatically.

Combining Fresh and Packaged Deals

The best way to shop at Morrisons is to build your list around the weekly packaged grocery deals while taking full advantage of the fresh offers on Market Street. This hybrid approach ensures you get well-rounded value. Compare their packaged deals with the latest Waitrose offers this week to see where you can save on premium goods.
