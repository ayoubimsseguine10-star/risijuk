A Guide to Freshness and Value in This Week's Morrisons Offers

Published: December 2024 | Category: Fresh & Quality

Morrisons stands apart in the UK grocery market with its unique "Market Street," which brings the feel of a traditional butcher, baker, and fishmonger into a modern supermarket. This focus on freshness is central to finding value in the Morrisons offers this week.

The Heart of the Store: Market Street

The various counters that make up Market Street are where Morrisons excels. This is where you'll find high-quality fresh produce and some of the store's best promotions. Be sure to look for multi-buy deals on fresh meat and fish, which allow you to buy premium ingredients at a great price.

Unlocking Value with the "My Morrisons" App

The My Morrisons app is the key to personalized savings. Unlike a simple points card, it requires active engagement to get the most benefit:

  • Activate Personalised Offers: The app provides money-off coupons tailored to your shopping history. You must manually activate these offers in the app before you check out.
  • Look for the "Basket Bonus": After scanning your card, the till might surprise you with a bonus offer, like a free product or a coupon for your next shop.
  • Join Specialist Clubs: If relevant to you, joining clubs like the "Baby Club" within the app can provide access to additional, targeted discounts.

Expert Tip

Morrisons is a partner of the "Too Good To Go" app. You can often purchase a "Magic Bag" of surplus food near its expiry date for a very low price—a great way to save money and reduce food waste.
