A Guide to Iceland's Best Deals This Week

Published on November 17, 2024 | Category: Budget Shopping

Iceland is a key destination for budget-conscious shoppers, offering more than just frozen food. By using their loyalty scheme and focusing on their core deals, you can find excellent value across the store. This guide breaks down the best ways to save with the Iceland offers this week.

The Iceland Bonus Card

The Iceland Bonus Card is one of the most straightforward loyalty schemes. For every £20 you load onto your card, Iceland adds £1. This is a simple and guaranteed 5% saving, making it a powerful tool for planning your spending.

How to Save More at Iceland:

  • Use the Bonus Card: Make a habit of loading money onto your Bonus Card to earn the 5% bonus. It's essentially free grocery money.
  • Focus on the £1 Deals: Iceland is well-known for its wide range of products priced at just £1. These are perfect for stocking up on staples.
  • Look Beyond Frozen: Always check the prices on everyday essentials like bread, milk, and eggs, as Iceland is often very competitive.
  • Take Advantage of Free Delivery: If you spend over the required threshold in-store or online, Iceland offers free home delivery, adding extra convenience.

The Bonus Card is the easiest way to consistently save at Iceland. Use it every time you shop.
