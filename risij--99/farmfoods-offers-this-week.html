A Guide to Stocking Your Freezer with Farmfoods

Published on Jan 14, 2025 | In Savings Guides

Farmfoods is a wonderful place for stocking up your freezer with family favourites. The secret to getting the best value is to combine their money-off vouchers with their brilliant multi-buy deals. Here’s our simple guide to the Farmfoods offers this week.

The Best Way to Save: Vouchers + Deals

Farmfoods is all about offering famous brands at great prices. The best way to save is to use one of their vouchers on top of the deals you find in-store.

A Simple 3-Step Plan for Your Shop

  1. Get Your Vouchers Ready: This is the most important step! If you sign up for emails on the Farmfoods website, they'll send you some really handy vouchers, like £5 off when you spend £60. It's well worth doing before you go.
  2. See What's on Offer: The weekly flyer shows you all the best multi-buy deals, like "3 for £10" on pizzas or chicken. We always post these on Risij.uk. See what deals would be most useful for your family's meal plan.
  3. Plan for a Big Stock-Up: Because the vouchers require a minimum spend, Farmfoods is perfect for a big 'freezer-fill' shop rather than just a few bits. It's a great opportunity to stock up on essentials that will last you for weeks.

By using this simple approach, you can get some amazing value and have a freezer full of easy meal options.

Ready to plan your freezer-fill? Take a look at the latest multi-buy deals in the Farmfoods offers this week section on our main site.
