Iceland Offers This Week: More Than Just Frozen

Published on October 23, 2024 | Category: Store Guides

While Iceland built its reputation on frozen food, the modern store offers so much more, including a full range of fresh groceries, pantry items, and household goods. The Iceland offers this week are always competitive, especially when you factor in their excellent loyalty scheme.

Get More with the Iceland Bonus Card

The Iceland Bonus Card is one of the most generous loyalty schemes available. It's not a points card; it's a savings card. Here's how it works:

  • Top up to save: You load money onto your card to spend in-store or online.
  • Get a bonus: For every £20 you load, Iceland gives you £1 for free. That's an instant 5% saving on top of all other offers.
  • Exclusive perks: Bonus Card holders get access to special competitions and occasional exclusive deals.

Top Deals to Look For

Iceland is famous for its aggressive multi-buy promotions, which often feature big brands. Keep an eye out for:

  • 3 for £10 (or similar): This recurring deal on items like pizzas, ready meals, and branded frozen goods is a family favourite.
  • Big Brand Collaborations: Iceland often partners with brands like TGI Fridays, Greggs, and Slimming World for exclusive product ranges.
  • Free Home Delivery: Spend over a certain amount in-store (usually £25) and they'll deliver it all to your door for free later that day.
