Spar: Savings Right Around the Corner

Updated: November 05, 2025 | Category: Shopping Tips

Spar is the heart of many neighbourhoods, the friendly local shop you can always rely on. While it's perfect for grabbing milk or bread, you can also find some surprisingly good deals on your everyday items. By checking the Spar offers next week in advance, you can save money without going out of your way.

Finding Value at Your Local Spar

Spar stores are run by local people, so some offers might differ slightly. However, there are always great national promotions to look out for.

1. Check Out the Mega Deals

Every week, Spar runs "Mega Deals" on popular branded snacks, drinks, and chocolates. These are often priced at just £1 or have great multi-buy discounts, making them perfect for stocking up on treats for the family.

2. Easy Evening Meal Deals

Many Spar stores have special offers to make dinnertime easier, like a deal on a pizza and a drink, or a ready meal and a dessert. It's a fantastic way to sort out a quick meal for less than the cost of a takeaway. We keep an eye on these deals and list them on our Spar offers page at Risij.uk.

3. Try the Award-Winning Spar Brand

Don't be afraid to try Spar's own-brand products! Many of them have won awards for their great taste and quality, and they offer brilliant value compared to the big brands. Their wines and ready meals are particularly good.

Need something in a hurry? Check the Spar offers for next week on Risij.uk to see if you can grab a bargain at your local!
