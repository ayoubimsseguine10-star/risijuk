Unlocking Value with Nectar and the Sainsbury's Offer This Week

Posted on October 19, 2023 in Loyalty Schemes

Sainsbury's has transformed its pricing strategy to compete directly with discount retailers, and the Nectar card is at the heart of it all. Understanding "Nectar Prices" is crucial to finding the best Sainsbury's offer this week.

What are Nectar Prices?

Similar to Tesco's Clubcard Prices, Nectar Prices offer instant discounts at the till for Nectar card holders. Hundreds of products across the store have two prices: a standard price and a lower price exclusively for those who scan their Nectar card or app. The savings can be substantial, often matching or beating prices at Aldi and Lidl.

More Than Just Prices: Maximizing Nectar Points

Beyond the instant discounts, the Nectar scheme still allows you to collect points. Here's how to boost your earnings:

  • Personalised Offers: Check your Nectar app every week for a set of personalized bonus point offers on products you frequently buy. Make sure to "save" them in the app before you shop.
  • Nectar Connect: Securely link your bank account to your Nectar account. You'll automatically collect points when you shop with partnered brands, without having to do anything else.
  • Partner Deals: You can earn and spend Nectar points with hundreds of partners, including Argos, eBay, and Esso.
