Unpacking Tesco's Weekly Offers: A Clubcard User's Guide

Published on July 26, 2024 | Category: Shopping Tips

Tesco is the UK's largest supermarket, and its Clubcard loyalty scheme is one of the most powerful tools for saving money on groceries. Understanding how to leverage it is key to finding the best Tesco offers this week.

Clubcard Prices: The Key to Savings

If you shop at Tesco, having a Clubcard is non-negotiable. "Clubcard Prices" offer instant, significant discounts on hundreds of items throughout the store. Here's how to make the most of it:

  • Always Scan Your Card: Whether it's the physical card, the app, or a key fob, make sure you scan it at the till to get the lower prices.
  • Plan Around the Deals: The Tesco app and website show the current Clubcard Prices. Plan your meals and shopping list around these deals to maximise savings.
  • Collect Points: For every £1 you spend, you earn 1 Clubcard point. 150 points can be turned into a £1.50 voucher to spend in-store or boosted with Reward Partners.

Beyond Clubcard: Other Tesco Deals

  1. Aldi Price Match: Tesco matches Aldi's prices on hundreds of basic products, from bread and milk to fresh produce. Look for the "Aldi Price Match" bubble.
  2. Multi-buy Promotions: Keep an eye out for deals like "Any 3 for 2" on items like cleaning supplies or snacks.
  3. Reduced to Clear Section: Tesco is excellent for its yellow-sticker reductions. Times vary by store, but evenings are usually the best time to find bargains on items nearing their expiry date.
