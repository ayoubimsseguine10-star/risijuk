The Ultimate Guide to Tesco Offers and Clubcard Prices

Posted on October 17, 2023 in Loyalty Schemes

Tesco's loyalty scheme is one of the most powerful tools for a UK shopper. The introduction of "Clubcard Prices" has made having a Clubcard essential for anyone looking to save money. Let's explore the Tesco offers this week.

Clubcard Prices: A Game Changer

Clubcard Prices are instant discounts available to all Clubcard holders. Hundreds of items, from fresh meat and vegetables to wine and household goods, have a special lower price that is applied automatically when you scan your card or app at the checkout. Shopping at Tesco without a Clubcard means you are often paying significantly more than you need to.

Beyond the Till: Maximizing Clubcard Vouchers

You still collect points on your shopping (1 point for every £1 spent). Every 150 points is worth £1.50 in vouchers, which are sent out quarterly. Here's how to make them go further:

  • Reward Partners: The best way to use your vouchers is with Tesco's Reward Partners. You can often exchange your vouchers for double their value on things like restaurant meals, days out, and cinema tickets.
  • Delivery Saver: Use your vouchers to pay for a Delivery Saver plan, which can save you a fortune on online grocery delivery fees.
  • Fuel Savings: You can use your vouchers to get money off petrol at Tesco petrol stations.
