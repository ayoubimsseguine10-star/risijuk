A Simple Guide to Morrisons Offers This Week

Published: October 19, 2024 | Category: Savings Guides

Morrisons is brilliant for its fresh food counters and great savings opportunities. To make sure you're getting the best value, you just need to know where to look. Here’s a simple guide to the best Morrisons offers this week.

The Morrisons More Card is a Must-Have

The More Card is your key to unlocking savings at Morrisons. It's free and easy to use, giving you two main benefits:

  • More Card Exclusives: Look for the special price tags around the store. These are lower prices available only to More Card holders.
  • Earn Points for Vouchers: You get points when you buy fuel or certain items in-store. Once you have 5,000 points, you get a £5 voucher (called a 'Fiver') to spend on anything you like.

The Magic of "Yellow Sticker" Deals

Morrisons is fantastic for its "yellow sticker" reduced items. Later in the day, they mark down fresh products that are near their sell-by date. It's the perfect time to find a bargain on bread, meat, or ready meals for that evening's dinner.

Treat Yourself with a 'Dine In' Deal

For an easy and delicious meal, check out the Morrisons 'Dine In' deals. You can usually get a main, a side, a dessert, and a drink for a set price. It’s much cheaper than a takeaway and perfect for a cozy night in.

By always using your More Card and keeping an eye out for those yellow stickers, the weekly Morrisons offers this week can help you save a lot on your shopping.
