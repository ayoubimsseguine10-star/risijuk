ASDA Rollbacks & Rewards: Your Ultimate Savings Guide

Updated: July 2024 | Category: Store Guides

ASDA's one-two punch for savings is its famous 'Rollback' and the powerful ASDA Rewards app. Mastering both is your ticket to a cheaper shop. Forget passive shopping; it's time to actively hunt down the value in the ASDA offers this week.

Hack #1: Weaponize the ASDA Rewards App

The ASDA Rewards app is a cashback machine. You build a 'Cashpot' by buying special 'Star Products' or completing 'Missions'. This is your primary tool for stacking savings on top of already low prices.

Your App-Based Game Plan:

  • Scan Before You Shop: Always check the app for Star Products and active Missions before you make your shopping list. Tailor your list to hit these targets.
  • Hunt for Star Products: These are your priority targets in-store. They often give you back 10% of their value into your Cashpot.
  • Complete Missions: Buying a certain number of products from a category (like fruit and veg) can unlock a cash bonus. It's a great way to get rewarded for your regular shop.
  • Cash Out Smart: Let your Cashpot build up and then use it to pay for an entire "free" shop. It feels great.

Hack #2: Time Your Strike with Rollbacks
