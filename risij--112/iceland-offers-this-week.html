How to Find the Best Iceland Offers This Week

Published on July 16, 2024 in Supermarket Guides

Iceland is the UK's undisputed king of frozen food, offering incredible convenience and value. But their deals go far beyond the freezer aisles. By understanding the Iceland offers this week, you can slash your grocery bill on a wide range of products.

The Famous Multi-buy Deals

Iceland's most well-known promotions are their multi-buy deals, which change regularly. Common examples include:

  • 3 for £10 on pizzas, ready meals, and chicken products.
  • 2 for £5 on tubs of ice cream and desserts.
  • 3 for £5 on frozen vegetables and sides.

These offers are perfect for stocking up the freezer and planning meals for the week ahead. Always check the signage to see which products are included in the mix-and-match deals.

Maximizing Your Savings with the Bonus Card

The Iceland Bonus Card is a simple but effective loyalty scheme. It's a pre-load savings card that gives you a reward for adding money to it.

  1. Load and Save: For every £20 you load onto your card, Iceland gives you an extra £1 for free. It's like getting an instant 5% return on your savings.
  2. Exclusive Perks: Bonus Card holders get access to special competitions, and occasionally, exclusive discounts.
  3. Free Home Delivery: Spend over £40 in-store and get your shopping delivered to your door for free.

More Than Just Frozen

In recent years, Iceland has massively expanded its range of fresh and grocery items. You can now do a full weekly shop there, with competitive prices on milk, bread, fresh meat, and fruit and vegetables. Look out for their deals on big-pack groceries like soft drinks and snacks, which often beat the major supermarkets.
