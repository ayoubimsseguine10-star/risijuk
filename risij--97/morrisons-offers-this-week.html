Your Complete Guide to Morrisons Offers This Week

Published on: October 19, 2024 | Category: Supermarket Guides

Morrisons stands out with its unique "Market Street," offering fresh produce, meat, and fish prepared by in-store specialists. To get the best value from your shop, it's essential to understand the various Morrisons offers this week, from loyalty card perks to weekly promotions.

Unlocking Savings with the Morrisons More Card

The Morrisons More card has been revamped to offer more ways to save. Here's what you need to know:

  • More Points: Earn points on selected products and fuel. 5,000 points get you a £5 voucher (a "Morrisons Fiver").
  • Exclusive Prices: Look for "More Card Exclusives" in-store and online for instant discounts at the checkout.
  • Personalized Offers: The app delivers offers tailored to your shopping habits.

Where to Find the Best Deals at Morrisons

Morrisons offers a variety of promotions that change weekly. Here's how to keep up:

  1. Check the Website: The 'Offers' section on Morrisons.com is the definitive source for current deals.
  2. Look for In-Store Signage: End-of-aisle displays and shelf-edge labels clearly mark the week's promotions.
  3. Visit Market Street: The butcher, baker, and fishmonger counters often have their own daily deals that provide excellent value and quality.
  4. Use Risij.uk: We compile the best promotions for you. Check our Morrisons offers page to plan your shop.

Pro Tip: The "Too Good To Go" App

For an extra bargain, check if your local Morrisons participates in the "Too Good To Go" app. You can get a "Magic Bag" of surplus food worth over £10 for around £3, helping to reduce food waste and save money.
