Morrisons: Market Street & My Morrisons Value Analysis

Data Compiled: October 19, 2024 | Sector: Traditional Grocery

The Economic Proposition of Market Street

Morrisons' "Market Street" concept, featuring in-store bakery, butchery, and fishmonger counters, serves as a key market differentiator. These counters provide access to fresh, high-quality products that frequently offer a superior cost-per-unit value compared to pre-packaged alternatives. To identify the most financially advantageous items, consumers should monitor the Morrisons offers this week on Risij.uk, where we highlight key deals from the Market Street counters.

Framework for Market Street Optimization:

  • Target promotions within "The Best" product range to acquire premium goods at a reduced cost basis.
  • Leverage the expertise of counter staff for product advice and preparation, reducing potential waste.
  • Monitor for end-of-day price reductions on fresh items for opportunistic savings.

Leveraging the My Morrisons Rewards Program

The "My Morrisons" loyalty program utilizes a model of instant, personalized digital rewards instead of a points-based system. The platform's algorithm generates tailored money-off coupons based on a user's purchase history. By activating these offers via the app before shopping, consumers can achieve direct and targeted savings on their most frequently purchased items.
