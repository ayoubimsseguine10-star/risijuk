The Cheat Code: How It Works

The system is brilliantly simple.

You load money onto your Bonus Card to use for future shops. For every £20 you load, Iceland instantly gives you £1 for free. That's a guaranteed 5% return on investment before you buy a single item. No other supermarket offers a direct cash bonus this clean and simple.

How to Execute the Glitch

  1. Get the Card: Pick one up in-store or register online. It's free.
  2. Load Your Funds: Decide on your shopping budget and load it onto the card at the till.
  3. Claim Your Bonus: The free cash is added instantly. Your £20 becomes £21. Your £100 becomes £105.
  4. Spend & Repeat: Pay for your shopping using your boosted balance.
