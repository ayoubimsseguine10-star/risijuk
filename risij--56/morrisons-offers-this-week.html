Finding Quality and Value in Morrisons Offers This Week

Posted on October 20, 2023 in Fresh Food

Morrisons stands out with its unique "Market Street," offering in-store butchers, bakers, and fishmongers that provide a traditional shopping experience. This focus on freshness, combined with competitive promotions, makes it a favourite for many. Let's look at the Morrisons offers this week.

The Heart of the Store: Market Street

Many of the best deals at Morrisons are found on Market Street. Look for "Ask us" deals where the expert staff can provide advice and special cuts. You'll often find multi-buy offers on fresh meat, fish, and baked goods that provide excellent value and quality.

My Morrisons Rewards

The "My Morrisons" loyalty scheme has shifted from points to personalized money-off coupons. Key things to know:

  • Personalised Offers: Check the app or website for offers tailored to what you usually buy. You might get 50p off your favourite cereal or £1 off at the butcher counter.
  • Basket Bonus: After you shop and scan your card, you might get a surprise "Basket Bonus," like a free bouquet of flowers or £5 off your next shop.
  • Join the Clubs: Sign up for specific clubs like the "Baby Club" or "Student Club" for extra targeted discounts.
