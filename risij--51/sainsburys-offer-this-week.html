Supercharging Your Savings with Nectar and Sainsbury's Offers

Published: October 17, 2023 | Category: Loyalty Programs

Sainsbury's and Nectar are one of the UK's most iconic loyalty partnerships. To get the best value, you need to go beyond simply swiping your card at the till. With the Nectar app and a keen eye for promotions, you can amplify your savings on the Sainsbury's offer this week.

The Nectar App is Your Secret Weapon

The physical Nectar card is just the beginning. The app unlocks a new level of personalised savings:

  • Weekly Personalised Offers: Every week, the app gives you a selection of bonus point offers based on your shopping history. Save your favourites before you shop to activate them.
  • Nectar Prices: Similar to Tesco's Clubcard Prices, Nectar Prices offer instant discounts at the checkout on hundreds of items. Just scan your app or card.
  • SmartShop Integration: If you use the in-store SmartShop scanners, the app will ping you with relevant offers as you walk around the store.

Tip: "Save" All Your App Offers

Get into the habit of opening the Nectar app and "saving" all of your weekly bonus point offers, even if you don't think you'll use them. You never know what you might pick up impulsively, and this ensures you won't miss out on points.

Look for the Aldi Price Match

Sainsbury's actively price-matches hundreds of its most popular products against Aldi prices. Look for the price match logo in-store. This is a great way to get the quality and range of Sainsbury's at prices competitive with the discounters, especially on staple items.
