Fresh Savings: Your Guide to Morrisons Offers This Week

Published on: 24th July 2024 | Category: Supermarket Deals

Morrisons stands out with its "Market Street," featuring in-store butchers, bakers, and fishmongers. This focus on freshness, combined with a robust loyalty scheme, provides many avenues for savings. Here’s how to take advantage of the Morrisons offers this week.

Get More with the Morrisons More Card

The loyalty scheme, "Morrisons More," is a points-based system that's simple to use. You earn points on selected products and at the fuel station, which can then be redeemed for vouchers.

More Card Highlights:

  • Earn Points: Collect points on hundreds of items in-store and online.
  • More Card Exclusives: Get instant money off at the till on specially marked products, just by scanning your card.
  • Bonus Offers: Check the app for personalised coupons and bonus point offers to boost your earnings.

Find the Best Deals! See all the current promotions and More Card Exclusives on the Morrisons Offers This Week page on Risij.uk.

Explore Market Street and Seasonal Offers

Don't overlook the Market Street counters. The staff can provide custom cuts of meat or specific fish fillets, reducing waste and often costing less than pre-packaged equivalents. Morrisons also runs excellent seasonal promotions and "when it's gone, it's gone" (WIGIG) deals on a wide variety of goods, so keep your eyes peeled as you shop.

Your Morrisons Savings Strategy

Before your shop, browse the weekly offers and check the Morrisons More app for any personalised coupons. Always scan your More card to get exclusive prices and earn points. A visit to the Market Street counters can also yield high-quality products at great prices, making your shop both fresh and frugal.
