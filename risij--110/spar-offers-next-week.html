A Simple Guide to Saving at SPAR

Simple Guide | Updated: October 2024

Your local SPAR is perfect for grabbing a few things when you're in a hurry. While convenience stores can be a bit more expensive, you can still find good value if you know where to look. Focusing on the SPAR offers for next week is the best way to save.

How to Find a Good Deal at SPAR

Even though every SPAR is a little different, there are three reliable ways to find value:

  • National Weekly Deals: Every week, SPAR runs the same deals across the country on popular things like snacks, drinks, and essentials. These are usually the best bargains.
  • SPAR's Own Brand: The products with the SPAR logo on them are a great way to save money compared to the big-name brands.
  • Meal Deals: Most SPAR stores offer a meal deal for lunch, which is usually a sandwich, snack, and drink for a set price. This is almost always cheaper than buying them separately.

A Smart Way to Shop at SPAR

Keep these simple tips in mind for your next visit:

  1. Look for Deal Signs: The best deals are always highlighted on signs in the store, often at the end of the aisles. Keep an eye out for these.
  2. Check National Deals Online: If you want to know the main UK-wide deals before you go, you can check a site like the Risij.uk SPAR page.
  3. Use It for Quick Top-Ups: SPAR is best for those times you just need one or two things. If you can time your visits to pick up items that are on offer, you'll avoid paying extra for convenience.

A Handy Part of Your Shopping Plan

A smart shopper knows which store is best for which job. SPAR is perfect for those small, quick shops, while a big supermarket like ASDA handles your main weekly grocery run. Using them both correctly is a great way to manage your budget.
